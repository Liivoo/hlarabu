298. HOW SWEET THE HOUR (S.S.321)

“The whole family in heaven.” (Ephe.3:15)

FANNY J. CROSBY Doh is F SCOTCH AIR

1) Manpâk ah derna pha kahōi, Ama manpâk inna,
Taksen ratha vâng de inna manpâk na katlung sik;
Inpâk na ṭongtep inthei sik, ṭongtep kalûr tak kha,
Ralei ah hin ni’nṭhen jônnum marvân ah inhmu rih.

Antor ning kani nikheng ah lân hmu tō êt sik me,
Jesu thisen marit jârra lân hmu tō êt sik me.

2) Lungset na inpâk kaom hi ni malung inpâk na,
Christa Pathen Sapa jârra nikheng ah ni’ntûp leh;
Inṭhen na ahong tlung jônnum aṭongtep jârra khan,
Inhôr tak ah bong khat sō leh chung ting inhmu sik me.

Inṭhen na ahong tlung jônnum, aṭongtep jârra khan,
Inhôr tak ah bong khat sō leh chung ting inhmu sik me.

3) Taksen na kha ni’nvon sik jo Sana khōpui sûng ah,
Lungset ṭimre hên ani hngâk khava ram kahōi ah;
Bong khat mathlêm na thlipui hi anchai sō pha tak leh,
Kum leikō hla nisak sik jo marvân ah ni’nhmu leh.

Bong khat mathlêm na thlipui hi anchai sō pha tak leh,
Kum leikō hla nisak sik jo marvân ah ni’nhmu leh.
