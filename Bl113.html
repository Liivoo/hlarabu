113. NEARER MY GOD TO THEE (S.S.581)

“Draw nigh to God and He will draw nigh to you.” (Jas.4:8)

SARAH F. ADAMS Doh is GLOWELL MASON

1) Pathen! Kahong hnai che, arahlâng roh,
Cross chung achang innum ei kadom roh;
Nalungset hla sak rong, Nahneng kahong jōn jo,
Pathen! Kahong hnai che, jōng rahlâng roh.

2) Jacob khōhme sena na en kapōah,
Inthim na kârra hin akei thōi roh;
Karahmang ah Nahneng Nang kahong jōn jo che,
Pathen! Kahong hnai che, ênṭangpui roh.

3) Na hlam jōng manjōng roh kahong nasik,
Marvân Rathla Intheng jōng tir pêk roh,
Kaka om pui sik leh kaka ṭhōi le sik ah,
Nahneng kahong jōn jo, alei en roh!

4) Ka lungjing avâr leh manpâk che rong,
Bethel kha Pathen Inn! kên kati sik;
Na âng ah kaom sik Nahneng kahong jōn jo,
Inreng na ralei hi kahong dahlon.

5) Jōng na mathla nei ah kajōng innum,
Kani nêk ah chung ah kajōng innum;
Na manpâk na hla rōk tûnchu kasak sik jo,
Nahneng det ah Pathen! Kahong hnai che.
