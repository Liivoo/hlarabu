328. JESUS! LOVER OF MY SOUL (S.S.227)

“The Lord also will be a refuge in time of trouble.” (Ps.9:9) [2nd Tune]

CHARLES WESLEY Doh is F S.B. MARSH

1) Ka rathla kahûk Jesu! Nahneng ah aman om roh,
Kahneng ah tuipui len lai, thlipui-khōpui ahrâng lai;
Athup roh kan Tlensōtpa! thlipui-khōpui abâng ah,
Marvân katlung aṭhōi roh, karathla alo pêk roh.

2) Inthup na lak nei mak eng, karathla Nang ah kaom,
Kei amasō jên mak roh, lungmông na apêk ṭhak roh;
Kataksen Nang ah kaom, inṭangpui Nang ah kanhmu,
Ahûk roh, aka-ûn roh, Namathla hnōi daihlim ah.

3) Oh! Christa! Hei rakip ah Nang teilo alak om mak,
Akatlu hei matho roh, akana hêm mandam roh;
Hmit kacho hei manvâr roh, Na rahming intheng Chunghnung!
Dik na kanei mak kei eng, Nang dik leh lungset kasip.

4) Marit lungset kasip che, lungset ah amantheng roh,
Hring leh damna tui hnâr che, Asyem roh, ei mantheng roh;
Hring na tui ripût Nang che, mankase ei man in roh,
Ka sûng ah manlōng inna, kumsôt ah hring nasik ah.
