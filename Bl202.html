202. FROM DEATH UNTO LIFE (S.S.884)

“We know that we have passed from death unto life….” (I Jn.3:14)

W.J.K. Doh is G WILLIAM J. KIRKPATRICK

1) Na malungset na rahming,
Kamanhret sōt hma khan;
Anhmang ah lei kaom eng,
Malung inreng tak ah.

Hallelujah! Lungset na,
Anipêk mankase ah;
Jesu thisen kei sik ah,
Akalûrpên kaeh.

2) Setna khō inthim sûng ah,
Thina leh injak na;
Anhmang sa kachang jo eng,
Khōdeng kathei tak ah.

3) Kei malung inreng tak ah,
Lei kathi jo eng ah;
Na malungset nôk jârra,
Najōng jong sōt jo eng.

4) Kei amalungset jârra,
Kumsôt hring kanei jo;
Na thisen kalûr vâng khan,
Na katlensōt jo eng.
