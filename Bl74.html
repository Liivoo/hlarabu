74. BREAK THOU THE BREAD OF LIFE (R.S.2)

“He looked up to heaven and blessed and brake the loaves….” (Mk.6:41)

MARY A. LATHBURYDoh is EbWILLIAM F. SHERWIN

1) Hring na bulhom khōi roh kei sik Rōngpu!
Dir pang ah nalei khōi kapōah khan;
Malung inri tak ah kajōng che Nang!
Ka rathla hin in rong oh! hring na ṭong.

2) Akadik vâng pe roh kei sik Rōngpu!
Galilee ah vâng napêk kapōah;
Khit ah kaom rakip masō hei roh,
Malung mông na kha kên kân hmu sik jo.

3) Kei sik achu hring na buhlom che Nang!
Na ṭong Intheng khan ahûk eng kaeh;
Sâk sik ah ape roh ka hring nasik,
Na dikna madu rong, amanthei roh.

4) Tûn na Rathla Intheng jōng tir pêk roh,
Kahmit asyem pêk roh, hmu thei nasik;
Na ṭong dik in-ip kha aman hmu roh,
Rōngpu kha hmu jo rung, na ṭong vâng hin.
