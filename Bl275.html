275. WEEPING WILL NOT SAVE ME (S.S.337)

“For by grace are ye saved through faith….” (Ephe.2:8)

R.L. Doh is Eb ROBERT LOWRY

1) Ṭa’n ei hûksōt mak ning,
Kahmit rathli asōk ah;
Ka lungdong na tlang mak ning,
A kumsôt ah tlang mak ning;

Ṭa’n ei hûksōt mak ning.
Kei sik ah Jesu kathi,
Thing cross chung ah anhem bek;
Ahngâk eng hûkmanhring sik,
Ama rōk hringsōt na.

2) Tlo’n ei hûksōt mak ning,
Heituk kachôn aṭhat ah;
Ama kên kabêk ṭhak ah,
Kamalung hrât na om mak;
Tlo’n ei hûksōt mak ning.

3) Hngâk hrât ah hring mak ning,
Inṭangpui neilo mi kei;
Lungset ah abêk lai hin,
Hngâk hrât ah kei hring mak ning;
Alungset rōk hring na.

4) Christa taksen hring na,
Pathen Sapa Jesu kha;
Ama ni taksen inchu,
Amân ahûksōt sik me;
Christa taksen hring na.
