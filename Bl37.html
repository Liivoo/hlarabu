37. LET THE SAVIOUR IN (S.S.363)

“If any man hear My voice and open…I will come in to him.” (Rev.3:20)

JONATHAN B. ATCHINSONDoh is DbEDWIN O. EXCELL

1) Mi inkhat kot ah kangir, manhlut roh,
Alei ngir na kasôt jo, manhlut roh;
Adahlon mak hma che hin manhlut roh Mi’nthengpa kha,
Pathen Sapa Jesu kha manhlut roh.

2) Na malung mahlang inna manhlut roh,
Kase sik hngâk mak jo ruh, manhlut roh;
Naṭhōn kaeh manhlut roh, Amân ahûk nger sik che,
Amân ahngâk ṭhak sik che, manhlut roh.

3) Akare thei mak che mo? manhlut roh,
Tûn Ama indei sōt roh, manhlut roh;
Namadu sik Arahming, inpâk nâm nachên sik jo,
Tûn Ama kot ah kangir, manhlut roh.

4) Marvân khōhme Mi’nthengpa! manhlut roh,
Revei amantluk pêk che, manhlut roh;
Na setna angei dam sik, ralei lungjing na inphut,
Marvân ah aṭhōi sik che, manhlut roh.
