157. THE PRECIOUS NAME! (S.S.91)

“…blessed be His glorious name for ever….” (Ps.72:19)

LYDIA BAXTER Doh is AbWILLIAM H. DOANE

1) Na malung ah invon ṭhak roh,
Ni Rōngpu Jesu rahming;
Nase-le na hmun rakip ah,
Mahngi mak roh Arahming.

Arahming marit kha,
Na malung ah von ṭhak roh;
Arahming marit kha,
Marvân ah inpâk nasik.

2) Jesu rahming invon inna,
Ama kha pho manchang roh;
Nabing ah nase na hmun âm,
Kut suk, ke suk nei mak tin.

3) Jesu rahming invon ṭhak roh,
Khōva ram ah nase’n num;
Aṭong hri na mandon inchu,
Na malung kahring sik jo.

4) Jesu rahming invon inna,
Malung ngam tak ah se roh;
Inreng na mahngi sō sik jo,
Ama hneng nader inchu.
