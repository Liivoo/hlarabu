39. JESUS! BELOVED OF MY HEART (S.S.646)

“My beloved is mine, and I am his….” (Song of Solomon.2:16)

GEORGE D. MACKAYDoh is CGEORGE C. STEBBINS

1) Jesu! Kamalung indu pa,
Namalung set kha kader che;
Nasōk kei chung ah jōng pe roh,
Kamanhret chêt nasik che ah.

Malung innêm ajōng pe roh,
Naram kahōi hmu nasik ah;
Kamalung hi jōng manvâr roh,
Kei sik Eh! Marvân ah Pathen!

2) Rōngpu! Namalungset jârra,
Setna inhmang hei najong sōt;
Inpâk tak ah karahlâng che,
Hmu rong na lungset marit kha.

3) Hngâk na hmun ram kahōi ah khin,
Rei marim kahōi tak kapâr;
Ka rathla inpâk nasik ah,
Nalungset na kakhe om mak.

4) Nang teilo khōvâr om thei mak,
Kan malung ri jōng en inna;
Vânhnōi inthim jōng manvâr roh,
Inpâk rakip Nangta kaeh.
