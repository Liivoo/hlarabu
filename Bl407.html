407. LUNGSET LUNGDI! (M.C.K.8)

“What therefore God hath joined together, let not man put asunder.” (Matt.19:6)

M. CHUNGRENGKHUP KOM Doh is Ab 30/06/2016

1) Lungset hmaisō katlai seng ah,
Inlâr jo tuning kalûr tak ah;
Hngâk hla kani hōi katlung jo,
Ni’npâk na kani kalûr.

Kumsôt ah kalêng sik me,
Inpâk, inreng pha rakip ah;
Lungset marit ah karôn sik che,
Oh! ’Di, lungset lungdi!

2) Lungdi hmaisō kara ngei pên,
Hmun tin, phat tin rangei chim mak eng;
Nang rōk che ’Di ralei chung ah,
Taksen na, ka inhôr na.

3) Ralei hōina, lûrna, lenna,
Khâr tor mak ning ni in lungset na;
Nat leh sat, khōdeng theina in,
Ṭhen sōt mak ning ni’ntêp na.

4) Taksa hme kaṭha, en kahōi,
Oh! Heituk ah kalûr jo tâk mo!
Hnem na kaeh lungset jao loah,
Lungset in achunghnungpên.

5) [Nu kaṭha tak hong chang inna,
Sōn leh pâr kaṭha hei syem sōt roh] (Groom)
[Pa kaṭha tak hong chang inna,
Syem sōt roh innshung kahōi] (Bride)
