95. JESUS LOVES ME (S.S.1155)

“We love Him because He first loved us.” (1 Jn.4:19)

A.B.WARNER & D.R.MCGUIRE Doh is EW.B.BRADBURY

1) Jesûn kei alungset eng,
Bible in ahri pêk jo;
Kên ratha kanei mannum,
Amachu akahrât pên.

Jesûn alungset,
Jesûn alungset;
Jesûn alungset,
Bible in amanthei.

2) Jesûn alungset jârra,
Kei sik ah jōng kathi jo;
Marvân kot amahlang ah,
Naipang hei hlut nasik ah.

3) Jesûn kei amalungset,
Sûn leh jân ahngâk ṭhak eng;
Ama tak nisen inchu,
Marvân ah aṭhōi sik me.
