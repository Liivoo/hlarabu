362. AWAKENING CHORUS (3rd ALEX.405)

“The Lord reigneth, let the earth rejoice!” (Ps.97:1)

CHARLOTTE G. HOMER Doh is C CHARLES H. GABRIEL

1) Thōi ruh! Thōi ruh! Manpâk na hla kha sak ruh,
Thōi ruh! Thōi ruh! Hla sak na kare inchôi ah;
Thōi ruh! Thōi ruh! Vânhnōi inpâk sip rasoh,
Marvân ânphut vâr in ra-âng rasoh;
[MALE VOICE IN UNISON]
Hme leh tlâng, dâm, phaichâm inpâk rasa manthang ruh,
Rasa rakip leh torna hla ôn ruh.

[CHORUS]
[FULL HARMONY]
Rōngpu Jehovah Reng in setna ator jo,
[UNISON: SP & TENOR]
Inpâk tak ah ôn ruh, sak ruh, Jehovah Reng!
[FULL HARMONY]
Vânhnōi ralei chung rakip vai arôn,
Alûr na pōnlek kadom mansâng ruh [BASS: Jehovah Reng!]
Jehovah Reng! Inpâk tak ah ôn ruh,
Jehovah Reng!

2) Thōi ruh! Thōi ruh! Manpâk na dâr manhring ruh,
Ave, ve ah thuhming kha ather ah hri ruh;
Vânhnōi, ralei, lungdong kha ajâng nasik,
Ȏn ruh, ôn ruh, lûrna inlâr rasoh;
[MALE VOICE IN UNISON]
Marvân Tirle hei leh hûksōt na hla kha sak ruh,
Set leh thina inphut châm inhnga jo.
