390. KA RANGEI CHE KAPA! (M.A.K.5)

“My son, forget not my law…let thine heart keep my commandments.” (Prov.3)

M. ATON KOM Doh is F 10/06/1997

1) Kapa hring lai mansena ṭong,
Heisak lo ah lei kalêng eng;
Ka-ôi sam rih kapa ṭong jai,
Inthei tor chang mak jo tuning chu.

Ka rangei che kapa lungset!
Kami chang sik nathôk pêk eng;
Manpâk che rong-eh nasa ke’n,
Pa hei mandonsōt na kani ning.

2) Sabakai ratha hrât kapō,
Jingkâr kavûr kadai don lo;
Sûn nilun kanisat athôk,
Asa sik ram leh tui amantluk.

3) Pathen inhret Noah kapō,
Intluk jo rih Pa rakip in;
Rengram kot khan hlut tor nasik,
Syemsōt jo rih Reng Christa inn-kō.
