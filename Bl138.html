138. WHEN WILL YOU SPEND ETERNITY? (S.S.430)

“Prepare to meet thy God.” (Amos.4:12)

ELISAH A. HOFFMAN Doh is FJOHN H. TENNEY

1) Na kumsôt hmun sik khôn amo,
Tiah nang leh kei ader me;
Kha hei khong mo na masôn sik?
Na kumsôt hmun sik kha khôn mo?

Na kumsôt hmun, na kumsôt hmun,
Na kumsôt hmun sik kha khôn mo?

2) An setna rakip dahlon ah,
Jesu an rahlâng mi hing jân;
Marvân hmun kha anta kachang,
Na kumsôt hmun sik kha khôn mo?

3) Marvân jôn na kot kasin hi,
Kalei dahlon jo tâk innum;
Hing ah hin kamasôn sik jo,
Ka kumsôt inn marvân kaeh.

4) Tûn tak ah hin inle inna,
Jesu natlenpa taksen roh;
Hing ah hin nân masôn jo roh,
Ka kumsôt inn marvân kaeh.

Ka kumsôt hmun, ka kumsôt hmun,
Ka kumsôt inn marvân kaeh.
