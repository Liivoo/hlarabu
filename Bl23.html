23. FALL INTO LINE (S.S.705)

“Fight the good fight of faith.” (I Tim.6:12)

WILLIAM H. GARDNER Doh is AbCHARLES K. LANGLEY

1) Hme le tlâng insâng rakip ah,
Rangei ruh Gospel bêk na;
Rangei ruh inpâk na hla kha,
Eini abêk na kaeh.

Re katlo sik ngir inhnêt ruh,
Re katlo sik ngir inhnêt ruh;
Inpun na pêngkun manhring rangei û-na,
Inpun ruh Christian sepei.

2) Re torna pōnlek mansâng ruh,
Ram rakip manjōng û-na;
Akaṭha ṭōn ni tlo lai seng,
Pathen in anṭangpui sik.

3) Re torna pêngkun manhring ruh,
Re chaina kare ôn ṭhak ruh;
Jesu ta kaeh akheng ah,
Ni thi’num ni tor nger sik.
