264. MUST I GO AN EMPTY HANDED? (S.S.789)

“…none shall appear before Me empty.” (Exo.23:15)

CHARLES C. LUTHER Doh is D GEORGE C. STEBBINS

1) Kan Tlenpa ratō nasik ah,
Kut karōk ah sik eng mo?
Ni khat bêk aṭōn tlo loah,
Hei vâng mo ka ratō sik?

Ka Rōngpu ratō nasik ah,
Kut karōk ah kase eng;
Mi inkhat bêk ṭhōi lo jên ah,
Hei vâng mo ka ratō sik?

2) Thi khan insir malung om mak,
Rōngpu’n aṭhōi jârra khan;
Rōk ah ka ratō sik jârra,
Insir leh lungjing kaom.

3) Kakum lei man hmang rakip kha,
Bêk male thei a-ên chu;
Bêk male ah Rōngpu hneng ah,
Inpâk tak ah ka pêk sik.

4) Mi rakip hlim êt jo û-na,
Kani ala om lai hin;
Thinân aphâk mak hma chei hin,
Ratō nasik mandon ruh.
