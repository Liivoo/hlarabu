287. WHAT A FRIEND WE HAVE IN JESUS (S.S.319)

“There is a friend that sticketh closer than a brother.” (Prov.18:24)

WILLIAM W. WALFORD Doh is D WILLIAM B. BRADBURY

1) Ni set lungjing na karôn sik,
Ṭhōn kaṭha ninei Jesu;
Ni set rakip rôn manhlut rih,
Pathen hneng ah derna ah;
Set leh râk malung inrim na,
Heisamo nide mak sik?
Pathen hneng ni mathlung mak jâr,
Ni kamuk na hei rakip.

2) Ni nei mo ṭong se mathlêm na?
Lungdong, lungjing na ênnum;
Malung tla loah om ṭhak rih,
Pathen hneng rôn manhlut rih;
Ni hmu sik mo ṭhōn kaṭha lak?
Ni kadepui hlam rin thei;
Jesûn amanhret ni tlâksam,
Derna Ahneng mathlung rih.

3) Tlâksam na leh ni phur rôn na,
Nile mo ni rôn kahrik?
Ni bêr sik Jesu kaom eh!
Derna Ahneng mathlung rih;
Ṭimre hên andahlon chên num,
Der roh na Rōngpu hneng ah;
Aka-ûn sik che Akut vâng,
Nang in hōi nathei sik jo.
