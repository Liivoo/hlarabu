213. ONLY A STEP TO JESUS (S.S.448)

“Then come thou, for there is peace.” (1 Sam.20:21)

FANNY J. CROSBY Doh is Ab WILLIAM H. DOANE

1) Jesu hneng se nasik,
Tûn kapha heimo natlo?
Naset malung thleng inna,
Rōngpu hneng ah hong roh.

Kethon khat rōk, kethon khat rōk,
Nang ala hngâk che, naset malung thleng inna;
Khan hring na vâng nahmu sik,
Mankase ah nahmu hring na kha hlônsōt mak roh.

2) Jesu nasor inchu,
Taksen na hring nanei sik;
Na setna en loah,
Lungset kut vâng ahngâk che.

3) Jesu jôn ah se sik kha,
Kethon khat rōk kaeh;
Pha thleng hma na malung ah,
Nan heimo namandon?

4) Jesu hneng ah inphut,
Na lei tlânsōt jo innum;
Insir ah nahong jôn sik kha,
Rōngpu’n ahngâk che.
