320. WHY NOT TONIGHT? (S.S.335)

“How long halt ye between two opinions?” (1 Kgs.18:21)

E. REED Doh is Eb IRA D. SANKEY

1) Ṭong Intheng kha masō mak roh,
Khōvâr kha nang in en ṭhak roh;
Naset malung mannêm ṭhak roh,
Ahûk sik che tuning jân hin.

Tuning jân hin, tuning jân hin,
Ahûk sik che tuning jân hin;
Tuning jân hin, tuning jân hin,
Ahûk sik che tuning jân hin.

2) Jing kani vâr hngâk mak roh,
Mahâm set nân abôm sik che;
Tûn tak inrin pha jo kaeh,
Ahûk sik che tuning jân hin.

3) Ralei chung nei ra-ôt mak roh,
Antheng neinâk heite om mak;
Tûn tak intluk pha jo kaeh,
Ahûk sik che tuning jân hin.
