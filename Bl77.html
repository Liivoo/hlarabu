77. JERUSALEM! MY HAPPY HOME

“…New Jerusalem, coming down from God out of heaven.” (Rev.21:2)

Doh is E

1) Jerusalem! Ka inn kahōi!
Kachi hla, kabâng hla;
Heitik tâk mo intheng tak ah,
[Ahōi khi hmu sik mo?] x2

2) Akur kani nêk ah kavâr,
Ahōije Chunghnungpên!
Akot rakip chung ah kavâr,
[A hlam kaphâm kavâr] x2

3) Arahōn sûng kavâr tak khi,
Hring na thing um kakêk;
Chunghrep ah khōpui kahōi kha,
[Mihring hmit in hmu mak] x2

4) Chunghrep ah kahōi marvân ram!
Khōpui kahōi Chunghnung!
Keichu heite mông na neilo,
[Thi sik um kaṭit mo?] x2

5) Mi’ntheng hei inṭhen phung mak jo,
A ê-na’m bâng tik nei mak;
Aṭhōi roh na khōpui sûng ah,
[Akei roh nakut vâng] x2

6) Nipu omna ram kahōi ah,
Kei um chungting se rong;
Ama kahmu leh kumsôt ah,
[Amanpâk hla sak rong] x2
