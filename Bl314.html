314. THE HOLLOW OF GOD’S HAND (S.S.530)

“…in the shadow of His hand hath He hid me….” (Isai.49:2)

E.S.L. Doh is C EDMUNDS S. LORENZ

1) Kan Tlensōtpân aṭhōi ah aching eng,
Hei lai thina ênnum kahring sik eng;
Thlipui-khōpui kârra’m kei athup eng,
Akutpha intheng sûng ah.

Akutpha intheng sûng ah,
Akutpha intheng sûng ah;
Hei lai thina ênnum kahring sik eng,
Akutpha intheng sûng ah.

2) Alungset kut bân vâng amatu eng,
Lung mông na ṭong vâng kei amathlêm eng;
Pathen kut ṭhōi ah kei amanhring eng,
Akutpha intheng sûng ah.

3) Alungset na kên mahngi mak jo ning,
Pathen leh kei tûnmo aṭhen sik eng?
Heitik chen’um kei amasō mak ning,
Akutpha intheng sûng ah.
