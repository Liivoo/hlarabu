267. AN OPEN BIBLE FOR THE WORLD (S.S.262)

“Thy word is a lamp unto my feet, and a light unto my path.” (Ps.119:105)

HENRY M. KING Doh is Bb WILLIAM J. KIRKPATRICK

1) Vânhnōi sik ah Bible inphon,
Ni lûrna leh hōi nasik ah;
Nan hōina ahmun rakip ah,
Nanta chôi mansâng chang rasoh.

Pathen ṭongpêk, na vâr sem roh,
Ralei chung leh tuipui chung âm;
Inthim ah khōdeng kathei hei,
Pathen âng ah hong ṭhōi hei roh.

2) Khava Sana khōvâr ah khan,
Kani vâr rangei mak jo hei;
Sumtuk leh jân inṭhen sik jo,
Set rakip um katlang sik jo.

3) Mi hên anpa hmai anhmu sik,
Rangei mandam anchen sik jo;
Adam khang ah anhmang sahei,
Ram rakip ah hri manthang ruh.

4) Malung kadong châm inhnga sik,
Hmit rathli’m kahi sō sik jo;
Malung kangûi kahlim sik jo,
Kumlei kōah kahōi sik hei.
