277. COMING (S.S.186)

“…the coming of the Lord draweth nigh.” (Jas.5:8)

WILLIAM O. CUSHING Doh is Ab IRA D. SANKEY

1) Oh! Chingtlâng ah kahngâk mihei,
Kani jōng kahnai jo;
Rōngpu ajōng kaṭum sik kha,
Ȏng û-na phôngsōt ruh.

Rōngpu ajōng nasik kani kha kahnai jo,
Rōngpu ajōng nasik apha kha kahnai jo;
Rōngpu ajōng nasik, hla sak rih nikheng ah,
Tlâng rakip um kavâr sō jo;
Rōngpu kha manpâk ruh.

2) Oh! Akaze Bêkrup hei kha,
Thōi ruh, hlim ruh, der ruh;
Ni kaṭhōi sik hmōi kapu kha,
Tûn Ama kajōng jo.

3) Sonle hei in anta lei hri,
Reng kha kajōng sik jo;
Marvân ah Tirle hei kha leh,
Ni kheng ah manpâk rih.
