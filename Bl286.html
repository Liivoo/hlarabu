286. HE LEADETH ME (S.S.542)

“For I…will hold thy right hand….” (Isai.41:13)

J.H. GILMORE Doh is D WILLIAM B. BRADBURY

1) Se na, hong na, hmun rakip ah,
Pathen in ani ṭhōi ṭhak eng;
Mandon leh tlo na rakip ah,
Pathen in ani ṭhōi ṭhak eng.

Pathen in ani ṭhōi ṭhak eng,
Ama kut khet in akei eng;
A Tirle taksen inchên tak,
Akut vâng aṭhōi ṭhak jârra.

2) Lungdong, lungjing, nat, thōk na in,
Inreng nân ani bôm innum;
Marvân ram ṭong, hring na ṭong kha,
Kamalung in amandon ṭhak.

3) Akahōipên kachang innum,
Tui-pui, tui-te kape innum;
Eden rahōn ah kalêng leh,
Pathen in a-om pui sik jo.

4) Kathi pha leh kei Pathen in,
Jordan re amanke sik eng;
Marvân ah kaom sik jo eng,
Inpâk ah Amân aṭhōi eng.
