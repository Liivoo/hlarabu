293. THERE IS A PARADISE OF REST (S.S.984)

“The Lord shall give thee rest.” (Isai.14:3)

FANNY J. CROSBY Doh is Eb IRA D. SANKEY

1) Châm hngat na hmun kahōi kaom,
Tuipui rekhat ting khan;
Jân inthim om mak Chungkhōpui ah khan,
Inreng, ṭap nâm om mak.

In hmu rih, khan in hmu rih,
Khava jing nisō in-êm reng hmun ah khan;
In hmu rih, khan in hmu rih,
Tuipui re ram kahōi avâr ṭhak na khan.

2) Khōpui kahōi kavâr ṭhak kha,
Ahōi hri thei chang mak;
Khan ah kahlut rakip hōina anhmu,
Kaom sōm sik jo me.

3) Lên lukhu kha lei kaom jo,
Jesûn kei apêk sik;
Dehrât ah ajōng sik kahngâk hei khan,
Anchen sik alûrna.
