124. FOLLOW ON (S.S.529)

“If any man serve Me, let him follow Me….” (Jn.12:26)

WILLIAM O. CUSHING Doh is AbROBERT LOWRY

1) Hmun rakip ah khan kên Jesu kha kajui sik,
Tuithlum lōng na hmun leh rei pârna ram ah khan;
Amân aṭhōi na rakip ah kên kajui ṭhak sik,
Hring na lukhu kha kên kahmu mak kâr seng.

Jui rong, jui rong, Jesu kahnung jui rong,
Asena rakip ah khan kên kajui sik;
Jui rong, jui rong, Jesu kahnung jui rong,
Asena rakip ah akahnung jui rong.

2) Kavadung ênnum Jesu kha kên kajui sik,
Thlipui hrât na leh tuithlum lōng na hmun ah khan;
Amân anṭangpui sik jônchu kei ṭi hêr mak ning,
Amân a-om pui inchu kei ṭi mak ning.

3) Dâm ênnum, tlâng ênnum, Jesu ase jônchu,
Ka rathla Jesu hneng ah khan om ṭhak rasoh;
Amân kei kadam tak ah aṭhōi ṭhak sik jo eng,
Hring na apêk sik eng kei Pathen hneng ah.
