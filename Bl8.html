8. HE ROLLS THE SEA AWAY (S.S.880)

“The Lord caused the sea to go back.” (Exo.14:21)

EMMA PITTDoh is FDANIEL B. TOWNER

1) Tênlai ah Pharaoh sōk hei kha,
Pathen in aṭhōi sōt;
Aṭōn tlo theina aman-en,
Tui-ôngpui aman-ṭhen.

Hei rakip in an ôi, hei rakip in an ôi,
Jesu hneng ah ṭit sik om mak;
Hei rakip in aṭongpêk an manjui.

2) Set in kamalung aman hnok,
Seitân ṭongpêk jârra;
Tûnchu kên mông na kanei jo,
Rōngpu lungset jârra.

3) Ṭit na hlam ah kase ravâng,
Rōngpu kêbi kajui;
Lungset ratha vâng aṭhōi ah,
Hlam rōng kakhe hma seng.

4) Rōngpu Jesu! Kahrang chet lai,
Na ram vâr kahmu sik;
Vânhnōi ṭit kaom ah inphut,
Na ram ah aṭhōi roh.
