160. LET US HEAR YOU TELL IT (S.S.898)

“…I will declare what He hath done for my soul.” (Ps.66:16)

J.M.W. Doh is AbJ.M. WHYTE

1) Rōngpu ’ngei damna kha hei không mo na hri?
Ave-ve ah hri nôk roh rangei jo rōng;
Ni hring nasik ah cross chung ah athi pêk,
Ave-ve ah hri nôk roh rangei jo rōng;
Na setna rakip amantheng jo mo?
Avâr intheng kha nachen jo mo?
Ama kha hōina, mông na nanei jo mo?
Ave-ve ah hri nôk roh rangei jo rōng.

Ave-ve ah hri nôk hi roh, hûksōt na nachen jo kha,
Thuhming kahōi kha hri nôk roh;
Lûrna hmun tlung tor na kaeh,
Ave-ve ah hri nôk roh rangei jo rōng.

2) Tlensōtpa kha heitik ah mo nân hmu pui?
Ave-ve ah hri nôk roh rangei jo rōng;
Innêm tak ah Jesûn a-en ṭhak che mo?
Hri nôk roh ave-ve ah rangei jo rōng;
Nânreng lai tak aṭhōn kaṭha alak,
Ama teilo inhmu mak jo tin;
Vânhnōi in aṭhat na hi anthei nasik,
Ave ah hri male nôk roh rangei rōng.

3) Re tlona nânjao lai ah nân hrât na kha,
Rangei êt rōng ave-ve ah hri nôk roh;
Akajao ther hei ratha hrât nasik ah,
Ave-ve ah hri nôk roh rangei jo rōng;
Setna kûngpui leh kan inre ravâng,
Christa’n tor na apêk sik jo ung;
Hûksōt sa seng in alak hûk nôk hei ruh,
Ave-ve ah hri nôk roh mi rakip sik.
