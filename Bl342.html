342. A MIGHTY FORTRESS (S.S.2)

“…the Lord is my rock, and my fortress and my deliverer.” (II Sam.22:2)

M.L. Doh is D MARTIN LUTHER

1) Ni re kur kha Pathen kaeh,
Ra-pe kha set hêr mak ning;
Ahûk me tui in-nông ah khan,
Thi leh nat inlâr mak ning;
Nipu, nipa kadōi hei khan ani hlô’num,
Ni Rōngpu ratha kha;
Ralei chung kut-chôi in,
Ahrât na de tor mak ning.

2) Ni bing ah an-ngam nâk che mo?
Ni hnong ni man hmang khâk sik;
Ni bing rōk ah ngir tor mak min,
Pathen leh ngir teilo chu;
Tûmo Jesu Christa rōk kati tor sik hei?
Rōngpu rahming kaeh;
Kum leikō inthleng mak,
Re ator nger sik kaeh.

3) Ralei chung setna ram ah hin,
Khōdeng amanthei me’num;
Ṭit sik lak heite om jên mak,
Pathen in asen sik me;
Om mak anthim rengpa hrât na,
Ṭit sik heite om mak;
Ahnûk nat na de rih,
Pathen beisō khat rōk;
Areng ram kumsôt ah sik.
