172. ALL THE WAY TO CALVARY (S.S.82)

“…the place…called Calvary, there they crucified Him….” (Lk.23:33)

ALFRED H. ACKLEY Doh is GPETER P. BILHORN

1) Eh! Rōngpu! Ka malung hi inthim rei eh!
Ênnum Rōngpu! Na lungset jârra;
Ka rathla kathi sa sik na manhring ah,
Ka setna rakip najōng en ah.

Calvary tlâng ah ni Rōngpu kajōng ah,
Eini ni hring thei nasik ah;
Calvary tlâng ah ni Rōngpu kajōng ah,
Ni hring thei nasik ah.

2) Setna kanei, Rōngpu! Kên na âng sûng ah,
Kahong kamuk che, kên Nahneng ah;
Ka Rōngpu Jesu! Calvary ah kathi,
Pathen lungset ṭong kên kân hmu jo.

3) Pathen in kei amalungset hrang hra eng,
Calvary tlâng ah Jesu kathi;
Amarvân lên in-ông na ajōng dahlon,
Rōngpu Jesu! Kên kamanpâk che.
