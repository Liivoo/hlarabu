144. GOD WILL TAKE CARE OF YOU (S.S.517)

“The Lord shall preserve thy going out and thy coming in….” (Ps.121:8)

FANNY J. CROSBY Doh is GIRA D. SANKEY

1) Pathen in aṭhōi sik che ṭi mak roh,
Sûn leh jân ah na ka-en ṭhak Ama;
Ata kachang tak ah ahngâk che nang,
Nân vaina rakip anṭangpui sik che.

Pathen in aching che pha chai katlung,
Nân Tlensōtpa leh na Rōngpu kaeh;
Alo pêk che nang in nader lai seng,
Jesu taksen roh hei rakip sik ah.

2) Pathen in ahmu che pha rakip ah,
Nasena, omna anṭhōipui ṭhak che;
Na hlam akei pêk che Rōngpu Jesûn,
Aṭhōi sik che tuipang ram kahōi ah.

3) Pathen in ahmu che na hring lai seng,
Ṭhat na rakip nahneng ah apêk che;
Na thi ning katlung ah mahngi mak roh,
Aṭhōi manhlut sik che ram kahōi ah.
