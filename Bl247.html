247. THERE IS A HAPPY LAND (R.S.680)

“In My Father’s house are many mansions…a place for you” (Jn.14:2)

ANDREW YOUNG Doh is F INDIAN AIR

1) Ram kahōi khat kaom, ram kahla ah,
Mi’ntheng hei om na kha vâr rangei mak;
A hla kahōi Chunghnung! Reng Christa manpâk na hla,
Alûrna hla sak ruh, bâng tik nei loah.

2) Ram kahōi kajôn ah nang ni hong ruh,
Khan om sôt mak jo ruh, in-êng mak ruh;
Eini inpâk sik me, set leh thi om mak jo ning,
Rōngpu hneng ni om leh, hōi bâng mak ning.

3) Hmit rakip kavâr jo, ram kahōi ah,
Lungset chi mak jo ning, Pa âng sûng ah;
Aram kalûr sûng ah Reng lukhu nikhu sik jo,
Sûn nêk ah kavâr mi kachang sik me.
