223. THE COMING OF THE KINGDOM (S.S.168)

“Look up…lift up your heads…your redemption drawe nigh.” (Lk.21:28)

DANIEL W. WHITTLE Doh is Bb JAMES McGRANAHAN

1) Marvân chung ting khōpui kalûr tak in eini ahngâk me,
Khan ah khan mi’ntheng kum tin ah kapung ṭhak;
Pathen ram jōng tlung nasik kha anitin ah kahnai jo,
Pathen ram jōng tlung nasik pha kahnai jo.

Oh! Pathen ram jōng tlung sik pha kahnai jo,
Oh! Pathen ram jōng tlung sik pha kahnai jo;
Oh! Kamalung inrin roh,
Pêngkun vâng ajōng bêk sik Sandampân;
Jôn lâr sik kalûr tak ah.

2) Inhôr na khōpui asyem sa tuipui rekhat ting kaom,
Tlensōtpa jôn lâr ning sik kha en ṭhak ruh;
Hâkser na rake sûng ah inphut eini alei hûksōt,
Pathen ram jōng tlung nasik pha kahnai jo.

3) Pathen ram jōng tlung ning ni’nTlensōtpa einin nihmu sik,
Mandon phâk lo pha tak ah jōng inlâr thei;
Hallelujah! Athi vâng ni’n Tlensōtpa lûr ṭhak rasoh,
Oh! Pathen ram jōng tlung sik pha kahnai jo.

4) Vânhnōi ralei mi’n anhngâk-hngâk cheah kachâm ri jo hei,
Oh! Anmalung kangui ah katlasam hei;
Pathen ṭong Intheng vâng marvân omchen kha manthei hei ruh,
Pathen ram jōng tlung nasik pha kahnai jo.
