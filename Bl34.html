34. YIELD NOT TO TEMPTATION (S.S.698)

“To him that overcometh will I give to eat of the tree of life.” (Rev.2:7)

H.R.P. Doh is BbHORATIO R. PALMER

1) Ṭhat mak na kaida ruh, ṭhat mak set kachang,
Tor nân abôm sik chei, alak tor nasik;
Hma lang jôn êt û-na, anthim manvâr ruh,
Jesu en ṭhak û-na, anthôpui sik chei.

Der ruh inṭangpui sik ah,
Malung mông apêk sik chei;
Nang ni’n nander ṭhak inchu,
Anṭangpui nger sik chei.

2) Ṭhat mak na kaida ruh, majôm mak û-na,
Pathen kaja û-na hri maset mak ruh;
Sing leh vâr hmu nasik malung mannêm ruh,
Jesu en ṭhak û-na, anṭang pui sik chei.

3) Lên lukhu ahmu sik hrât insâng pên in,
Setna achang innum tak asen inchu;
Jesu ni’n Tlensōtpân ratha apêk sik,
Jesu en ṭhak û-na, anṭang pui sik chei.
