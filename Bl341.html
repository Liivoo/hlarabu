341. TAKE TIME TO BE HOLY (S.S.608)

“Be ye holy; for I am holy.” (I Pet.1:16)

W.D. LONGSTAFF Doh is F GEORGE C. STEBBINS

1) Nân theng nasik ah Rōngpu leh inthlop roh,
Ahneng ah om inna aṭong majôm roh;
Pathen sa chang inna katlasam sen roh,
Hei rakip kanêk ah khan pêk na jong roh.

2) Nân theng na kha mipui in hmu hei rasoh,
Jesu rōk leh kadik tak ah inthlop roh;
Ama en ṭhak inna napō nasik ah,
Nachôn ṭhat na kha mihe’n hmu hei rasoh.

3) Nân theng nasik Ama na hlam mankei roh,
Hei lai ênnum Ama hlonsōt phit mak roh;
Nânpâk na, nânreng na Rōngpu jui ṭhak roh,
Jesu jui ṭhak inna aṭong taksen roh.

4) Intheng ah om roh, na rathla man mông roh,
Namalung ah omchen akheng ah pe roh;
Rathla’ntheng in lungset ah ṭhōi che rasoh,
Marvân chung ah nang na om thei nasik ah.
