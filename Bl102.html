102. FAINT, YET PURSUING (S.S.681)

“…he and the three hundred men faint, yet pursuing.” (Judges.8:4)

W.R. GRISWOLD Doh is EbGEORGE C. STEBBINS

1) Ni tlâksam innum masō mak rih,
Inpâk na nihmu thei nasik ah;
Jesu Christa kahnung jui ṭhak rih,
Asena hmun rakip ah jui rih.

Ani katlai Ama jui ṭhak rih,
Ka tlasam eng tiah mahngi mak rih;
Hrât na apêk sik me,
Ni Rōngpu Jesu Christa kahnung jui ṭhak rih.

2) Ni tlâksam innum mahngi mak rih,
Khōdeng theina nidên num de rih;
Eini jârra thi – khō ahlo jo,
Ni tor nasik hrât na lak om mak.

3) Ni tlâksam innum mahngi mak rih,
Khōdeng theina nidên num de rih;
Christa Rōngpu cross rôn ṭhak inna,
Jōng ruh eini’n num rôn pui jo rih.
