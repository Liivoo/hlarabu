195. SAFE IN THE GLORY LAND (R.S.422)

“And they shall see His face….” (Rev.22:4)

JAMES L. BLACK Doh is Ab JOHN R. SWENEY

1) Mi intheng hei hlam marō kan hro lai,
Jesu Christa’n kan hlam akei;
Marvân ting kan jôn ah kase ung ah,
Vârpa ârsi’n kan hlam akei.

Marvân inn kahōi kan jôn ah,
Inpâk na leh hring na kan hmu sik;
Kan tlung inchu inpâk sik jo ung,
Hring na ram kahōi ah.

2) Taksen na hlam kadik kan hro lai hin,
Inlungset ṭong kaṭha kan jui;
Rōngpu kan hmu leh inpâk sik jo ung,
Ama leh kalêng sōm sik ung.

3) Hrang kanchet innum, kan inreng innum,
Thina, tuipui, hmeipui, kâr âm;
Ni Rōngpu Jesûn re amanke sik,
Amanpâk hla nisak sik jo.
