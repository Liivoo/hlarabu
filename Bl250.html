250. BEAUTIFUL VALLEY OF EDEN (S.S.908)

“There remaineth a rest to the people of God.” (Heb.4:9)

WILLIAM O. CUSHING Doh is Eb WILLIAM F. SHERWIN

1) Phaichâm kahōi Eden rahōn!
Inreng na kabâng sik;
Malungset mi’nreng hei sik ah,
Nalungset châm hngat ah.

Phaichâm kahōi Eden rahōn!
Inn marit leh intheng;
Inreng na inphut kahei thlir ah,
Nalungset châm hngat na kha.

2) Malung inreng hei sik ah khan,
Na sana vâr kavâr;
Marvân Ngeva hei hla sak kha,
Jōng inthang ram tin ah.

3) Khan ah khan inn kahōi kanei,
Katlensa hei om na;
Khōpui kalûr tak sûng ah khan,
Hla marit kha ansak.
