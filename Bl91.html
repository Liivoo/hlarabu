91. OH! WONDROUS NAME! (S.S.96)

“Wonderful, Counselor. The Mighty God.” (Isai.9:10)

VICTORIA FRANCES Doh is CIRA D. SANKEY

1) Ama rahming inthang tak kha,
Ngeva hên anmanhret;
Ajōng sik anlei manhret eh!
Vânhnōi inhôr na Pa!

Hringsōt na ṭong ani’n theipa,
Akahrât pên kaeh;
Kum khō hring na Rōngpu,
Ama Pathen Sapa kaeh.

2) Anmanpâk arahming inthang,
Marvân mi intheng hên;
Akalûrpên Ama rahming,
Eini in ngai nasik.

3) Manpâk ruh arahming inthang,
Vânhnōi intlen Ama;
Ama jârra lungset ni hmu,
Marvân ram tlung nasik.
