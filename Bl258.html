258. FORWARD! (R.S.877)

“Put on the whole armour of God…to stand against…the devil.” (Ephe.6:11)

CARRIE E.E. BRECK Doh is Bb GRANT COLFAX TULLAR

1) Ni kadōi hei kasō Christa’n hlam akei,
Atir na hmun tin ah insûm hêr mak min;
Alungdo khet kha nimanhret mak innum,
Hmun tin ah nijui ṭhak sik.

Hma lang nam ruh Rōngpu’n ati me,
Ṭongtep sa ram kajôn ah se ruh;
Hma lang nam ruh hla ansak lai seng,
Ni Reng Christa’n amantor sik me.

2) Christa kha eini’n ni taksen ṭhak inchu,
Seitân maṭhit jârra inchek hêr mak min;
Kangam tak ah rathla chem jâm hmang pumma,
Akahnung nijui ṭhak sik.

3) Ni hrât na pōnlek kha kadom mansâng ruh,
Ṭhat mak, dik mak hên anhnai tor mak nasik;
Ni hlam kakei Christa’n vânhnōi ator jo,
Eini’n Ama jui ṭhak rih.

4) Sôt mak jo ning heituk re ahrât innum,
Torna chen sōm pumma injôk sik jo me;
Inpâk ah tor na hla kahōi nisak sik,
Akahnung nijui inchu.
