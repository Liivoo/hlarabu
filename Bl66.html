66. WILL THE CIRCLE BE UNBROKEN? (3rd ALEX.15)

“…the whole family in heaven and earth is named…” (Ephe.3:15)

ADA R. HABERSHONDoh is AbCHARLES H. GABRIEL

1) Marvân kahōi lungset ram khi,
Mi intheng hei inn kaeh;
Anhneng ah om thei nasik ah,
Vânhnōi setna hlon sōt roh.

Vânhnōi ralei lahôn hmang sik,
Jajâm ah, jajâm ah;
Mi intheng hei leng na ram ah,
Ṭimre hên anhngâk me.

2) Kumsôt inn kahōi tak ah khin,
Innsung khat kachang sik me;
Khava inn ah kaom sik ah,
Phung leh châng rakip anbêk.

3) Tlensōtpa thina hmun kahōi,
Lungset na ṭong inhri ṭhak;
Insyem ṭhat pha a-om lai hin,
Alungset ṭong taksen roh.

4) Inkhat sâm ah kasōk hei ah,
Innsung khat karōk jo hei;
Inn kahōi ah ṭimre hei leh,
Inhmu ning kaom tâk mo?
