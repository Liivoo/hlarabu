47. OH! HAPPY DAY (S.S.866)

“…whoso trusted in the Lord, happy is he.” (Prov.16:20)

PHILIP DODDRIDGEDoh is GEDWARD F. RIMBAUT

1) Rōngpu rahming ah inpâk ruh,
Ni’n Tlensōtpa Jesu Christa!
Malung katla he’m ṭhōi jo rih,
Ahneng ah ṭhōi sik amadu.

Inpâk ruh, inpâk ruh, Jesûn niset arôn sō jo,
Der nasik dân amanthei jo, anitin ah hrât thei nasik;
Inpâk ruh, inpâk ruh, niset rakip amantheng jo.

2) Nide sik kha ade pêk jo,
Amân aṭhōi na jui ṭhak rih;
Innêm leh lungset vâng abêk,
Manpâk êt ruh ni Rōngpu kha.

3) Malung kangûi um thô jo ruh,
Ama châm inhngat na kaeh;
Ni’n hmang jônnum aṭhōi sik me,
Ama manpâk ruh, dam nasik.

4) Marvân ah Rōngpu kare khan,
Sûn tin ah malung amanpâk;
Ni hring lai, kani atlâk hma,
Heitik ah hring na ṭōn tlo rih.
