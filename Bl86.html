86. ARE YOUR WINDOWS OPEN? (S.S.167)

“His windows…open toward Jerusalem, he kneeled...” (Dan.6:10)

P.P.B. Doh is EPHILIP P. BLISS

1) Hebrew mi sōk kachang kha nân hmu mo?
Jing leh khōhlei ader ṭhak sik;
Apin den ah Zion amanhret ṭhak,
A Pathen hneng ader ṭhak.

Jerusalem lang ting kot nân mahlang mo?
Inreng na ênnum lei sôt tak mak jo ning;
Rōngpu inrang tak ah jōng kaṭum jo,
Ni hngâk ṭhak sik kha kachang jo.

2) Hmei ah anhlôn chei innum ṭi mak ruh,
Humpui om na achang innum;
Pathen in aṭhōi sik me, ṭit mak ruh,
Rathla ajōng tîr pêk sik.

3) Daniel ang ah reng um ṭit mak hei ruh,
Pathen in aṭhōi jârra khan;
Ni khōdeng theina ênnum hla sak rih,
Hei rakip ṭi sik om mak.
