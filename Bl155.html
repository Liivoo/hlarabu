155. A CALL FOR REAPERS (S.S.1086)

“The harvest truly is plenteous but the laborers are few.” (Matt.9:37)

J.O. THOMPSON Doh is AbJ.B.O. CLEMN

1) Hmun rakip ah sa kaom kha,
Ât pha katluk sō jo hei;
Ram kavâr leh phai ah mi he’m,
Tûnchu kahmin sō jo hei.

Oh! Rōngpu! Kan derna che hi,
Nang in jōng rangei pêk roh;
Sa ka ât sik jōng tir inna,
An inhmang sō mak hma hin.

2) Jing leh sûn ṭim achang innum,
Kani kha atlâk innum;
Oh! Rōngpu! Sa ka ât sik ah,
Anmani tir sōt hei roh.

3) Oh! Inrang roh kaṭōi jo hei,
Sana kapō saphe kha;
Rōngpu hneng na mathlung inchu,
Inpâk ah na om pui sik.
