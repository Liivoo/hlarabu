269. WELCOME FOR ME (S.S.469)

“When he was a great way off his father saw him.” (Lk.15:20)

FANNY J. CROSBY Doh is Bb WILLIAM J. KIRKPATRICK

1) Chung ah Vaṭhu kapō kalêng eng ram tin ah,
Katlânsōt Rōngpu hneng ah inphut;
Tâk nalungset len in kei nahei bêk nôk eng,
Kamanhret kên nalungset na kha.

Katlai seng ah na bêkna kha,
Kei sik inpâk ah bêkna kha;
Vaṭhu kapō Nahneng ah sik ah,
Rōngpu! Tûn kên kahong jōn che.

2) Jōng na mathla vâng ram tin ah kajōng innum,
Ka Rōngpu âng sûng rōk kha kaeh;
Nalungset kare khan kei nahei bêk nôk eng,
Kamanhret kên nalungset kare.

3) Rōngpu kanei anphut thi sik ṭit mak jo eng,
Thi nasik in kei ahnai innum;
Kani tlâk mak na ram marvân ka-en ṭhak sik,
Malung ah inpâk hla kasak sik.
