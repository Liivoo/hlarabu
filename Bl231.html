231. LORD I’M COMING HOME (S.S.471)

“I will rise and go to my father….” (Lk.15:18)

E.A. BARNES Doh is Ab WM. J. KIRKPATRICK

1) Pathen ânphut lei kahla eng,
Tûn inn kahong jôn;
Kaset hlam hro lei kasôt jo,
Rōngpu! Kahong eng.

Kahong eng, kahong eng,
Set nôk mak nasik;
Lungset ah nasa chang rong,
Rōngpu! Kahong eng.

2) Kum katam tak len hmang jo eng,
Tûn kahong jôn che;
Insir leh hmit rathli pumma,
Nahneng kahong jôn.

3) Kaset hlam hro lei kasôt jo,
Tûn kei kahong eng;
Nalungset ṭong taksen jo rong,
Rōngpu! Kahong eng.

4) Kataksa leh karathla hi,
Tûn chu kachâm jo;
Karathla aman nger pêk roh,
Rōngpu! Kahong eng.
