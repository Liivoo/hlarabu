219. THE ANGEL’S SONG (S.S.28)

“Glory to God in the highest and on earth peace….” (Lk.2:28)

R.L. Doh is G ROBERT LOWRY

1) Jânṭim lai ah rasa jōn thang,
Marvân hla kahōi tak jōn sak ah;
An hla sak kha marit kaeh,
Akasak hei kha marvân Tirle.

Chunghnungpên ah lênpên rasoh,
’Hnōi ah inpâk na ṭong lêng sonna;
Vânhnōi ah inthang sō rasoh,
Mihring inpâk êt rasoh.

2) Kemông kakhêm hei khan anhmu,
An rangei ah hla marit tak kha;
Kakhup hei ah chibei anbuk,
Hla kalûr marit ansak lai seng.

3) Pathen Sahrik manthi Jesu!
Nibat kachôi sik ajōng kaṭum;
Malung inreng na ajōng de,
Niset karôn ni Rōngpu Jesu!
