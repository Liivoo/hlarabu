375. THE FIRST NOEL (C.H.B.154)

“…unto to you is born this day…a Saviour…Christ the Lord.” (Lk.2:11)

Doh is D ENGLISH MELODY

1) Amankei Noel! Tirle hei ta hri,
Jōng katlung jo kemông kakhêm hei hneng;
Hleipuk ah an kemông ankhem lai,
Phebi jân thangṭim kadai ah.

Noel! Noel! Noel! Noel!
Israel hei Reng kha inte jo.

2) Mi kasing hei in chung ârsi anjui,
Avâr khan nisō ting hlam akei hei;
Ankutchôi bôm an mahlang sōt ah,
Inkûn hei ah an kamuk jo.

3) Eini num malung leh ratha khat ah,
Ni’ntlensōtpa manpâk hla kha sak rih;
Malung leh ratha inpêk sōt ah,
Intet Reng khi kamuk jo rih.







Words & Musical Notes Translation: M. Chungrengkhup Kom
