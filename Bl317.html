317. THE HARBOUR BELL (S.S.395)

“…our Lord…saved us…according to His own purpose and grace….” (II Tim.1:9)

JOHN H. YATES Doh is F IRA D. SANKEY

1) Hringkhō hi tui innông kapō setnân ator man hmang rih jo,
Setna thlipui ahrâng pha leh sumpui ang ah jōng kajing rih;
Inri tak ah ani ôn me, rakōng ṭhum hmun dâr mahring in,
Anhmang sahei hring na hlam ting Rathla invai hei inpâk ruh.

Hong ruh, hong ruh, anreng – anvai,
Anibêk me, masôn jo rih;
Ṭhum hmun anphut anibêk me,
Marvân dâr inhring na hmun ah.

2) Bêkna kare masôn jo rih, kare insâng ah masôn rih,
Eini ani ônna ting son, kare insâng ah masôn rih;
Mi katam tak aṭhen jo hei, Amanvâr chêt chêt sik jo hei,
Kahneng ah hong ruh tiah abêk, rakōng ṭhum hmun dâr inhring khan.

3) Hōnna kahrât, insing thei roh, Rōngpu ṭongtep kha jui ṭhak roh,
Torna hla kha nisak bei sik, akade tor mihei seng kha;
Pathen hrât nân abôm sik hei, Alungset na hri thei chang mak,
Tuipui in-nông rekhat ting khan, rakōng ṭhum hmun dâr in abêk.

4) Lungset kanei Rōngpu! Jōng roh, jōng syem ther roh kanhringkhō hi,
Oh! Aṭhōi roh Chungkhōpui khan, hōi chunghnung na innpui sûng ah;
Thlipui-khōpui khan om mak jo, kan hōi hla sak bâng mak jo ning,
Thisen ah kan kaṭhōisōt Pa khan keini inhngat na apêk.
