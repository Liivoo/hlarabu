187. AT THE CROSS (S.S.883)

“I am not ashamed.” (II Tim.1:12)

ISAAC WATTS Doh is ER.E. HUDSON

1) Eh! Jesu kei inlungset Pa!
Kei sik ade pêk jo;
Na thisen marit natôk jo,
Set karôn kei jârra.

Cross chung ah! (x2) Khōvâr kahmu na khan,
Kaset malung inthim amanvâr sō;
Kha kân hmu jo ah, kên ka taksen ah,
Kahōi jo eng tûnchu malung ah.

2) Set kanei kei asen jârra,
Athisen kalōng jo;
Amalung set inlak tak leh,
Aṭhat na tei tor lo.

3) Ni kasyem Pa eini jârra,
Inreng tak ah kathi;
Kani leh thlân anhmu tâkkha,
En ngam mak hei Ahmai.

4) Kaset hmai atlai mak ravâng,
Cross chung ah vahmu ning;
Kamalung in mandon sonna,
Hmit rathli’m lōng rasoh.

5) Kahmit rathli alōng innum,
Kabat chôi tor mak ning;
Jesu! Ka rathla kapêk che,
Heite lak nei mak eng.
