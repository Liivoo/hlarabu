147. JESUS IS CALLING! (S.S.396)

“Come unto Me and I will give you rest.” (Matt.11:28)

FANNY J. CROSBY Doh is CGEORGE C. STEBBINS

1) Jesûn lungset tak ah nang abêk che,
Tuning hinnum ala bêk che;
Heisamo lungset na hmun nadahlon?
Tûn kahla chêt chêt jo che.

Tuning hinnum abêk ṭhak che,
Jesûn abêk che lungset tak ah nang abêk che.

2) Setna kanei hei châm hong inhnga ruh,
Tuning hinnum abêk ṭhak chei;
Nanset rôn Ahneng ah va maṭhum ruh,
Amân nang ni hōt mak chei.

3) Jesûn ahngâk chei Ahneng ah hong ruh,
Tuning hinnum ahngâk ṭhak chei;
Nanset rôn pumma Ahneng ah hong ruh,
Hong ruh! Insûm mak jo ruh.

4) Jesu bêkna kare kha rangei ruh,
Tuning hinnum rangei ṭhak ruh;
Tak kasen hên inpâk na anhmu sik,
Inrang tak ah hong hrât ruh.
