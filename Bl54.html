54. BEULAH LAND! (R.S.389)

“…new Jerusalem…prepared as a bride….” (Rev.21:2)

EDGAR PAGE STITESDoh is GJOHN R. SWENEY

1) Ning tluk na ram katlung jo eng,
Nei rakip keita kachang jo;
Khan ah khan inthim kavâr sō,
Jân inti om nôk mak jo ning.

Oh! Beulah Land! Ram kahōi tak,
Na tlâng insâng ah kangir eng;
Tui-ôngpui re ah kahei thlir,
Kei sik ah naman tluk inn kha;
Avâr lûrje hri thei chang mak,
Kumsôt innpui marvân inn kha.

2) Kan Tlensōtpân alôn pui eng,
Ânthlop pui jo eng kahōi ah;
Alungset kut vâng akei eng,
Marvân innpui kakim-ve ah.

3) Marim kahōi thli hrâng na ram,
Thing kûng rakip in-êmreng sō;
Reipâr marit hei ṭi phung mak,
Kumsôt hring na tuidung lōng na.

4) Ngeva hei leh inthlop na hmun,
Pōn bang kasi Ngeva hei leh;
Mahring kahōi ah marvân ah,
Tlensōt na hla kahōi ansak.
