356. WONDERFUL WORDS OF LIFE (S.S.357)

“The words that I speak unto you they are spirit, and they are life.” (Jn.6:63)

P.P.B. Doh is G PHILIP P. BLISS

1) Kare kahōi ah sak nôk roh,
Hring na ṭong inlak tak;
Aṭhat na kha ei manthei roh,
Hring na ṭong inlak tak;
Aṭong hei hi hring na,
Hring na ṭong amanthei.

Ṭong kahōi tak, ṭong inlak tak,
Hring na ṭong inlak tak;
Ṭong kahōi tak, ṭong inlak tak,
Hring na ṭong inlak tak.

2) Christa’n mi rakip amanchen,
Hring na ṭong inlak tak;
Mi kaset hei kha abêk ṭhak,
Hring na ṭong inlak tak;
Mankase ah asem,
Marvân hlut theina ṭong.

3) Ṭong kahōi Gospel ṭong Intheng,
Hring na ṭong inlak tak;
Apêk lungmông, rangei mandam,
Hring na ṭong inlak tak;
Jesu rōk kha hring na,
Intheng na ṭhak Ama.
