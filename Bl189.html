189. THE SUFFERING OF THE LORD

“The place…called Calvary…there they crucified Him.” (Lk.23:33)

Doh is Bb

1) Tlensōtpa thisen jōng kalōng,
Kaset intheng nasik;
Alu intheng kha inkûn ah,
Ka rathla hring nasik.

Kemôngte! Nun kahōi Pa kha Calvary ah kathi,
Kemôngte! Thâ sa kathōi nôk Pa kha;
Kan Tlensōtpa kaeh.

2) Set leh suk ṭhe kanei jârra,
Thing cross lêr ah kaṭap;
Lungset marit lum alo eng,
Apêk kumsôt hring na.

3) Kani’n Ahmai aser tâkkha,
Avâr manchên ngam mak;
Tlensōtpân ka setna rakip,
Arôn pêk cross chung ah.

4) Kara ngōi kên um kanam sik,
Rōngpu cross âng kûng ah;
Ade nat na kamandon sik,
Ahme kahmu pha leh.
