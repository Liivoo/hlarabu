31. I AM COMING (S.S.481)

“Come unto Me…and I will give you rest.” (Matt.11:28)

ALLIE STARBRIGHT Doh is FIRA D. SANKEY

1) Inkhat sara kachang kei hi,
Rōngpu bêk na majôm rong;
Christa hmu leh taksen tak ah,
Tûn Nahneng kahong jôn jo.

Kei kahong eng! Kei kahong eng,
Vâng hmu nasik kahong eng;
Kei kahong eng! Kei kahong eng,
Nahneng ah sik kahong eng.

2) Malung innêm antheng pên Nang!
Kahong jôn che Jesu kên;
Ahngâk ṭhak roh ahei tik ah,
Hlôn sōt nôk mak nasik ah.

3) Tûn Nahneng ah kaom lai hin,
Ka rathla châm in hnga sik;
Jingkâr kani sōk nôk hma seng,
Lungset vâng hong kathang eng.

4) Inpâk tak ah ahngâk inna,
Setna ei hûksōt inna;
Kahmai hi amantlai pêk roh,
Kaset amantheng pêk roh.
