304. ON JORDAN’S STORMY BANKS (S.S.973)

“Thine eyes shall see…behold the land that is very far off.” (Isai.33:17)

S. STENNETT Doh is G T.C. O’KANE

1) Jordan tuipang ah kangir eng,
Kahei thlir kahmit vâng;
Canaan ram kahōi leh aṭhat,
Kaom na hmun sik kha.

Châm hngat na ram kahōi leh inpâk na,
Tui rekhat ram kahōi tak ah khan;
Kemôngte leh Mosi hlather kha sak pumma,
Jesu leh kalêng sap sik me.

2) Phai inchâm leh Jesu lêng na,
Vâr hlop rangei mak min;
Pathen leh Asapa om na,
Jân um om mak jo ning.

3) Ram kahōi kha kahmu pha leh,
Vâng rakip kachen sik;
Rōngpu hme kha kahmu pha leh,
Kumsôt kahring sik eng.

4) Ka rathla inpâk na kasip,
Kei lêng sôt mak jo ning;
Jordan tuipui alen innum,
Re kake nger sik eng.
