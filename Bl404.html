404. HMŌIṬHŌI KANI (M.C.K.2)

“… shall a man…cleave unto his wife: and…be one flesh.” (Gen.2:24)

M. CHUNGRENGKHUP KOM Doh is G 11/08/2005

1) Eden ah Pathen ṭongpêk,
Hōi katlung jo hmōiṭhōi kani;
Sak leh thang, sim leh mâr rakip,
Hong jo ruh Pathen manpâk rih.

Manpâk rih Chunghnung Pathen!
Hmōiṭhōina kani kalûr;
Pathen tamanchun sangram,
Chang na hringkhō sung ah;
Pathen vâng chen ṭhak ruh.

2) Ralei hringkhō om sung ah,
Hring na, dam na, hōi na, mông na;
Pathen vâng ṭhat hei rakip hi,
Hōi tlung rasoh nan hringkhō ah.

3) Ṭōn kaṭha tlo nasik ah,
Insûm mak ruh nan hringkhō ah;
Innshung kahōi hei syem sōt ruh,
Intei puithei innshung chang ruh.
