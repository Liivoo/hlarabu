50. THE LORD IS KING! (S.S.1081)

“The Lord is King forever and ever.” (Ps.10:16)

J.L. STERINGDoh is GIRAD D. SANKEY

1) Kumsôt ah hla kha sak ruh, marvân ah kasōk,
Na Pathen Chunghnungpên! kha lûr ṭhak rasoh;
Pathen rōisyem vâr na khan,
An Serângdâr mahring vâng khan;
Inhnak tak ah an-ât ah Reng! anti jo.

2) Tor na hla kha rangei ruh, vânhnōi inthang jo,
Tui sōret kalên kapōah hōi hla ansak;
Hallelujah! Manpâk ruh!
Ralei hi atlung sō kâr seng;
Kalûrpên ah om rasoh, tiah ansak.

3) Marvân mihe’n manpâk ruh Inthum ah Inkhat,
Nipa Pathen leh Asa, Rathla Intheng;
Kum leikō âm inlak mak,
Kumsôt ah ni’n Tlensōtpa kha;
Ama kha anmanpâk ṭhak Rōngpu! tiah.
