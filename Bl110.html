110. I WILL NOT FORGET THEE (118)

“…yet will I not forget thee.” (Isai.49:15)

C.H.G. Doh is AbCHARLES H. GRABRIEL

1) Mahngi mak che ning tiah ṭong atêp eng,
Ka rathla heite’n ṭhōi manhmak tor mak ning;
Khō inthim na hmun phaichâm rekhat lang ting,
Kum leikōah hring na kani kavâr ṭhak.

Mahngi mak che ning na hring lai seng,
Kên kamatu ṭhak che, Masō jên mak che ning;
Mahngi mak che ning nahring lai seng,
Nân Tlênpa eng kaeh, Ka-ên ṭhak sik che.

2) Lungset leh marit tak ah lum alo eng,
Hei vâng mo alungset na kama le sik?
Hukmanhring na ṭong hri manthang le jo rong,
Hring na ṭong atêp mi’n anchen thei nasik.

3) Mahngi mak che ning tiah ṭong atêp eng,
Manpâk na hla sak pumma chôi ka-ân rong;
Vânhnōi ṭhōn chang nân adahlôn eng innum,
Anitin ah Jesûn kei ahnai ṭhak eng.

4) Sana kot kûng ah kei kangir pha tak leh,
Inreng khōdeng thei rakip kabōi sō sik;
Inn ah hong hlut roh sōk kaṭha! sōk kadik!
Inti bêkna rasa kha inthei sik jo.
