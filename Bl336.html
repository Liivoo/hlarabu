336. GUIDE ME OH! THOU GREAT JEHOVAH! (L.S.K. Hla.424)

“…guide me with Thy counsel, and afterward receive me to glory.” (Ps.73:24)

W.WILLIAM & J.HUGHES Doh is A [1st TUNE] C.W.M.RHONDDA

1) Aṭhōi roh Chunghnung Jehovah!
Kan vai na ralei ah hin;
Ka tlasam eng, Nang kahrât che,
Na bân hrât vâng aṭhōi roh;
Marvân bu vâng, marvân bu vâng,
Kei avei mankhop ṭhak roh;
Kei avei mankhop ṭhak roh.

2) Tui hnâr kapût jōng mahlang roh,
Na hring tui jōng manlōng roh;
Kalôn le na hmun rakip ah,
Na vârna vâng aṭhoi roh;
Kahrât Chunghnung! Kahrât Chunghnung!
Ka hring na leh kan thup na;
Ka hring na leh kan thup na.

3) Jordan tuipui in-nông chung ah,
Ṭit mak nasik ei pe roh;
Tui tlân hrât na ei matu roh,
Canaan ram katlung nasik;
Manpâk na hla, manpâk na hla,
Vâng kamanpâk ṭhak sik che;
Vâng kamanpâk ṭhak sik che.
