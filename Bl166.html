166. CHRIST RECIEVETH SINFUL MEN (S.S.390)

“They that be whole need not a physician but they that are sick.” (Matt.9:12)

EMMA F. BEVAN Doh is DJAMES McGRANAHAN

1) Christa’n set karôn alo,
Mi rakip hei hri pêk ruh;
Hlam le ah kase hei leh,
Anvai hei, anhmang sa hei.

Sak inna, sak nôk inna,
Mi kaset alo Christa’n;
Manhret na manthei hei ruh,
Christa’n set karôn alo.

2) Hong ruh hōi apêk sik chei,
Taksen ruh Christa ṭong hri,
Amân set karôn alo,
Nang um alo nger sik che.

3) Ka ṭhat mak na om mak jo,
Ka malung intheng seng jo,
Ka setna amantheng jo,
Ṭit mak eng om chen dân kha.

4) Set karôn Christa’n alo,
Kei um ahlon sōt mak jo,
Ka setna amantheng jo,
Antheng seng kachang jo eng.
