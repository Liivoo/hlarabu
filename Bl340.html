340. THE OLD RUGGED CROSS (Ch.S.H.155)

“…bearing His cross, went forth into a place called…Golgotha.” (Jn.19:17)

G.B. Doh is Bb GEORGE BENNARD

1) Ram kahla tlâng chung ah cross marō kha kangir,
Inzak leh khōdeng thei hmêrchik na;
Khava cross marō kha kamaritpên kaeh,
Vânhnōi set karôn khan anlei thâ.

Khava cross marō kha kamarit,
Kahrât na kamatung mak hma seng;
Cross marō kha kamatu tlit sik,
Ni khat lên lukhu leh lân thleng sik.

2) Khava cross marō kha vânhnōi mi’n anlei hōt,
Kei sik ah chu marit tak kaeh;
Pathen Kemôngte kha alûrna adahlon,
Calvary inthim ah jōng kathi.

3) Cross marō chung ah khan thisen marit kalōng,
Aṭhat inlak tak kha kên kahmu;
Khava cross chung ah Jesûn ade ah kathi,
Arangei mandam ah amantheng.

4) Khava cross marō kha kadik tak ah jui rong,
Khōdeng thei, inzak na achang ah;
Innpui kahla tak ah nikhat chu abêk sik,
Kumsôt ah alûrna kachen sik.
