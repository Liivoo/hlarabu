209. THERE’S ROOM FOR ALL (S.S.820)

“And yet there is room.” (Lk.14:22)

FANNY J. CROSBY Doh is Ab GEORGE C. STEBBINS

1) Rathla inhmang hûksōt roh, hlam invai vaṭhōi roh,
De thei tak ah ṭhōi hei roh, hlam kasin a-ênnum;
Lungset tak ah ṭhōi hei roh, Christa bêkna vâng khan,
Revei na innsung ah hmun mi rakip sik kaom.

Hmun kaom, hmun kaom,
Revei thlai intluk sō jo;
Tiah manthei hei roh.

2) Khōdeng theina hmun anphut, khō inthim kâr anphut,
Set khō inthim kâr anphut, sana khōvâr lang teng;
Asat manvâr intheng ah Jesu hneng be hei roh,
Revei thlai semsa kaeh, tiah manthei hei roh.

3) Lungset rathla vâng se roh, Rōngpu sik ah se roh,
Hringsōt na Rathla ṭong hi vânhnōi ah phôngsōt roh;
Inrang roh kani tlâk hma, jân jōng katlung bei sik,
Christa lungset rehōi kha mi rakip sik kaeh.
