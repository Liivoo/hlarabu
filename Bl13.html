13. WHEN I SHALL WAKE (S.S.909)

“I shall be satisfied, when I awake, with Thy likeness.” (Ps.17:15)

HORATIUS BONAR Doh is GGEORGE C. STEBBINS

1) Jing nisō in-êmreng ning kathōi pha,
Khava kani kha tla nôk mak jo ning;
Kanisō vâr kha in-êmreng ṭhak sik,
Khava ning ka malung katlai sik jo.

Kamalung katlai sik, kamalung katlai sik,
Jing nisō in-êmreng ning kathōi pha;
Kamalung katlai sik, kamalung katlai sik,
Jing nisō in-êmreng ning kathōi pha.

2) Na hmaisō vâr kalûr kha kahmu pha,
Nasa nakut vâng na kakō sik hei;
Vâng pêk na kot khâr na mahlang tak leh,
Khava ning ka malung katlai sik jo.

3) Lungset hei seng kha kahmu pha tak leh,
Inpâk tak ah kânkō pui sik jo hei;
Kei sik ah naṭhat, nadik inchên sik,
Khava ning ka malung katlai sik jo.

4) Kân Tlensōtpa hmaisō kahmu tak leh,
Athi pêk na eng kaman chên sik jo;
Aheitik ah hla vâng kamanpâk sik,
Khava ning ka malung katlai sik jo.
