3. THE CITY OF GOLD (S.S.819)

“The city was pure gold like unto clear glass.” (Rev.21:8)

FANNY J. CROSBYDoh is AbWILLIAM H. DOANE

1) Thina tuire khōpui kahōi tak khat kaom,
Ahōi dân hri manchên theilo kha!
Kani tlâk leh khō inthim hi’m omdin mak jo,
Khava Sana khōpui sûng ah khan.

Kani tlâk mak na ram,
Thingcher in-ai phung mak;
Mi intheng he’n an in Tlensōtpa anhmu ah,
Khava Sana khōpui sûng ah khan.

2) Khava ram ah ni’n Tlensōtpa ni Rōngpu kha,
Taksen kanei hei khan anhmu sik;
Kumsôt ah ârsi ang ah akavâr sik jo,
Khava khōpui kahōi tak ah khan.
