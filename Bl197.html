197. VICTORY THROUGH GRACE (S.S.673)

“He went forth conquering and to conquer.” (Rev.6:2)

S. MARTIN Doh is Bb JOHN R. SWENEY

1) Tûnchu re tlo na tor nasik ah Rōngpu khan ahrât chen ah,
A sepei rup hei re katlo sik re tuk na hmun lang ting khan;
Aloi hei re hma ajôn pui, re katlo sik amantluk,
An hlam kakei manpâk pumma, kahōi tak ah anmanpâk.

Re ṭit tâkkha tûn mo ator?
Tute om phit mak hei ning;
Mi kahrât hei sik ah khat chu,
Tor na ṭongkamsa kaeh.

2) Tûnchu re tlo na tor thei nasik Reng sa inlak kha tumo?
A sepei rakip aṭhōi hei kha heituk mo anmanpâk sik!
Ni Rōngpu leh ni’n Tlensōtpa, Sandampa Reng Sa kaeh,
Aram sûng ah marvân ârsi ka âng ah kavâr ṭhak hei.

3) Tûnchu re tlo na tor nasik ah vai karôn Rōngpu Nang che!
Ralei reng in-ông na leh alûrna akheng ah inhmang sō sik;
Vânhnōi abâng ning katlung ah taksen ah kangir hei kha,
An re antlo sō pha kha leh châm hngat na annei sik jo.
