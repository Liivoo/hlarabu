29. I WILL SING THE WONDROUS STORY (S.S.875)

“I will sing of the mercies of the Lord for ever.” (Ps.89:1)

FRANCIS H. RAWLEY Doh is EbROWLAND H. PRICHARD

1) Ṭong marō inlak hla sak rong,
Christa kei jârra kathi;
A inn marit ajōng dahlon,
Calvary tlâng ah sik ah.

Thuhming inlak hla kha sak rong,
Christa kei jârra kathi;
A lûrna hla kha sak û-na,
Alungset hri manthang ruh.

2) Kemông kapō len hmang jo eng,
Tâkkha Jesûn ajong sōt;
Abân ratha hrât na vâng khan,
Hringna hlam ah aṭhōi eng.

3) Ṭhat mak na lei kaom jo eng,
Tûn Jesûn ahmu nôk jo;
Ṭit nasik ahmun rakip ah,
Amân hrât na apêk eng.

4) Khō inthim in akatôm eng,
Setna karrâm kaom eng;
Tâkkha Rōngpûn a-om pui ah,
Setna heite nei mak eng.
