364. HARK! THE HERALD ANGELS SING (S.S.30)

“Glory to God in the highest and on earth peace….” (Lk.2:14)

CHARLES WESLEY Doh is G FELIX MENDELSSOHN

1) Rangei ruh Tirle hei hla, Reng ther inte lûr rasoh,
Ralei ah cham lêng rasoh, Pathen leh set kanei hei;
Inbêk-incham nôk jo hei, vânhnōi mitin inpâk ruh,
Bethlehem khōpui ah khan Reng Christa jōng inte jo;
Rangei ruh Tirle hei hla, Reng inte kha lûr rasoh.

2) Kumsôt Rengpa Christa kha Chunghnungpên ah an manpâk,
Alen hri ṭhak hûksōt Pa, nuhngâk intheng vonchôi ah;
Taksa ah jōng injōr jo, chôi ka-ân rih Tlensōtpa,
Immanuel! Jesu kha nihneng ah jōng kaom jo;
Mihring ah jōn jōr Reng kha manpâk ah om ṭhak rasoh.

3) Chamna Reng kha manpâk ruh, manpâk ruh kani ther ning,
Hring na khōvâr ajōng rôn, Amathla’n azâp mandam;
Mihring hei sandam nasik alûrna ajōng dahlon,
Intet ther ṭong ajōng rôn mihring hei har nasik ah;
Mihring ah jōng inte jo, Reng ther inte lûr rasoh.
