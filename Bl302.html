302. THERE IS POWER IN THE BLOOD (S.S.145)

“The blood of Jesus Christ, His Sōn, cleansêth us from all sin.” (1 Jn.1:7)

L.E.J. Doh is Bb L.E. JONES

1) Set kârra inphut intheng jo che mo?
Thisen rōk kaeh nân theng nasik;
Setna rakip nân nator sō sik mo?
Thisen ratha hrât rōk kaeh.

Thisen rōk khan ṭōn inlak ator,
Kemôngte thisen in;
Thisen rōk khan ṭōn inlak ator,
Kemôngte thisen marit khan.

2) Kavur nêk âm kabâng thei sik che mo?
Thisen rōk kha nabang thei nasik;
Ni hringkhō sei setnân amanbōi ri,
Jesu thisen rōk kha hring na.

3) Na Reng Christa Jesu ṭōn na tlo mo?
Thisen rōk kaeh ṭōn tlo nasik;
Nitin amanpâk hla nân nasak mo?
Thisen rōk kha nân taksen roh.
