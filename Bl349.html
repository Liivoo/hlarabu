349. BEHOLD! THE BRIDEGROOM COME (C.S.531)

“Watch…for ye know not when the Master…cometh….” (Mk.13:35)

J.M.W. Doh is Ab JOHN M. WHYTE

1) Akahring ṭhak kare kha nithei sik,
En roh! Rōngpu kajōng!
Jân inthim, ṭhangṭim pha kahngâk ṭhak kha,
Rathla hringkhō rôn kahrik kajâng sik;
Akahring ṭhak kare kha nithei sik,
En roh! Rōngpu kajōng!

Oh! Inrin ruh, Rōngpu jōng pha leh,
Oh! Inrin ruh, Rōngpu jōng pha leh;
Sûnṭim achang, Khōhlei achang,
Ṭhangṭim achang, Jingkâr achang;
Oh! Inrin ruh, Oh! Inrin ruh,
Oh! Inrin ruh, Rōngpu jōng pha leh.

2) Anrin ṭhak thaihmei invâr kha chang ruh,
En roh! Rōngpu kajōng!
Kajōng nger sik, an êng – ansûm innum,
Ama leh lungmông ah kalêng sik me;
Jing khōvâr kare kahōi nithei sik,
En roh! Rōngpu kajōng!
