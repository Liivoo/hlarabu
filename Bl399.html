399. MARVÂN RAM AH (M.C.K.32)

“Sing praises to God…sing praises unto our King, sing praises.” (Ps.47:6)

M. CHUNGRENGKHUP KOM Doh is G 12/06/2018

1) Châm hngat nathei om mak hiva ralei ah,
Ṭa jai sui leh inreng na kasip;
Kachim jo ka hringkhō hi heitik katlung sik eng?
Ka marvân ram inn kahōi ah.

Oh! Heituk hrep ah kahōi sik jo mo!
Amanpâk na hla kahōi;
Bâng lo jên ah nisak sik jo inpâk ah,
Khōpui kalûr chunghnung Oh! Marvân ram ah.

2) Ni’nṭhen puisa marit ṭimrele hei leh,
Lân hmu nôk sik me marvân ram ah;
Ni Pathen leh kumsôt ah kalêng sap sik jo me,
Chitik neilo kalêng sik me.

3) Marvân Khōpui ther Jerusalem ah khin,
Tui hre, vonṭâm om nôk mak jo ning;
Hmit rathli sōk na rakip amanhi pêk sik me,
Thi leh nat hi’m om mak jo ning.

[Bâng lo jên ah nisak sik, Oh! Marvân ram ah]
(Repeat 3 times to fade)
