118. WALKING IN THE SUNSHINE (S.S.1099)

“He saith unto them, follow Me.” (Matt.4:19)

W. ROBERT LINDSAY Doh is BbIRA D. SANKEY

1) Kani vâr ah se ruh, vâr kahōi ah khan,
Jingkâr radai kârra reipâr ang û-na;
Kamông tak ah se ruh, nitin ah jui ruh,
Hmun tin ah Jesu jui ruh.

Jui ruh, Jesu kha jui ṭhak ruh,
Jui ruh, nitin ah jui ruh;
Jui ruh, asena rakip ah,
Hmun tin ah Jesu kahnung jui ruh.

2) Khō inthim na hmun leh avâr na hmun âm,
Inpâk tak ah om ruh, inreng kabâng sik;
Tlensōtpa bêkna me kare rangei ruh,
Hmun tin ah Jesu jui ruh.

3) Sana vâr ra-âng leh inpâk na ram ah,
Thlipui ahrâng innum ṭit na nei mak ruh;
Alungset na ṭong kha mandon ṭhak û-na,
Hmun tin ah Jesu jui ruh.
