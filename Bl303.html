303. PRAISE HIM! PRAISE HIM! (S.S.208)

“I will sing praises unto my God.” (Ps.146:2)

FANNY J. CROSBY Doh is Ab CHESTER G. ALLEN

1) Manpâk êt ruh ni’n Tlensōtpa Jesu Christa!
Alungsêt na hla marit vâng sak ruh;
Mi’ntheng raja’num hla marit vâng anmanpâk,
Hrât leh lulên Ama ta rōk kaeh;
Kemông kapō Reng Jesûn a-en ṭhak me,
Abân hrât vâng nitin aṭhōi ṭhak me;
Manpâk êt ruh alûr-alên hri manthang ruh,
Amanpâk na hla kahōi ah sak ruh.

2) Manpâk êt ruh ni’n Tlensōtpa Jesu Christa!
Set intlen na cross chung ah jōng kathi;
Ni hringsōt na Ama kumsôt hlungpui kaeh,
Manpâk êt ruh cross ah kathi Jesu;
Set intlensōt Jesu kha manpâk ṭhak ruh,
Alungset hi heituk ah anlak mo!
Ni hringsōt na kumsôt hlungpui Jesu kaeh,
Manpâk êt ruh cross ah kathi Jesu.

3) Manpâk êt ruh ni’n Tlensōtpa Jesu Christa!
Mi’ntheng raja’n Hosanna! hla ansak;
Hûksōtpa Jesu! Kumkhō ah Reng chang rasoh,
Manpâk êt ruh Thempu leh Reng Jesu!
Kalûr tak ah Christa jōng inlâr nôk sik,
Hrât leh lulên Rōngpu ta rōk kaeh;
Hûksōtpa Jesu! Kumsôt ah Reng chang rasoh,
Manpâk êt ruh, Reng leh Rōngpu Rengpa!
