180. OH! WHY NOT TONIGHT? (A.Ch.H.397)

“Holding forth the word of life; that I may rejoice….” (Phil.2:16)

ELIZABETH REED Doh is EbJ. CALVIN BUISHEY

1) Hring na ṭong inṭhen pui mak roh,
Vâr kha hmit kathim de mak roh;
Mi kaset malung nger mak roh,
Tûn jân inhûk roh.

Tûn jân tak hin,
Inhûk mak tin mo?
Tûn jân tak hin,
Hring na nei jo roh.

2) Jing kani sōk mak um aja,
Na khō hmu kha hong katlôm thei;
Tûn tak pha aṭhat lai te hin,
Inhûk in hring roh.

3) Rōngpu’n lungset ah ala hngâk,
Lungset hi heisak mak tin mo?
Inrang ah na malung thleng roh,
Tûn jân inhûk roh.

4) Rōngpu akalô inhlut seng,
Amân tute kathler nei mak;
Ṭōn rakip ator sō reng jo,
Tûn jân inhûk roh.
