84. I HOPE TO MEET YOU ALL (R.S.952)

“God shall wipe away all tears from their eyes.” (Rev.21:4)

FANNY J. CROSBY Doh is AWILLIAM J. KIRKPATRICK

1) Lân hmu nôk sik me ti kamandon,
Ralei hring na abâng leh;
Thuhming marō ṭong lân thei sik me,
Khōvâr inpâk na ram ah.

Khōvâr na ram ah, khōvâr na ram ah,
Nipa omna ram inn kahōi ah khin;
Lân hmu tō nôk sik me, lân hmu tō nôk sik me,
Re torna lên lukhu ni hmu sik.

2) Lân hmu nôk sik me ti kamandon,
Hring na thingkûng daihlim ah;
Ni’n Tlensōtpa ni manpâk sik jo,
Eini alungset jârra.

3) Lân hmu nôk sik me ti kamandon,
Ni’n Tlensōtpa âng sûng ah;
Lungset mankase ni hmu jârra,
Amanpâk na hla sak rih.

4) Lân hmu nôk sik me ti kamandon,
Ralei ṭōn abâng phât leh;
Nikut ni kabên sik, inpâk ah,
Kumsôt ah hōina ram ah.
