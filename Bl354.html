354. I AM COMING TO THE CROSS (S.S.477)

“Him that cometh to Me I will, in no wise, cast out.” (Jn.6:37)

W. MCDONALD Doh is G [SAME TUNE: 109] W.G. FISCHER

1) Cross kûng ting kei kahong eng,
Mi sara, mi’nreng, mi’nvai;
Kei mi kaset chunghnung eng,
Kumsôt hring na kanei jo.

Rōngpu! Nang kataksen che,
Calvary ah Kemôngte!
Cross kûng ah kei inkûn eng,
Ei lungset roh, ei hûk roh.

2) Set sûng ah lei kaom eng,
Malung in alei jong che;
Innêm tak ah abêk eng,
Mantheng che rong, ati eng.

3) Ralei, ṭimre, nei leh hrât,
Akheng ah kapêk sōt che;
Karathla leh kataksa,
Kumsôt ah Nata kaeh.

4) Jesu! Kên kahong jōn che,
Karathla ei hûksōt roh;
Karathla in kum khōah,
Manpâk che rong Kemôngte!
