333. LET THE BLESSED SAVIOUR IN (R.S.188)

“...if any man hear… and open…I will come in…sup with him….” (Rev.3:20)

L.H.P. Doh is Eb L.H. PARTHEMORE

1) Tlensōtpa kot ah kangir, manhlut roh,
Manhlut roh, manhlut roh;
Kot araki na kha lei kasôt jo,
Manhlut roh, manhlut roh.

Tlensōtpa kot kûng ah kangir ṭhak,
Phat kasôt tak alei hngâk jo che;
Naset malung mantheng roh,
Na Tlensōtpa om nasik, mantluk roh, manhlut roh.

2) Ṭhōn kadik leh ṭhōn kaṭha tak kaeh,
Manhlut roh, manhlut roh;
Amân nang anthôpui ṭhak sik jo che,
Manhlut roh, manhlut roh.

3) Ama kha hnung manle nôk mak jo roh,
Manhlut roh, manhlut roh;
Ama ajōnle mak lang kabei jo,
Manhlut roh, manhlut roh.

4) Innêm tak ah abêk che rangei roh,
Manhlut roh, manhlut roh;
Nânpâk nasik Ama inhlâng jo roh,
Manhlut roh, manhlut roh.
