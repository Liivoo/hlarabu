218. NOTHING BUT THE BLOOD OF JESUS (S.S.874)

“Without shedding of blood there is no remission.” (Heb.9:22)

R.L. Doh is G ROBERT LOWRY

1) Kei intheng na thei heimo?
Jesu thisen rōk kha kaeh;
Tûn mo kei amanhring tor?
Jesu thisen rōk kha kaeh.

Akasōk thisen khan,
Kei kavur aman ang;
Kên alak nei mak eng,
Jesu thisen rōk kha kaeh.

2) Kei setna intheng tor chu,
Jesu thisen rōk kha kaeh;
Kei hringsōt thei nasik chu,
Jesu thisen rōk kha kaeh.

3) Set kasôp intheng thei chu,
Jesu thisen rōk kha kaeh;
Kên heite’m tlo tor mak eng,
Jesu thisen rōk kha kaeh.

4) Hei rakip kei inhôr na,
Jesu thisen rōk kha kaeh;
Kei ka hringsōt thei nasik,
Jesu thisen rōk kha kaeh.
