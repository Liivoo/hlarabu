331. I SHALL KNOW HIM (S.S.967)

“…then shall I know even as also I am known.” (1 Cor.13:12)

FANNY J. CROSBY Doh is Ab JOHN R. SWENEY

1) Ka hringkhō ṭōn anchai pha thina tui re kake leh,
Jing khōvâr kahōi tak kha kahmu sik jo;
Tui rekhat katlung tak leh kan Tlenpa hmai kahmu sik,
Katlai seng ah kei alei ratō sik eng.

Kamanhret sik, kamanhret sik,
Ahmai hmu tak ah kangir pha leh;
Kamanhret sik, kamanhret sik,
Akut thir inhêm na kahmu sik.

2) Ahmaisō tlai kahmu pha karathla inpâk sik jo,
Lungset hmit en ah kei ani lei en sik;
Heituk ah anpâk sik eng mo hingtuk lungset nahi!
Kaom nasik Chungkhōpui alei syem pêk.

3) Ni rangei ṭimre hên an omna anibêk ṭhak eng,
Ni’nṭhen na tuipang kha kên kamandon sōt;
Eden rahōn ah hla vâng kei ani jōng ratō sik,
Hma tak Rōngpu hmai hmu sik kâr kahngâk hla.

4) Pōn marit invonna khōpui kot kha katlung tak leh,
Hmit rathli sōk nôk mak na aṭhōi sik jo;
Inpâk ah Ngeva hei leh kênnum hla kasak sik jo,
Hma tak Rōngpu hmai hmu sik kha kanpâk na.
