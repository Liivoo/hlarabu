75. FANCY GOD’S LOVE

“God so loved the world that He gave His only begotten Son….” (Jn.3:16)

1) Mandon ruh kasopui hei! Nipu’n alungset na,
Niset intheng nasik ah, ni malung mandon rih;
Ni malung kadik mak hi, aset na heipên mo?
Niset rôn inna se rih, Amân amantheng sik.

2) Kên Christa kataksen jo, ahlam karahlâng jo,
Kamalung ah setna kha, tûn kamalung inreng;
Tui lōng kapōah hnet lo, mi’nreng mi’nvai kei eng,
Hrât leh damna neilo kei, khôn mo hōi kan hmu sik.

3) Joseph in akalōng kha akatōm pōnbang vâng,
Ava hre jo hlung thlân ah, ama ho’n le nôk jo;
En ruh! Jesu kathōi jo, marvân mi jōng kaṭum,
A-om na ara-âng sō, alênna aphông jo.

4) Tûn kaphâm namalung ah nala taksen mak mo?
Ahnōi ah inkûn inna alungset na der roh;
Chunghrep ah Jesu chu kên heisak lo tlo mak ning,
Kathi sō leh Ama hneng, kei kahlut thei nasik.
