401. RŌNGPU LUNGDO (M.A.K.4)

“…he that doeth the will of My Father…shall enter into the Kingdom….” (Matt.7:21)

M. ATON KOM Doh is D 03/03/1994

1) Ralei chung hi malung tluk mak na,
Inṭhen na ram kha kaeh;
Tâkkha Rōngpu ram syem kahōi tak khan,
Eini tlensōt saseng kalêng sik me; Rōngpu’n abêk na me rangei jo rih.

Rōngpu lungdo ah om rih,
Pha chaiting jo kha kaeh;
Se rih chen kanei sik ah,
Rōngpu hnōm pêk nasik ṭōn phut jo rih.

2) Marvân Rengram hlut thei nasik,
Omchen marō hei dahlon rih;
Taksa hōina khan thina aṭhōi sik,
Christa kataksen mi rakip hei kha;
Kumsôt mi vâng kaṭha kachang sik hei.

3) Ralei oi leh madu na hin,
Phat kasôt tak om mak jo rih;
Môngna, damna Christa hneng injong rih,
Rōngpu ṭōn ah inpê inhlut mi hei;
Mi kasing leh mi kahōi hei kaeh.
