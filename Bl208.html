208. SOLDIER OF THE KING (S.S.684)

“…endure hardness, as a good soldier of Jesus Christ.” (II Tim.2:3)

D.W. WHITTLE. Doh is G GEORGE C. STEBBINS

1) Pathen sepei eini, thisen vâng akatlen,
Reng Christa inthopui sik ah sepei ah insyem rih;
Ṭit lo jên ah eini’n inpâk na hla sak rih,
Sepei eini Christa Rōngpu’n ni malung amantlai.

Pathen sepei eini,
Amanpâk hla sak rih;
Hlam rin tak ah aṭōn tlo rih,
Ni Reng Christa sik ah.

2) Pathen sepei eini, amanpâk hla sak rih,
Ni Reng Christa kha thingrake chung ah anlei thâ jo;
Arahming invon rih, kha ni mahamṭhat na,
Rōngpu Jesu sepei minchu, Ama kha kaja rih.

3) Pathen sepei eini, Ama leh inlâr rih,
Tûn anreng ni de pui inchu apōnlek kadom rih;
Lên lukhu hmu nasik kani kha kahnai jo,
Christa sepei eini seng in ni Reng khi en ṭhak rih.
