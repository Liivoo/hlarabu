225. ENDEAVOURER’S MARCHING SONG (S.S.1100)

“Speak…that they go forward.” (Exo.14:15)

FANNY J. CROSBY Doh is Ab IRA D. SANKEY

1) Christian remi hei! Bêkna rangei ruh,
Tumo ṭongpêk rasa kajui sik?
Re hlam kakei Christa kha jui inna,
Eini Endeavour in nitor sik.

Nam ruh remi hei, bêkna rangei ruh,
Hma ting nam ruh, pho leh chem thô ruh;
Pōnlek kalek ruh, avârna sûng ah,
Re hmun ting katlai seng ah nam rih.

2) Ahnai ahlat ah pêngkun rangei ruh,
Jehovah rahming vâng nitor sik;
Aram tep pêk me akut kahrât vâng,
Amân eini aṭhōi mathlung sik.

3) Remi kahrâng hei! Hmating nam êt ruh,
Pha thleng hma kahrât tak ah de ruh;
Nanrup-nanrup ah ông ruh, re hmun ah,
Tor nasik ah eini’n tak tlo rih.

4) Ni ṭōn tor sō ah ni re tor sō leh,
Chung ting Jesu leh intō sik me;
Ahme nihmu leh inpâk êt sik me,
Kum khō alungset inn ni hlo sik.
