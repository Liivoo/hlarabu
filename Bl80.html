80. TELL ME MORE ABOUT JESUS (S.S.45)

“…I will tell thee what the Lord hath said to me….” (1 Sam.15:16)

PHILIP P. BLISS Doh is EbJAMES MCGRANAHAN

1) Chung leh hnōi ah manhret kaeh,
Kei sik ah ṭhat leh dik jârra;
Thuming marō kha kather ṭhak,
Jesu nunchen kha hri roh.

Jesu nunchen kha hri roh,
Jesu nunchen kha hri roh;
Kalungset Pa manhret nasik,
Jesu nunchen kha hri roh.

2) Ralei reipâr chu kachi rih,
Inreng na kasûm kajing ṭhak;
Hringkhō marit tak kachi rih,
Jesu nunchen kha hri roh.

3) Taksen mak nân ator pha leh,
Rôn kahrik inreng kachang pha;
Kânṭangpui sik ajōng pêk roh,
Jesu nunchen kha hri roh.

4) Ram kalûr kha kahmu pha leh,
Na hmun syemsa kachen pha leh;
Kha kakumsôt hla chang rasoh,
Jesu nunchen kha hri roh.
