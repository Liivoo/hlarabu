203. THE HANDWRITING ON THE WALL (S.S.432)

“…the king saw the part of the hand that wrote.” (Dan.5:5)

KNOWLES SHAW. Doh is F IRA D. SANKEY

1) Kaldia reng Belshazar leh aram rongpu hei seng kha,
Revei na inn ah sana thleng vâng ju anlei inlai khan;
Ajân ah anhōi lai tak rengpa inn kalûr sûng ah,
An malung ṭit inper kasip kha bang ah kut kha kaeh.

Bang ah kha Pathen kut kaeh,
Bang ah kha Pathen kut kaeh;
Ṭong kadik ralei chung ah khan taksen na nanei sik mo?
Kut khan bang ah asûn lai seng khan.

2) En ruh! Sōk kachang Daniel mipui hma khan kangir ah,
Reng inlensak kha alen na ṭit lo hêr ah amanse;
Ṭong insûn kha atei ah mi hingja gik-na kaeh,
Rengpa tûnchu na reng vairôn na hi kabâng jo ati.

3) En ruh! Taksen leh hrât na ṭong kadik phôngsōt nasik,
Rathla Intheng in apêk kha amalung hrât na kaeh;
Judea ram ah a-inna asōk chang na hmun ah khan,
Ṭong inlak hingja amanhret, bang ah Pathen kut sûn khâm.

4) Hing ah hin eini set nâm akasûn khat kaom eh!
Set kanei he’n nan set malung Rōngpu hneng pe sōt jo ruh;
Pha kahnai kathleng sō jo, phōng leh châng rakip sik ah,
Mi kaset hei set tlo na kha bang ah insûn ka ang sik.
