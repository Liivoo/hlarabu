334. FEAR NOT! (S.S.811)

“I am thy Shield and thy exceeding great reward.” (Gen.15:1)

E.G. TAYLOR Doh is A GEORGE C. STEBBINS

1) Pathen in athlop che,
Ṭit na neijên mak roh;
Rōngpu’n anṭangpui che,
Re kha nator nger sik.

Pathen ṭongpêk kaeh,
Ṭit na nei hêr mak ruh;
Rōngpu Jesu jârra,
Ka ngam tak ah om ruh.

2) Inreng, ṭa rasa kha,
Pathen in athei ṭhak;
Na ṭajai sui na kha,
Ṭhat na apêk sik che.

3) Na omna hmun tin ah,
Nân ṭit na nei mak roh;
Amân abôm sik che,
Hrât na apêk sik che.

4) Rōngpu kajōng bei sik,
Rup katlôm hên en ruh!
Alûrna hei seng vâng,
Eini ajōng ṭhōi sik.
