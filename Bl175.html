175. NEVER SAY GOOD BYE (S.S.974)

“…building of God…not made with hands, eternal in the heavens.” (II Cor.5:1)

FANNY J. CROSBY Doh is GIRA D. SANKEY

1) Inpâk na inn ah intûp hei,
Inṭhen na om mak ning,
Û leh nai ah kaom sōm hei,
Inṭhen na om mak ning.

Inṭhen na om mak ning,
Inṭhen na om mak ning;
Marvân ram kahōi ni tlung leh,
Inṭhen na om mak ning.

2) Inreng invai ni dahlon leh,
Inṭhen na om mak ning;
Tûn ṭa pumma ni’nṭhen innum,
Lân hmu tō nôk sik me.

3) Ê-na, hōina ram ni tlung leh,
Inṭhen na om mak ning;
Christa âng ah kaom sik me,
Inṭhen na om mak ning.
