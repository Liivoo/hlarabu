162. THOU THINKEST, LORD OF ME (S.S.566)

“…I will remember…and establish…an everlasting covenant.” (Ezek.16:60)

E.D. MUND Doh is DE.S. LORENZ

1) Malung in-nôk um ka ratō,
Khōdeng thei ah kaom innum;
Ka mandon ah inpâk jo eng,
Rōngpu mandon jârra.

Nang in na mandon eng,
Nang in na mandon eng;
Nahneng ah heimo kaṭit sik,
Nang in na mandon eng.

2) Hring lai nunchen kajôm lai hin,
Ka rathla ṭhōisōt jo innum;
Malung ’reng ah kaom lai um,
Rōngpu’n na mandon eng.

3) Khōdeng theina atlung jônnum,
Malung ’reng ah kaom innum;
Ka mandon ah inpâk sik eng,
Rōngpu’n na mandon eng.
