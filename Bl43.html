43. BRINGING IN THE SHEAVES (S.S.757)

“Bringing his sheaves with him.” (Ps.126:6)

KNOWLES SHAWDoh is CGEORGE A. MINER

1) Lungset na hmuchi kha jingkâr leh sûn ṭim ah,
Jingkâr radai kai lai hinnum kavôr ah;
A-ât pha tluk sik leh akhôm pha kahngâk hla,
Inpâk ah karôn sik ka hmuchi vôr kha.

Karôn manhlut sik kasachi vôr kha,
Malung inpâk tak ah karôn manhlut sik;
Karôn manhlut sik kasachi vôr kha,
Malung inpâk tak ah karôn manhlut sik.

2) Kani sat âm kavôr, khō inthim âm kavôr,
Phebi dai leh khōsûr sik ṭit lo jên ah;
Akhôm pha atluk ah kaṭōn kabâng pha leh,
Malung inpâk tak ah karôn manhlut sik.

3) He ruh Rōngpu sik ah, se rih niṭa pumma,
Ni khōdeng thei mahngi inna tlo êt rih;
Ni’nreng nibâng pha leh alei ratō sik me,
Malung inpâk tak ah karôn manhlut sik.
