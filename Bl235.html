235. WILL YOUR ANCHOR HOLD? (S.S.879)

“An anchor of the soul both sure and steadfast.” (Heb.6:19)

PRISCILLA J. OWENS Doh is F WILLIAM J. KIRKPATRICK

1) Hringkhō thlipui-khōpui in-nông lai hin,
Na rathla thirchak-ai kha kahnet nâk mo?
Tuipui in-nông in atlen kalâng hi,
Na thirchak-ai kha akahnet nâk mo?

Rathla thirchak-ai eini’n ninei,
Thlipui-khōpui kârra ni’nsik sik;
Hlungpui kahnet tak ah inkhit roh,
Tlensōtpa lungset inri tak sûng ah.

2) Thina hlampui kalên ni hro lai hin,
Manset nasik in eini ahngâk me pha;
Thlipui-khōpui leh tuipui an-nông pha,
Rathla thirchak-ai kha nanei nâk mo?

3) Thina tuipui lōng ahong khang pha leh,
Anchai na kaphuk kha ahong dai pha leh;
Thlipui in-nông kha ahôn hnak innum,
Hring na inn ah thirchak-ai manhnet roh.

4) Ṭhum hmun kavâr Sana khōpui kalûr,
Jingkhō ahong vâr leh kha nahmu sik jo;
Hringkhō thlipui in-nông anchai sō leh,
Na rathla thirchak-ai kahnet sik mo?
