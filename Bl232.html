232. OVER JORDAN (R.S.392)

“…the Lord went before them…in a pillar of a cloud…and fire….” (Exo.13:21)

MARY B.C. SLADE Doh is Eb JAMES R. MURRAY

1) Lungset leh marit tak ah Hûksōtpân aṭhōi sik mo?
Hme leh tlâng, dâm, phai kahōi Jordan re ah;
Eini châm inhnga sik me tuipang kalûr ni tlung leh,
Inpâk ah ratō na hmun Jordan re ah.

Jordan re ah, Jordan re ah,
Eini châm inhnga sik me tuipang kalûr ni tlung leh;
Jordan re ah, Jordan re ah,
Hōina khan aratō sik Jordan re ah.

2) Ratu inthûk hlung kârra ni hro sik ram katlung ah,
Aṭhōi manhlut nger sik me Jordan re ah;
En ruh! Jân ah hmei hlom vâng, sûn leh kasûm vâng nihma,
Aṭhōi mathlung nger sik me Jordan re ah.

3) Ator na rathla hrât vâng Hûksōtpân aṭhōi sik me,
Sehne, khoihning lông na ram Jordan re ah;
Khan thei kûng rakip inra tuire kapût kapōah,
Ṭâm leh thi om mak jo ning Jordan re ah.

4) Abêkna ram katlung ah Hûksōtpân aṭhōi sik mo?
Canaan ram ni tlung hma seng Jordan re ah;
Na âng sûng ah om nasik ei ṭhōi roh, ei hûksōt roh,
Inṭhen tik om mak jo ning marvân ram ah.
