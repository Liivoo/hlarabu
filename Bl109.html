109. WEDDING HYMN (S.S.477)

“… shall a man…cleave unto his wife: and…be one flesh.” (Gen.2:24)

SIDNEY LANIER Doh is GWILLIAM G. FISCHER

1) Eden ah khan Pathen in,
Alei syem me nu leh pa;
Sanghram khat amanchang hei,
Vâng rakip alei pêk hei.

2) Pathen in Adam leh Eve,
Amanchun hei tak khat ah;
Kha hei kha marit tak ah,
Inhlō len phut na kaeh.

3) Nunchen marō kajôm ah,
Kaom hei hi hei sanghram;
Nu leh pa andahlôn ah,
Inṭhen nôk mak nasik ah.

4) Inlungset ṭhak hei sonna,
Tûn inn hlo jo hei rasoh;
Tak khat leh ratha khat ah,
Lung ri khat rôn hei rasoh.

5) An hringkhōsei bâng hma seng,
Pathen kaja hei sonna;
Ralei an dahlôn pha leh,
Hlam khat hro sap hei rasoh.
