357. TO GOD BE THE GLORY (S.S.23)

“Give unto the Lord the glory due unto His name.” (Ps.29:2)

FANNY J. CROSBY Doh is Ab WILLIAM H. DOANE

1) Pathen lûr ṭhak rasoh ṭōnpui atlo sōt,
Vânhnōi alungset jârra Asa apêk;
Tu ênnum set intheng nasik anpe sōt,
Hlut thei nasik marvân kot amahlang jo.

Manpâk ruh! Manpak ruh!
Vânhnōi mi’n thei rasoh;
Ni Rōngpu manpâk ruh,
Mipui tlai êt rasoh;
Rōngpu Jesu rahming ah,
Pa hneng hong ruh;
Akut syem kalên chung ah,
Lûrna pe ruh.

2) Thisen vâng akatlen kumsôt hring na kha,
Pathen kataksen antep rakip hei sik;
Ṭhat mak na’nphut anhrung sōt takkasen kha,
Jesu ânphut rangei mandam achen jo.

3) Ṭōnpui amanthei jo, akut syem kalên,
Jesu ânphut inpâk na kalên Chunghnung!
Ântheng, ansâng leh kalên Chunghnung sik ah,
Ni’n lârna hmun tin ah khan Jesu nihmu.
