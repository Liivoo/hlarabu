244. ONWARD, EVER ONWARD (R.S.905)

“…endure hardness, as a good soldier of Jesus Christ.” (II Tim.2:3)

MARIAN W. HUBBARD Doh is Eb CHARLES H. GABRIEL

1) Christa’n amadu mi’ntheng remi kahrât,
Re hmun hma ting nam roh, nang sik hmun kaom;
Se roh, de ruh ralei chung setna re hi,
Marvân khōpui ah inpâk man nihmu sik.

Hma ting nam ruh cross remi,
Ṭit lo jên ah taksen êt ruh;
Hma ting nam ruh cross remi,
Rōngpu ṭong kha taksen û-na eini nitor sik hei.

2) Seitân in nimalung hi asuk manbōi,
Tâk Rōngpu lungset na jârra nitor sik;
Hma lei kakei Christa remi rakip khâm,
Christa lungset Chungkhōpui kha anchen sik.

3) Eini’n rathla kahrok ah hma ting nam rih,
Der ṭhak leh inṭhōi ah Jesu en ṭhak ruh;
Christa Rōngpu’n hei rakip amantor sik,
Hosanna! Ȏn ruh, Seitân katlân sik hei.
