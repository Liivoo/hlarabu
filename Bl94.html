94. WHEN THE ROLL IS CALLED UP (S.S.983)

“For the trumpet shall sound.” (1 Cor.15:52)

J.M.B. Doh is AbJAMES M. BLACK

1) Pathen bêkna pengkûn anhring tak leh ralei kabōi sik,
Kum leikōah hring na kani kavâr sik jo;
Amalung katōk seng hei tuipang ah amantûp sik jo,
Rahming teina kârra kêm katla sik eng.

Khava rahming teina kârra,
Khava rahming teina kârra;
Khava rahming teina kârra,
Rahming teina kârra kêm katla sik eng.

2) Khava jing kahōi tak ah Christa kathi kathô sik hei,
Anthōi ning lenna anchang ning jo khanchu;
Amarvân chung ah akadei sōt seng intûp sik jo hei,
Rahming teina kârra kêm katla sik eng.

3) Ka hringkhō abâng kâr seng ah kên Pathen ṭōn katlo sik,
Alungset ṭong marit kha kahri ṭhak sik;
Khanna ka hringkhō atluk ah ka ṭōntlo kachai tak leh,
Rahming teina kârra kêm katla sik eng.
