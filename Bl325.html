325. JESUS SHALL REIGN (S.S.1084)

“He shall have dominion from sea…river unto the ends of the earth.” (Ps.72:8)

ISAAC WATTS Doh is E HATTON

1) Hmun rakip ah Jesûn vai arôn,
Ke kûng, ke lêr ahlō sō sik;
A Reng ram vai kha kalên sik,
Are-re ah kaom sik jo.

2) Phung rakip leh ṭong rakip in,
Lungset hla kahōi ansak sik;
An karesō innêm nêng ah,
Alungset na ṭong an ôn sik.

3) Ahneng ah derna bâng mak ning,
Manpâk na lên lukhu ankhu;
Arahming kahōi inthang sik,
Kamuk ah kaom ṭhak sik jo.

4) Ralei chung mihring rakip in,
Reng kha kajana anpêk sik;
Marvân mi’n hla vâng anmanpâk,
Ralei mintlung rasoh, anti.
