365. MY MOTHER’S PRAYER (3rd ALEX.116)

“My son…forsake not the law of thy mother.” (Prov.6:20)

JUDSON W.VAN DEVENTER Doh is Db WINFIELD S. WEEDEN

1) Kanu marit! Mahngi mak ning,
Amansena kare innêm;
Kasa nani dahlon jônnum,
Kader pêk na che mandon roh.

Kanu marit kamandon ning,
Kahneng ah kaom mo kati;
Tlaitlâk thli hrâng rasa kathei,
Kanu der rasa mo kati.

2) Kanu marit! Mahngi mak ning,
Heitik hei lai ahla jônnum;
Kanu der pêk na kare kha,
Kamalung sûng ah kaom ṭhak.

3) Heitik chennum mahngi mak ning,
Kanu’n ader pêk na eng kha;
Mârkhup thlêp ah aṭong jai sui,
Kamalung hmit in ahmu ṭhak.

4) Sensōt Pân kei alungset na,
Mahngi mak ning aheitik âm;
Kanu derna arahlâng pêk,
Kaset arangei mandam jo.

Hûksōtpa Nang! Kamanpâk che!
Marvân mi’ntheng hei âng sûng ah;
Nu marit leh inhmu nasik,
Hōi ei mathlung pêk roh Rōngpu!
