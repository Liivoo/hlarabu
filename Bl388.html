388. CHȎI MANSÂNG (M.C.K.13)

“Jesus Christ the same yesterday…today and for ever.” (Heb.13:8)

M. CHUNGRENGKHUP KOM Doh is G 24/11/2016

1) Pha leh châng inthleng rih, mihring hringkhō’m kathleng rih,
Set inthim in amanbōi rih jo;
Alungset inlak khan re kator kachang jo me,
Manpâk na dâr manhring jo rih.

Anthleng tik neilo ni Rōngpu Pathen!
Alungset hingtuk hrep kalên;
Golden Jubilee kani ning hin,
Chôi mansâng na hla kahōi kansak.

2) Bêkna kare kathei, Christa tabêk sōt mi hei,
Damlai kani, pha om lai te hin;
Christa rahming ah malung khat leh ratha khat ah,
Pôn katho tak ah inṭang rih.

3) Vânhnōi hmun rakip ah hûkmanhring na ṭong kahōi,
Hri manthang sik, eini ṭōn kaeh;
Ratha sêt lo jên ah, aṭongpêk kha jui pum ah,
Ratha karôk ah hma hnam rih.
