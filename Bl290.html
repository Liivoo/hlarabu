290. CHRIST! THE TRUE PILOT!

“…in the days of Noah…the ark… wherein…eight souls were saved.” (I Pet.3:20)

Doh is G

1) Hring na rakōng nikhat la’ntor sik,
Reng Christa kha arakōng kator;
Rakōng ah phung rakip kakip sik,
Marvân chung kahōi ah aṭhōi sik.

Ram kahōi ah se rih, ram kahōi ah se rih,
Rakōng ah hong hlut ruh û leh nai;
Ram kahōi ah se rih, ram kahōi ah se rih,
Rakōng ah hong hlut ruh û leh nai.

2) Jesûn marit tak ah abêk che,
Rakōng ah hlut ruh michôn kase;
Zion kajôn rakōng lak om mak,
Pha hom nasik hlut ruh rakōng ah.

3) Re kena rakōng ase jônchu,
Ho’nle nôk mak ning nabêk innum;
Zion ram kahōi katlung sik jo,
Kaṭap sik jo che nang tuipang ah.

4) Jesûn lungset tak ah ahngâk che,
Inrang ruh rakōng ah hlut nasik;
Hring na rakōng alak om mak jo,
Insûm mak roh rakōng lap mak tin.
