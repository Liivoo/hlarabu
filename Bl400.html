400. KEI AH OM ṬHAK RUH (M.A.K.02)

“Abide in Me, and I in you…and…bringeth forth much fruit….” (Jn.15:4-5)

M. ATON KOM Doh is Bb 09/01/2015

1) Pa’n Asa alungset kapō hrep ah,
Rōngpu’num Ama kaṭit alungset;
Rōngpu Jesu ṭongpêk kajui mihei chung ah khan,
Alungset marit na leh aṭhat na kha kum leikō kaeh.

Kei ah om ṭhak û-na, Ke’m nanhneng om ṭhak rong,
Karâ hingja nannei sik, Rōngpu’n ahri pêk me;
Rōngpu sik ah se rih, ratha set lo jên ah,
Re torna lên lukhu marit kha nichen sik jo.

2) Ni hringkhō dam sung ah, pha om lai hin,
Ṭōn kaṭha tlo nasik ah intluk rih;
Ṭhat mak ṭōn tlo na jârra bângtik nei loah khōdeng,
Theina leh ha karôt na thina kha kum leikō kaeh.

3) Khat leh khat inlungset na ṭōn tlo rih,
Christa’n eini hneng ah ṭong apêk me;
Pathen ni lungset sik leh mihring ni’nlungset jônchu,
Ralei chung ah marvân Rengram kha kum leikō nichen sik.
