111. THERE IS LIFE FOR A LOOK (S.S.123)

“Look unto me, and be ye saved, all the ends of the earth….” (Isai.45:22)

A.M. HULL Doh is GE.G. TAYLOR

1) Mi inkhei kha na en inchu hring na kaom,
Tûn kapha hring na kha la kaom;
Cross thing lêr ah mi inkheipa kha en ruh!
Set kanei hei katlen ah om ruh.

Hring nasik en ruh!
Mi inkhei kha nân en inchu hring na kaom;
Tûn kapha hring na kha la kaom.

2) Heisamo Jesu set karôn ah kaom hi?
Nang sik ade achang mak inchu;
Heisamo intheng na thi kha kasōk sik?
Na bat kachôi achang mak inchu.

3) Pathen bêkna che hi taksen mak tlo mak roh,
Ṭit sik hei lak om nôk mak jo ning;
Vânhnōi ralei inhmang ning jôn lâr nôk sik,
Aṭōn tlo seng ator sō sik jo.
