262. STANDING ON THE PROMISES (S.S.877)

“All the promises of God in Him are yea and …Amen!” (II Cor.1:20)

R.K.C. Doh is Bb RUSSELL K. CARTER

1) Rōngpu Christa jârra ṭongpêk kha jui ruh,
Hring na ṭong kha Ama manpâk nasik ah;
Rathla kahrok tak ah manpâk êt jo rih.
Rōngpu ṭongpêk dung jui ah ngir ruh.

Ngir rih! Ngir rih!
Rōngpu Jesu ah ngir ruh a ṭongtep jui ruh;
Ngir ruh! Ngir ruh!
Rōngpu ṭongtep ah khan kei kangir eng.

2) Dân ṭong jui kha heitik chen tlasam mak ning,
Tlâksam na leh ṭit na Amân anihûk;
Anhmang thei mak sik ṭong kha Ama kaeh,
Rōngpu ṭongpêk dung jui ah ngir ruh.

3) Aṭongpêk jui sik kha kei tlasam mak ning,
Kei sik ah athisen kha alōng jârra;
Are-ah Christa kha kamanpâk sik jo,
Rōngpu ṭongpêk dung jui ah ngir ruh.
