62. WHAT DID HE DO? (ALEX.77)

“…Christ Jesus came into the world to save sinners….” (1 Tim.1:15)

JAMES M. GRAYDoh is DbWILLIAM OWEN

1) Calvary tlâng ah lungset na,
Khangther nôk âm lân hri hi sik;
Calvary tlâng ah lungset na,
Khangther nôk âm lân hri hi sik.

Kumsôt ah in hri ṭhak sik jo,
Manpâk êt rih, manpâk êt rih, manpâk êt rih;
Sopui hei! Manpâk êt rih, manpâk êt rih.

2) Marvân ah hla lak kanêk âm,
Akahōi Chunghnung kha kaeh;
Marvân ah hla lak kanêk âm,
Akahōi Chunghnung kha kaeh.

3) Kemôngte thisen intheng na,
Jōng kalông setna kârra hin;
Seraphim kare hei nêk âm,
Akahōi Chunghnungpên kaeh.

4) Pi leh pu hei marvân chung ah,
Manpâk na hla nila sak sik;
A inn ther kahōi sûng ah khin,
Lântup êt sik me nikheng ah.

5) Jesu ’ni jârra jōng kathi,
Alungset na hri tor lo jên;
Akathei mak hei sik ah chu,
Lânlâr nôk sik ni’n Tlensōtpa!
