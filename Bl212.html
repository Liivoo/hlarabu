212. HE LIVES ON HIGH! (M.H.420)

“He blessed…parted from them, and carried up into heaven.” (Lk.24:51)

B.B. MCK. Doh is Bb B.B. McKINNEY

1) Mi kaset hei kahûk inhring sik ah,
Ralei chung ah hin Jesu kajōng;
Amân rahling lukhu ajōn khu ah,
Calvary ah kei sik ajōng kathi.

Kathōi ah marvân kake jo,
Amân ka setna amantheng sō jo;
Kathōi ah marvân kake jo,
La kajōng nger sik vōikhat.

2) Jesu marvân ah kake nôk sik ah,
Thlân sûng ah inphut ah kathōi sōt;
Vōikhat Jesu la kajōng nôk nger sik,
Aṭong kajui hei seng aṭhōi sik hei.

3) Nan setna rakip phông ruh Jesu hneng,
Hring na apêk sik mankase ah;
Jesu ṭong kataksen mi rakip in,
Kumsôt ah inpâk na annei sik jo.

4) Jesu hneng hong ruh rôn kahrik rakip,
Ama hneng hōina kakip sō jo;
Tu ênnum Jesu hring kataksen seng,
Kumsôt ah châm hngat na annei sik jo.
