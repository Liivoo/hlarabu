393. NU MARIT! (M.C.K.31)

“Who can find a virtuous woman? For her price is far above rubies.” (Prov.31:10)

M. CHUNGRENGKHUP KOM Doh is F 11/05/2018

1) Hlung mankatam hei kanêk âm aman katam pên,
Tekâk pui thei kaom nâk sik mo kanu teilo?
Lungset tak ah narôn eng ah, marit ah nahrung eng,
Heituk ah ’lungdai kaom jo mo NU kaṭha nei hi!

Rōngpu kaṭit NU kaṭha chu manpâk sik arahōi,
Ator chen ah aṭōn tlo karâ kha ahmu ṭhak sik;
Pha rakip ah lungset kathem, nun kahōi nachang nahi,
Aheitik ah kan ôi sik jo che oh! kan NU MARIT!

2) Lungset leh marit tak ah na mansena rakip,
Mahngi hêr mak ning ralei hringkhō katlung bit ah;
Kalung ri ah invôn pum ah nitin kajui ṭhak sik,
Na manthei na eng hi keisik hmeivâr kapō kaeh.

3) Mahngi thei chang hêr mak ning nalungset inlak hi,
Na der pêk na eng, na inpêk sōt na rakip hi;
Nei leh hao in, lûr leh len in na hmun lo tor mak ning,
Kan rangei manlûr che oh! Kan Nu!
Vâng ṭhat chen ṭhak roh.
