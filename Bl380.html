380. OH! CHRISTMAS KANI (M.C.K.24)

“...For He shall save His people from their sins.” (Matt.1:21)

M. CHUNGRENGKHUP KOM Doh is D 23/11/2017

1) Rōngpu Ngeva Gabriel ṭong hri,
Nuhngâk intheng Mary chung ah;
Rathla Intheng inhnga jo,
Naidông Jesu ralei chung ah.

Jōn jōr jo marim rih oh!
Set anphut ani hûk sik;
Manlûr jo rih ralei chung ah,
Inpâk na kani lunpui;
Oh! Christmas Kani!

2) Oh! Bethlehem! David Khōpui,
Mahâm kaṭha kachang jo che;
Kalûr jo che ralei ah,
Hla marit vâng kan ôi sam che.

3) Ṭong kahōi inpâk na kalen,
Kemông kakhêm hei chung ah khan;
Katlung jo anvâng kaṭha,
Inthang jo chei ralei chung ah.
