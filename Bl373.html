373. SILENT NIGHT! (C.H.B.234)

“And they… found Mary…Joseph, and the babe lying in a manger.” (Lk.2:16)

JOSEPH MOHR Doh is Bb FRANZ GRUBER

1) Jân inthim dai nêng ah hei rakip kadai rip,
Ka mông tak ah anu’n asa;
Naidông intheng, akabir a-en,
A-oiyah! A-oiyah! Ka mông ah amanje.

2) Jân inthim dai nêng ah anthim kha kavâr sō,
Kemông kakhêm he’n hla ansak ah;
Hallelujah! Reng kamuk ruh,
Christa Rōngpu tuning inte jo tiah anphông.

3) Jân inthim dai nêng ah Pathen Sa vâr intheng,
Ahmaisō anphut amanvâr ah;
Ahei kakho alungset vâr,
Rōngpu Jesu ’ntet ning, Rōngpu Jesu ’ntet ning.

4) Jân inthim dai nêng ah ârsi ’nlak hei kavâr,
Hla sak sōm rih Tirle hei leh;
Hallelujah! Ni Rengpa sik,
Ni’ntlensōtpa Christa Jesu tuning inte.




Translated By: (L) Rev. Sumkholen Serto,
Former Executive Secretary, KRBCA, Manipur.
