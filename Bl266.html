266. CHRISTIAN! WALK CAREFULLY (S.S.617)

“…walk worthy of the vocation wherewith ye are called.” (Ephe.4:1)

G.C.S. Doh is G GEORGE C. STEBBINS

1) Christian inching thei ruh setna katam,
Nasena hlam ah ṭit na katam eh!
Manhret loah mathlêm nân ajui che,
Kum khōah setna da sik ajui che.

Christian inching thei ruh,
Christian inching thei ruh;
Christian inching thei ruh,
Setna katam.

2) Christian inching thei ruh ṭhat mak katam,
Marvân ânnim ah ânthim jo innum;
Pha bong khat sō leh hong kavâr sik jo,
Khan are-ah châm inhnga sik jo che.

3) Christian hei inder ruh tlu mak nasik,
Mahngi mak roh Rōngpu kha der ṭhak roh;
Thi nasik katlung ah ngir inhnet roh,
Derna revon phô ah invon ṭhak roh.

4) Christian inching ṭhak ruh innêm tak ah,
Khava ram na tlung tak leh Tlensōtpân;
Hong hlut roh na Rengpa inpâk na ah,
Tiah Abêk che, malung inpâk sik.
