360. IT CAME UPON THE MIDNIGHT CLEAR (M.H.11)

“Bethlehem…out of thee shall He come forth…to be Ruler….” (Micah.5:2)

EDMUND H. SEARS Doh is Ab R.S. WILLIS

1) Hla kalûr marō anjōng sak,
Jân ṭhangṭim adai lai;
Ngeva hên an Serângdâr leh,
Ralei ting jōn kûn hei;
Ralei chung ah cham lêng rasoh,
Mihring hei hneng ṭhat na;
Ngeva hei hla sak kahōi kha,
Vânhnōi in thei rasoh.

2) Marvân ânphut jōng kaṭum hei,
Anmathla anzâr ah;
Vânhnōi ah anreng rakip hneng,
An hla sak inthei ṭhak;
Malung inreng hei chung ah khan,
An mathla anzâr khum;
Rasa mahring inhnak tak ah,
Ngeva hên hla ansak.

3) Hringkhō rôn kahrik karôn hei,
Nan rôn kahrik rôn kha;
Hringkhō hlam kahro tor mak hei,
Nan hro tor nasik ah;
Inpâk nasik kha en ruh tûn,
Jajâm ah jōng inlâr;
Nan phur kahrik maṭhum û-na,
Ngeva hla rangei ruh.
