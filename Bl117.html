117. SOUND THE BATTLE CRY (S.S.703)

“Be…faithful unto death and I will give…a crown of life.” (Rev.2:10)

W.F.S Doh is BbWILLIAM F. SHERWIN

1) Hrât ruh re tuk na Rōngpu Christa sik,
Seitân re kasōk en ṭhak ruh;
Intluk ah om ruh khan ngir inhnet ruh,
Aṭong Intheng nanpho manchang ruh.

Ngir ruh remi! Tor nasik mandon ruh,
Se ruh remi! Aṭong rôn û-na;
Hma ting nam ruh Hosanna! ti û-na,
Jesu Christa’n ni re hlam akei.

2) Hrât ruh re katlo hma ting hnong û-na,
Ni re tor nasik ṭong kaom;
Re hi nitor leh remi hei seng khan,
Lên lukhu kalûr ninei sik jo.

3) Vânhnōi Pathen! Kan derna rangei roh,
Ei sen roh! Lungset marit vâng;
Re hi anchai leh remi rakip khan,
Hring na lên lukhu an rahlâng sik.
