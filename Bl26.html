26. GO AND WORK (S.S.748)

“…that My house may be filled.” (Lk.14:23)

FANNY J. CROSBY Doh is CIRA ALLAN SANKEY

1) Christian hei! Je mak jo ruh,
Alak hei anṭap lai hin;
Rōngpu'n ṭong apêk jo me,
Kahōi ah om mak jo rih.

Se û-na tûn ṭōn phut ruh,
Anhmang hei vaṭhōi hei ruh;
Set inthim ah kaom hei,
Revei na ṭhōi manhlut ruh.

2) Tlensōtpa ni lungset mo?
Aṭongpêk ni jui nâk mo?
Ninei hei ni phe nâk mo?
Abêkna ni thei nâk mo?

3) Ama ni taksen nâk mo?
Amanthei ni jui nâk mo?
Amata kachang me mo?
Ṭōn vâng ni taksen phông rih.

4) Cross kadom nger sik kaom,
Lên lukhu nitup inchu;
Ratha hrôk ah hma nam rih,
Pha kanai – kani katlôm.
