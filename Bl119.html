119. BE YE STRONG IN THE LORD (S.S.686)

“Be strong in the Lord.” (Ephe.6:10)

DANIEL W. WHITTLE Doh is GIRA D. SANKEY

1) Rōngpu ratha hrât leh aṭongpêk majôm ruh,
Ṭong kaṭha hi ram tin ah phông û-na;
Malung inreng indōi na tui kalōng a-ang innum,
Rōngpu rahming tunmo ande tor sik?

Kahnet tak ah ngir ruh,
Rōngpu ṭongpêk me majôm inna;
Eini Rōngpu sepei me aṭongpêk me kajôm ah,
Lên lukhu kalûr tak ni hmu sik.

2) Ama ratha hrât song inna hma ting jôn rih,
Re tlo rih hnung ting manle lo jên ah;
Rōngpu Jesûn ni re tlona a-om pui ṭhak sik me,
Hma ting jôn ruh Aratha hrât vâng khan.

3) Rōngpu ratha hrât leh aṭongpêk jôm û-na,
Vânhnōi ralei setna re nitor leh;
Bâng lo jên ah amanpâk na hla nisak sap sik jo,
Thi leh malung inreng om mak jo ning.
