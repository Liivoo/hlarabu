156. I AM RESOLVED (A.A.Ch.H.50)

“I press toward…the prize of the high calling of God….” (Phil.3:14)

PALMER HARTSOUGH Doh is BbJAMES H. FILLMORE

1) Ralei chung inpâk na leh hōina hi,
Kên rangei mak jo ning;
Akadik leh akaṭha tlo jo rong,
Tiah kangir jo eng.

Jesu ah inhnga jo eng,
Heituk kahōi mo?
Chunghnungpên Rōngpu Jesu!
Kahong jōn che.

2) Tlensōtpa Rōngpu hneng hlut nasik ah,
Set rakip kahlon jo;
Rōngpu Jesu kha akadik tak leh,
Akahring ṭhak kaeh.

3) Tlensōtpa kahnung kên kajui sik jo,
Akadik chang nasik;
Aṭongpêk eng dungjui ah om pumma,
Kên ka razen sik jo.

4) Amarvân Reng ram kahlut thei nasik,
Set tlo nôk mak jo ning;
Mi teitôm na leh enkada na eng,
Hnuk nat pui mak jo ning.
