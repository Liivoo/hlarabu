306. BLEST BE THE TIE THAT BINDS (S.S.506)

“And above all things have fervent charity among yourselves….” (I Pet.4:8)

J. FAWCETT Doh is F H.G. NAGELI

1) Christian lungset hrui in,
Akhit manchun jo me;
Pumkhat chang ah malung rôn hi,
Chung ting ah leh in ang.

2) Nipa Reng in – ông na,
Eini khat leh inkhat;
Ni’n hôrna lungdo inkhat rōk,
Ni’n ngai na Reng inkhat.

3) Eini ni’nreng pha leh,
Inbôm tō êt inna;
Eini inkhat leh inkhat hi,
Inlungset tō êt rih.

4) Eini ni’nṭhen pha leh,
Ni malung kaher ri;
Tâkkha ni lân hmu nôk sik hi,
Ni’n hôrna Chunghnungpên!
