17. I NEED THEE EVERY HOUR (S.S.577)

“Hear me, for I am poor and needy.” (Ps.86:1)

ANNIE S. HAWKS Doh is GROBERT LOWRY

1) Nang ka rangei ṭhak che,
Lungset Pathen!
Nang kapō nun kahōi,
Ra lak om mak.

Eh! Rōngpu ka rangei che!
Sûn sôt ei om pui roh;
Vângṭhat ajōng pe roh,
Rōngpu! Kahong eng.

2) Nang ka rangei ṭhak che,
A-om pui roh;
Mathlêm na hlut mak ning,
Na hngâk inchu.

3) Nang ka rangei ṭhak che,
Khōdeng thei nâm;
Kahneng ah om inna,
Ei manhring roh.
