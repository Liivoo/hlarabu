130. AM I A SOLDIER OF THE CROSS? (R.S.480)

“…endure hardness, as a good soldier of Jesus Christ.” (II Tim.2:3)

T.C. O’KANE Doh is GCcROLINE WICHERN

1) Kemôngte kajui ah inṭang,
Cross sepai eng kaeh;
Ṭit mak ning a-re vatlo sik,
Arahming kaphông sik.

Thlipui ahrâng lai,
Kahnet tak ah ngir ṭhak rih;
Kahnet ah ngir ruh,
Lên lukhu hmu nasik ah.

2) Rei pârna marvân kahōi ah,
Aniṭhōi nger sik eng;
Marvân ah inpâk man hlo na,
Mi lak hei in-êr hei.

3) Kei sik ani kadōi katam,
Thisen tui kape lai;
Ralei ah kên Rōngpu teilo,
Ṭhōn ralak nei mak eng.

4) Rōngpu! Ratha ajōng pe roh,
Kên hma lang hnong jo rong;
Rōngpu! Nân nânṭangpui eng inchu,
Inreng sik ṭit mak eng.
