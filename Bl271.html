271. WHAT A WONDERFUL SAVIOUR (S.S.119)

“And His name shall be called Wonderful.” (Isai.9:6)

E.A.H. Doh is D ELISHA A. HOFFMAN

1) Jesûn setna asôp mantheng,
Tlensōtpa inlak tak kha;
Athisen in akatlen me,
Tlensōtpa inlak tak kha.

Tlensōtpa inlak tak kha,
Rōngpu Jesu Christa;
Tlensōtpa inlak tak kha,
Ni Rōngpu Jesu.

2) Akatlen me athisen vâng,
Tlensōtpa inlak tak kha;
Ni Pathen in a-om pui ṭhak,
Tlensōtpa inlak tak kha.

3) Hlam amanthei ah aṭhōi eng,
Tlensōtpa inlak tak khan;
Kadam seng ah aṭhōi ṭhak eng,
Tlensōtpa inlak tak khan.

4) Hrât na leh lungmông nâm apêk,
Tlensōtpa inlak tak khan;
Kei intheng tak ah ada roh,
Tlensōtpa inlak tak khan.
