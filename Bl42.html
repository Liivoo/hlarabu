42. WORK, FOR THE NIGHT IS COMING (S.S.778)

“The night cometh, when no man can work.” (Jn.9:4)

ANNA L. WALKER COGHILLDoh is FLOWELL MASON

1) Ṭōn tlo ruh khōvâr lai hin, jingkâr kavâr ah hin,
Radai tui akai lai hin, rei apâr lai hin;
Tlo ruh kani hlut hma seng, kani dai âm tlo ruh,
Ṭōn tlo theina lo jên sik jân jōng katlung sik.

2) Ṭōn tlo ruh anthim hma seng, kani hlut mak hma seng,
Tlo ruh kani om lai seng, châm inhnga mak ruh;
Avâr lai seng ṭōn tlo ruh, tlo sik anhmu lai seng,
Ṭōn tlo ruh inthim sik jo, ê-na kahnai jo.

3) Ṭōn tlo ruh jân kahnai jo, tlo ruh kani om lai,
Ṭōn tlo ruh pha om lai hin, ê-na kahnai jo;
Tlo ruh ajingte ênnum châm inhngat nasik ah,
Ṭōn tlo ruh jân kahnai jo, tlo bâng nasik ah.
