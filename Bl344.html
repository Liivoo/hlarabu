344. TEMPERANCE BELLS (S.S.700)

“Jesus said, I came…to save the world.” (Jn.12:47)

W.S. Doh is G WILLIAM STEVENSON

1) Rangei ruh! Bêkna dâr inhring,
Inpâk na hla kha inthei;
Ratha ngar leh lungmông apêk,
Innpui kahōi sûng ah sik.

Rangei ruh! Inpâk hla,
Dâr mahring leh hla inthô;
Rangei ruh! Dâr mahring,
Jubilee pha anhring kha.

2) Êng kasepa ni’nre pui lai,
Rōngpu’n ami alungset;
Kan re pui ratha kabōi sik,
Avairôn na katlang sik.

3) Û leh nai rakip intûp rih,
Eini in-êng mak jo rih;
Jing khōvâr kapō ang û-na,
Intûp ning kani ah hin.
