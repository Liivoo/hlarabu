106. BEYOND THE SWELLING FLOOD (S.S.915)

“God shall wipe away all tears from their eyes.” (Rev.21:4)

A.E. CHILDS Doh is GJOHN H. TENNEY

1) Intheng jo me thisen jârra,
Inhmu sik me tui rekhat têng;
Inṭhen sik ṭit nôk mak jo min,
Setna inphut kahring jo me;
Jesu thisen jârra.

Thisen tui rekhat têng, thisen tui rekhat têng,
Thisen tui rekhat têng, inṭhen nôk mak jo min;
Inṭhen nôk mak jo min, inṭhen nôk mak jo min,
Inṭhen nôk mak jo min, Jesu thisen jârra.

2) Nat leh sat ṭit nôk mak jo min,
Marvân ram ni inn kahōi ah;
Rathla Intheng in abêk me,
Nihmit rathli kahi sik jo;
Jesu thisen jârra.

3) Ṭimre madu hei nihmu sik,
Inpâk na hla nisak sap sik;
Ka rathla malung amanhring,
Sana kot pui um inhlang sik;
Jesu thisen jârra.
