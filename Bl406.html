406. HMŌIṬHŌI NA INNSHUNG (M.C.K.1)

“…they are no more twain, but one flesh.” (Mk.10:8)

M. CHUNGRENGKHUP KOM Doh is F 19/07/2016

1) Pathen rōisyem pha kahōi kani lunpui,
Jōng katlung jo alungset marit jârra;
Lungset kathem Oh! Pathen! Kanmanpâk che,
Manpâk om rasoh aphat tin ah.

Hmōiṭhōina innshung kan inpâk puitluk ning hin,
Oh! Chunghnung Pathen! Kan manpâk che;
Damna jōng pê roh, vâng kaṭha jōng pê roh,
Ralei hringkhō om sung ah Christa vairônna innshung;
Hōi tlung tor nasik Oh! Pathen! Vâng ṭhat kander che.

2) Lungset kathem oh! Nu le Pa, û leh nai,
Hiva ralei hringkhō ah na jōng tir hei;
Anlungset len je hri tor lo, tei tor lo,
Damna jōng pê roh oh! Kander che.

3) Innshung kahōi marvân Rengram inhnai pên,
Raleichung innshung kahōi rōk chang lo ah;
Marvân chunga’m keini sik chen kander che,
Rōngpu lungset marit kander che.

4) Christa’n abêk-rup alungset ṭhak kapō,
Kahnet tak ah kaom lungset chang rasoh;
Pa lungdo ah amanchun lungset na hi,
Aheitik ah lungset lêng rasoh.
