158. NAILED THE CROSS (3rdALEX.54)

“The place…called Calvary…there they crucified Him.” (Lk.23:33)

FRED. P. MORRIS Doh is FGRANT COLFAX TULLAR

1) Tlensōtpa kaset intheng nasik ah kathi,
Akathi sa kei aman hring jo;
Ka cross hlam hro sik kha Amân alei hro pêk,
Ka setna rakip amantheng jo.

Ka setna cross chung ah ka Rōngpu’n arôn pêk,
Setna khangja arangei mandam;
Lungdong hri theilo jên ade Calvary ah,
Kaset Ȏ katam achôi pêk jo.

2) Kei sik ah nun kahōi leh dehrât ka Rōngpu!
Ka malung dong na amantheng jo;
Tûn bân chu kei sik apum ah kabōi sik jo,
Kaset cross chung ah amathlang jo.

3) Tûnchu kên Rōngpu kahnung kajui ṭhak sik jo,
Inpâk pumma aṭong hri jo rong;
Lung ri leh bei tak âm amanpâk hla sak rong,
Ka setna tûnchu kabōi sō jo.
