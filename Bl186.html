186. REJOICE IN THE LORD (S.S.207)

“Let the righteous be glad! Yea, let them exceedingly rejoice.” (Ps.68:3)

MARY E. SERVOSS Doh is BbJAMES McGRANAHAN

1) Inpâk ruh, Pathen ah hōi ruh,
Malung intheng, mi intheng hei;
Rōngpu Pathen kha taksen ruh,
Mandon ṭhak ruh nan malung ah.

Hōi ruh! Hōi ruh! Inpâk ruh! Pathen ah hōi ruh!
Hōi ruh! Hōi ruh! Inpâk ruh! Pathen ah hōi ruh!

2) Amarōk Rōngpu, manpâk ruh!
Marvân leh ralei mi seng in;
Ama hneng hōina kaom eh!
Asen sik, hring na apêk sik.

3) Amân a-en me, inpâk ruh!
A-om na marvân chung ah khin;
Alungset kut vâng aṭhōi me,
Amathlung sik aram ah khin.

4) Thlipui, khōpui anthō innum,
En ruh! Rōngpu lêng na marvân!
Ama ṭong kajui hei aṭhōi,
Marvân mi intheng hei lêng na.

5) Pathen jârra inpâk û-na,
Nan malung ah khan manpâk ruh;
Hri leh rai Ama ṭong kaeh,
Hallelujah! ti manpâk ruh.
