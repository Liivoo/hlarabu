386. VÂN RAM KAHŌI (M.C.K.4)

“…they may rest from their labours….” (Rev.14:13)

M. CHUNGRENGKHUP KOM Doh is F 04/08/2004

1) Châm hngat na hmun kahōi kaom,
Sana Khōpui kalûr tak kha;
Ahōije hri tor lo,
Mi intheng hei lêng nasik ram kaeh.

2) Sopui hma lei kase hei leh,
Inhmu tō sik me khan ah khan;
Khōpui kalûr sung khan,
Kalêng sap sik jo me akumsôt ah.

3) Hmit rathli aman hi pêk sik,
Thina leh malung inrim na;
Nat leh ṭana rakip,
Ommak jo ning chung vân ram kahōi ah.

4) Kani leh thla rangei mak jo,
Jân inthim um om mak jo ning;
Kemôngte hmeivâr khan,
Asat manvâr sik vân ram kahōi kha.

5) Ni Rōngpu kajōng bei sik jo,
Alungset hnōi ah hong jo rih;
Dehrât ah ṭōn tlo rih,
Hring na lên lukhu nichen nger sik jo.
