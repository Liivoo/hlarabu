307. WHY DO YOU WAIT? (S.S.351)

“Be good comfort, rise, He calleth thee.” (Mk.10:49)

G.F.R. Doh is A GEORGE F. ROOT

1) Hngâk mak jo ruh eh! Sopui!
Nang insûm jo che, inrang roh;
Nan Tlensōtpân ala hngâk ṭhak che,
An – ông na hmun intheng ah khan.

Hong roh, hong roh,
Ahneng ah tûn hong roh;
Insûm mak roh,
Tûn Ahneng ah hong roh.

2) Heimo na lân hôr pui hi?
Nan sûm jônchu inhmang sik che
Rōngpu Jesu! Hring na kûng kaeh,
Ama teilo hlam lak om mak.

3) Manhret mak che mo sopui!
Na sûng ah Pathen Rathla kha?
Na hring nasik inlo inhlut roh,
Naset rôn kahrik hlonsōt roh.

4) Hngâk mak jo roh eh! Sopui!
Sapâr khôm pha inkhe sik jo;
Nân Tlenpân vâng pêk sik ahngâk che,
Nan sûm jônchu thina kaeh.
