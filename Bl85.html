85. JESUS! MY ALL (S.S.478)

“Hear my cry oh! God, attend to my prayer.” (Ps.61:1)

FANNY J. CROSBY Doh is AbSCOTCH AIR

1) Rōngpu! Nalungset hmun ah katlu eng,
Kabêkna thei inna kei aṭhōi roh;
Amantheng inna tûn kên naṭōn phut jo rong,
Set intheng na Rōngpu! Kahei rakip.

2) Hmit rathli pumma kamalung kathleng,
Taksen na amannei jo roh kei tûn;
Không ah mo inṭang sik eng ka hlamrin na hi?
Kei athi pêk Jesûn – kahei rakip.

3) Rangei ruh lungset kare’n abêk chei,
Kabêk na che kare athei pêk roh;
Kamalung in-ôk na rakip kabâng sō jo,
Nang ta chang rông Jesu! Kahei rakip.

4) Nalungset hmun ah kei kaom ṭhak eng,
Nalungset ṭongkam na khan aṭhōi roh;
Taksen na ka rathla jōng thei kamadu pên,
Kei jârra jōng kathi Rōngpu Jesu.
