395. MANDONSŌTNA HLUNGPUI (M.C.K.30)

“…this heap is a witness between me and thee this day….” (Gen.31:48)

M. CHUNGRENGKHUP KOM Doh is G 26/04/2018

1) Intêp na hlungbuk Galeed,
Laban leh Jacob sōn leh pâr hei sik;
Mandonsōt na hlungbuk kapō,
Intung jo mandonsōt na hlungpui hi.

Sōn leh pâr, khang rakip ah,
Mandonsōt ah kaom ṭhak sik jo;
Ralei hringkhō om kâr seng,
Mandonsōt na hlungpui kachang sik jo.

2) Jordan tuidung rekhat ting,
Rōngpu ṭōn tlo inlak mandon nasik;
Intungsōt hlungpui kapōah,
Intung jo, Rōngpu ṭōn tlo inlâr jo.

3) Akahring ṭhak hlung kapō,
Rathla Bêk-Inn sak na khan inhmang rih;
Inpêk sōt ah aṭōn tlo rih,
Ni’nhôr na rakip nichen nger sik jo.



# This song was composed by M. Chungrengkhup Kom
in remembrance of beloved (L) M. Ringkhupjang Kom
(1939 – 02/04/2017), wife of M. Korthatlin Kom
Lairamwangma Baptist Church, Sagang, KRBCA, Manipur.
