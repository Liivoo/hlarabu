73. HOW SWEET THE NAME! (S.S.112)

“…a name which is above every name.” (Phil.2:9)

JOHN NEWTONDoh is EbALEXANDER REINAGLE

1) Jesu rahming kalûr tak hi,
Rangei ruh ami seng;
Malung kadong sik ah mông na,
Akadam mak, dam na.

2) Ṭit na leh malung dong na hi,
Lungdong inreng invai;
Akheng ah amathlang sō jo,
Mông mak na rakip khâm.

3) Rathla nat dam theina Ama!
Lungdong mông na Ama!
Rathla hring nâm Ama kaeh,
Akadam hei ê-na.

4) Christa hlungpui chung ah ngir na,
Kapho leh ka chem jâm;
Ka-karōi inbuk na Ama!
Alungset karōi hi.

5) Jesu kapu, kaṭhōn, kare,
Ka Pathen, ka Thempu;
Kahring na hlam kasât Ama!
Kader na rahlâng roh.

6) Kathi sō leh arahming hi,
Ka hring nasik kaeh;
Amanpâk na hla kahōi kha,
Bâng tik nei loah sak rong.
