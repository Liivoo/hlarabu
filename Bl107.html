107. I KNOW HIS VOICE (S.S.861)

“They know His voice.” (Jn.10:4)

JOHNSON OATMAN, J R. Doh is AbHOWARD E. SMITH

1) Ka Rōngpu kahmu mak ravâng,
Ka hneng ah a-om pui ṭhak eng;
Aṭōn madu katlo lai hin,
Amanpâk na kare kathei.

Amanpâk na kare kathei,
Kamalung inpâk na kasip;
Hei lai ênnum a-en ṭhak eng,
Akare kathei manpâk ruh!

2) Amalungset ka mahngi num,
Akare rasa kathei ṭhak;
Vânhnōi setna arôn sō jo,
Taksen êt rih, hring nasik ah.

3) Kadik tak ah amanthei eng,
Kamalung ah châm inhngat na;
Khiva ram khi katlung pha leh,
Rōngpu manpâk hla kasak sik.
