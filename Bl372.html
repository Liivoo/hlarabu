372. SALVATION HAS BEEN BROUGHT DOWN

“…the place…called Calvary, there they crucified Him….” (Lk.23:33)

A.E.B. Doh is Bb ALBERT EDWARD BRUMLEY

1) Jesu’n aleipêk ahringkhō Calvary tlâng chung ah,
(B-Calvary) Eini set jârra Calvary tlâng ah (B-Calvary);
Lên lukhu nichen thei nasik Athisen atôk jo.

Manpâk êt ruh Arahming kha,
(B-Arahming manpâk ruh Reng kalen Rengpa kha);
Manpâk ruh hûksōt na ajōng rôn jo (A-Marvân ah),
Va ôn rih, ram tin ah va phôngsōt rih (B-Tuning se rih);
Va hri jo rih (A & T-Malung kakui hei hneng ),
Tuning anphut (A & T-Ṭōn phut jo rih tuning );
Pathen ṭong hri ruh lên lukhu chen nasik (kha chen thei nasik)
(B- Marvân ah) Hri pêk ruh mankase ahûksōt na;
(A-Mi kaset) Tuntak hin ram rakip ah va hri ruh ,
(B-Va hri jo ruh) Ram rakip ah (A&T-Ralei mihring rakip);
Va hri le ruh(A & T-Pathen mi rakip hneng),
Manpâk êt ruh hûksōtpa Rengpa kha.

2) Akabôm sik om lo hêr ah nibat rakip arôn,
(B-Calvary) Oh! Nibat rakip Jesu’n arôn jo (B-Calvary);
Aṭongtêp lungset jârra re torna aman nei jo.

3)Marvân ah inn kahōi tak amantluk sōt pêk jo me,
(B-Calvary) Marvân ram ah khan inn kahōi kaom (B-Calvary);
Amarit jârra eini marvân mi kachang jo me.

(A-Alto, B-Bass, T-Tenor) (Staff Notation to Tonic Solfa by: M. Chungrengkhup Kom)
