230. JESUS IS RISEN (S.S.146)

“…the firstfruits of them that slept.” (I Cor.15:20)

E.A. BARNES Doh is C WILLIAM J. KIRKPATRICK

1) En ruh! Thōi ning Easter kani,
Inpâk lunpui kani;
En ruh! Hlungpui kha len hlik jo,
Thlân kha lei karōk jo.

Alei hri kapō kathōi jo,
Jesu thlân ah inphut kathōi;
Easter ning hin inpâk hla sak ruh,
Rōngpu Jesu kha kathōi sōt jo.

2) En ruh! Jesu intōm na pōn,
Inthlep sōt ah kaom;
En ruh! Thlân kha lei karōk jo,
Jesu lei om mak jo.

3) En ruh! Ama kase sōt jo,
Ralei chung ah kalôn;
En ruh! Marvân chung ah inphut,
Alûr na jōng inpe.

4) En ruh! Jesu thōina kani,
Ṭong leh hla vâng phông rih;
En ruh! Kutchôi pêk ning kani,
Rōngpu re tor ning hi.
