33. WE SHALL STAND BEFORE THE KING (S.S.966)

“Thou shalt stand before Me, saith the Lord.” (Jer.15:19)

E.O.E. Doh is AbEDWIN O. EXCELL

1) Rōngpu hma kangir sik me,
Sonle leh hla nisak sik;
Akin leh jajâm ah, sana khōvâr ni la hmu,
Amanpâk hla nisak sik, akin leh jajâm ah.

Rōngpu hma kangir sik me,
Sonle leh hla nisak sik;
Rōngpu lûrna chang rasoh,
Hallelujah! Hallelujah!
Rōngpu hma kangir sik me.

2) Marvân dâr vo manhring ruh,
Rōngpu hma kangir sik me;
Akin leh jajâm ah, ni’nreng na nibâng sik jo,
Arahming ni manpâk sik, akin leh jajâm ah.

3) Ka rathla nachen chôi roh,
Rōngpu hma kangir sik me;
Akin leh jajâm ah, nahrât man kha nahmu sik,
Ama hneng kaom sik che, akin leh jajâm ah.
