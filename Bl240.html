240. GOLDEN HARPS ARE SOUNDING (S.S.87)

“Him that God exalted…to be Prince and a Saviour.” (Acts.5:31)

F.R.H. Doh is A FRANCES R. HAVERGAL

1) Marvân Tirle rajân Sana ṭing-ṭang vâng,
Hla marit sak pumma kot pui anmahlang;
Marvân Rengpa Jesûn thina ator jo,
Hla marit sak pumma marvân ajōn jo.

Thina ator sō ah Jesu kathōi jo,
Eini sik hmun kasyem marvân kake jo.

2) Eini hûksōt jârra Ama jōng kathi,
Reng lukhu ankhu ah Apa changting khan;
De hêr nôk mak jo ning, thi nôk mak jo ning,
Lûrna Rengpa Jesu! Marvân kake jo.

3) Eini sik hmun Asyem amarvân inn ah,
Hong ruh nang ni ati, lungset vâng abêk;
Nang ni sik hmun kada, naipang hei ati,
Kumsôt hring na leh lungset Jesu kaeh.
