192. ALL HAIL IMMANUEL (3rdALEX.217)

“Worthy is the Lamb.” (Rev.5:12)

D.R. VAN SICKLE Doh is Bb CHARLES H. GABRIEL

1) Akalûrpên Immanuel! Kan lukhu nahma kan hlik,
Mi rakip in akajana nan kare vâng kamuk ruh;
Ni’n Sandampa manpâk nasik marvân,
Khōngpui jōn hring sonna amahring kha jōn thang rasoh;
Ȏn roh! Ȏn roh! Ȏn roh! Ȏn roh! Immanuel!

Oh! Immanuel! Immanuel! Oh! Immanuel! Immanuel!
Lenna leh ṭong kadik leh lûrna;
Sing na leh hrât na Nangta kaeh, tûnnum, hnung ênnum,
Oh! Immanuel! Immanuel! Oh! Immanuel! Immanuel!
Reng ah reng rakip! Rōngpu! Immanuel! Rengpa!

2) Akalûrpên Immanuel! Hûksōt hên ankake che,
Reng lên lukhu kha mankhu ruh, ôn ruh alen in-ông na;
Vânhnōi mi na Sandam seng in,
Naṭong tanna len in-ông na kumsôt hla kahōi kha ansak;
Ȏn roh! Ȏn roh! Ȏn roh! Ȏn roh! Immanuel!

3) Akalûrpên Immanuel! Thlân anphut kathōi Rengpa!
An repui hei ator sō jo, Ama rahming kalûr jo;
Set leh thina dôk hmun ator,
Thi leh nat na om mak jo ning, Ama rahming lûr ṭhak rasoh;
Ȏn roh! Ȏn roh! Ȏn roh! Ȏn roh! Immanuel!
