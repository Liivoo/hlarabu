248. SAFE WITHIN THE VEIL (S.S.938)

“…entered into that within the veil.” (Heb.6:19)

E. ADAMS Doh is G J.M. EVANS

1) Ṭhum hmun kahōi ram in-êm reng,
Hring na thing inra na hmun;
Hring tui intheng kalōng ṭhak jo,
Thang vân ram kahōi ah khan.

Thli khōpui ṭit mak jo min,
Hring na ram kahōi ah khan;
Kaom na hmun kahnet sik,
Hmun kahōi kahlô sik jo.

2) Kalôn lele na kahmu sik,
Vâng pêk na kut injâr kha;
Rōngpu Serângdâr leh Rosêm,
Khōngpui leh baza inthei.

3) Ram kahōi ah se rih eini,
Sana vâr ra-âng na ram;
Thlipui-khōpui ṭit mak jo min,
Vâr in-êm reng sō sik jo.

4) Mathlêm na nikhe sō tak leh,
Ralei hringkhō abâng leh;
Tlensōtpa ni manpâk sik jo,
Aṭhōi manhlut sik jo me.
