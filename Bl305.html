305. ROLLING ON THE WHEELS OF TIME

“Man is like to vanity: his days are as a shadow that passeth away.” (Ps.144:4)

Doh is G

1) Apha dung jui ah kathleng rih,
Anitin ah katleng rih;
Ralei chung ṭōn tlo dahlon ah,
Inn hlam kajôn ah se rong.

Kathleng ṭhak, thleng ṭhak hringkho, kathleng ṭhak,
Kathleng ṭhak, thleng ṭhak hringkho, kathleng ṭhak;
Apha dung, pha dung jui ah kathleng ṭhak,
Kathleng ṭhak, thleng ṭhak hringkho, kathleng ṭhak.

2) Akum dung jui ah kase rih,
Chung leh hnōi kut syem rakip;
Malung ah inpâk na kasip,
Inreng – invai kasip eh!

3) Ralei chung mi lising raja,
Kên ka-en mantûp hei hi;
Ka ṭōn tlo hei kachai pha leh,
Inn jôn ah kase sik eng.

4) Rōngpu hneng nitin ah der ruh,
Hlam ’dik ah aṭhōi nasik;
Kha teilo hlam ’dik lak om mak,
Kha rōk kha hlam ’dik kaeh.
