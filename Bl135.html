135. THOUGH THOU TAKE…WE LOVE (3rdALEX.375)

“God shall wipe away all tears from their eyes.” (Rev.21:4)

MARTYRDOM C.M Doh is AH. WILSON

1) Kanta rangei kan inpâk na,
Jōng kapâr kan kârra;
Kan maduna achi hma hin,
Ṭa pumma kan ṭhen jo.

2) Mihring mandon phâk lo jên ah,
Kapâr marit tak ah;
Set ralei ah kalêng sik ah,
Ni Rōngpu’n phe mak jo.

3) Heisamo ni lungset sik ah?
Apa hneng ah sik chu;
Marvân ah mi intheng hei leh,
Kalêng sap sik jo hei.

4) Tûn ṭa pumma ni’nṭhen jônnum,
Lân hmu tō nôk sik me;
Inpâk tak ah marvân ram ah,
Kalêng sap sik jo me.

5) Oh! Ahme leh aṭhat na kha,
Kanpâk na rakip kha;
Ṭap hrât ah manhring thei mak ning,
Thina khan aṭhōi jo.

6) Thina hi rangei mak jo hei,
Rōngpu lungset mihên;
An marvân kot pui kha kaeh,
Rōngpu hme hmu nasik.
