371. COUNT YOUR BLESSINGS (S.S.306)

“…God…hath blessed us with all spiritual blessings…in Christ.” (Ephe.1:3)

JOHNSON OATMAN JR. Doh is Eb EDWIN O. EXCELL

1) Hringkhō ah insuk sêt na pha sung ah hin,
Malung tlâk suk na pha ahong chang pha leh;
Jong sōt roh! Pathen vâng pêk kha kho hi mo?
Na hmu pha leh nang in inlak nasak sik.

Jong sōt roh! Pathen vâng pêk che kha,
En roh! Rōngpu lungset na che kha;
Jong sōt roh! Vâng kha inkha-khat ah,
Na hmu pha leh nang in inlak nasak sik.

2) Malung châm leh inrim na nalanei mo?
Natarôn cross kha akahrik rei jo mo?
Jong sōt roh! Pathen vâng kha inkha-khat ah,
Inhnôk na om mak ning, kahōi sik jo che.

3) Nang in mihei ran leh sûm na-en phat leh,
Mandon roh Christa tapêk sik che ran kha;
Khava kapō kha rachôk sik hmu mak tin,
Khava kakhe kha om mak ning ralei ah.

4) Khōdeng thei na, inpâk na pha rakip ah,
Malung châm na leh inrim na nei mak roh;
Pathen in ahmu pêt, amanhret sō pêt,
Marvân Ngeva he’n anching ṭhak sik jo che.

Translated By: M. Linpa Kom, Khonomphai B/Church
