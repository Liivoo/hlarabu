295. ONE IS OUR MASTER (S.S.500)

“…One is our Master, even Christ; and all ye are brethren.” (Matt.23:8)

ELIZA E. HEWITT Doh is Ab WILLIAM J. KIRKPATRICK

1) Inkhat rōk kaeh ani’n Tlensōtpa kha,
Amalungset nân akhit me kaeh;
Lungset na khan eihûk manvâr nôk rasoh,
Hmei sûk rasoh akathi nôk mak sik.

Jesu rahming ah hong ruh sopui hei!
Inkûn rih ni Tlensōtpa âng sûng ah;
Inkûn rih a-âng sûng ah innêm tak ah,
Alûrna ni chôi mansâng mak lai seng.

2) Rōngpu inkhat rōk inpâk ah rajen sik,
Rōngpu lungdo hlam ah eini’n tlo rih;
Ahrât nân ni tlâksam pui amathluk tor,
Ni rangei rakip Amân apêk me.

3) Rōngpu inkhat rōk inpâk ah rajen sik,
Aheitik âm Alungset inthleng mak;
Kaja tak ah jui ruh, pōnlek kalek ruh,
Arahming lên ṭhak rasoh, are-ah.

4) Rōngpu inkhat rōk kaeh aṭōn tlo sik,
Inrin ah hngâk rih ajōng nasik kha,
Inthleng sik me Ama lûrna kapōah,
A Reng hmun ah inpâk man nihmu sik.
