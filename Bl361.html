361. WONDERFUL GRACE OF JESUS (C.S.H.399)

“For ye know the grace of our Lord Jesus Christ.” (II Cor.8:9)

H.L. Doh is D HALDOR LILLENAS

1) Jesu lungset inlak tak, kaset kanêk kalên,
Kabei in hri sōt tor mak, heikhong mo kahri sik?
Karôn kahrik alo sōt, karathla amanhring,
Jesu lungset inlak khan kei ahûksōt eng.

Jesu lungset marit inlak, atepui thei om mak,
[Jesu lungset marit inlak tak kha]
Tui-ôngpui inthûk kanêk ah inthûk ah inthûk;
[Tui-ôngpui inthûk kanêk ah inthûk]
Jesu lungset marit inlak kha kei sik ah katluk,
[Tlâng insâng nêk insâng, kalōng tuipui kapōah]
[Alungset na kei sik katluk hrep]
Ka setna hei seng arangei mandam;
[Ka setna hei seng arangei mandam sō jo]
Kan jak na hei seng khâm alo sōt,
Oh! Manpâk ruh! Jesu rahming marit kha;
Manpâk ruh!

2) Jesu lungset inlak khan anhmang sa ajong sōt,
Kei arangei mandam eng, kei ahûksōt jo eng;
Thir rake akeitan, kei amasō jo eng,
Jesu lungset inlak khan kei ahûksōt eng.

3) Jesu lungset inlak khan ajōng rôn hring na kha,
A Ratha hrât na vâng khan Pathen sa amanchang;
Arachôk marvân mông na aheitik ah sik ah,
Jesu lungset inlak khan kei ahûksōt eng.
