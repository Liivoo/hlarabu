268. WHAT MUST IT BE TO BE THERE (S.S.923)

“There shall be no more death, neither sorrow nor crying.” (Rev.21:4)

E. MILLS Doh is C GEORGE C. STEBBINS

1) Eini’n ni hri ṭhak ram kahōi,
Avâr intheng ah kavâr ṭhak;
Alûrna hei kham in hri ṭhak,
Tâk heimo khan akachang sik?

Khan ah khan, khan ah kha,
Oh! Heimo akachang sik khan?
Khan ah khan, khan ah kha,
Oh! Heimo akachang sik khan?

2) Ni hri ṭhak Sana ram kahōi,
Abang hlung mankatam kaeh;
Aṭhat, alûr inhri tor mak,
Tâk heimo khan akachang sik?

3) Ni hri lung mông leh lungset na,
Ahrui lûrna katluk anjêm;
Chungting ah vâng pêk na hla vâng,
Tâk heimo khan akachang sik?

4) Ni hri setna inphut masō,
Lungdong leh mathlêm mandonna;
Khōdeng theina leh sûng leh vâng,
Tâk heimo khan akachang sik?
