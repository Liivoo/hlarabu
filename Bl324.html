324. VERILY VERILY! (S.S.359)

“He that believeth on Me hath everlasting life.” (Jn.6:47)

J.M.G. Doh is G JAMES McGRANAHAN

1) Tlensōtpa! Kei sik ah jōng kathi jo,
Ka rake rôn ahlik sōt pêk jo eng;
Sapa kataksen rakip hei khan,
Kumsôt hring anchen sik.

Inchên khet, inchên khet ah kahri kaeh,
Ṭong kather, ṭong inthang, ṭong kadik ta hri;
Sapa kataksen rakip khan chu,
Kumsôt hring na anei.

2) Kaset Ȏ rakip achung ah katlung,
Karâk rakip achôi pêk jo eng;
Rōngpu’n ati, takkasen mihe’n,
Kumsôt hring na anchen jo.

3) Sara kachang jônnum Rōngpu kanei,
Kaset jônnum aṭong kha kataksen;
Tu ênnum takkasen rakip in,
Kumsôt hring anchen jo.
