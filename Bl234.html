234. HARK! TEN THOUSAND HARPS (S.S.525)

“Let all the angels of God worship Him.” (Heb.1:6)

T. KELLY Doh is G LOWELL MASON

1) Kare insâng tak ah ôn ruh,
Jesu lûr na hla sak ruh;
Jesu vai rôn na marvân leh,
Ralei chung lungset kasip;
A Reng in-ông na en ruh!
Avai rôn na ralei hi’m;
Hallelujah! Hallelujah!
Jesûn ralei vai arôn.

2) Lûrna Rengpa kumsôt sik ah,
Hring na lên lukhu anei;
Asyemsa hring na kachen hên,
Thina rahim ankhe jo;
Alungset hri thei chang mak,
Nahme hmu sik kâr kân hngâk hla;
Hallelujah! Hallelujah!
Nahme hmu sik inhôr ung.

3) Rōngpu inrang tak ah jōng roh,
Kani kalûr kân hngâk hla;
Bêkna rasa kânthei pha leh,
Marvân ralei inhmang sik;
Sana pêngkun vâng ansak,
Ni Rengpa kha lûr ṭhak rasoh;
Hallelujah! Hallelujah!
Ni Reng kha lûr ṭhak rasoh.
