149. SEND THE GOSPEL LIGHT (S.S.1085)

“Oh! Send out Thy light and Thy truth.” (Ps.43:3)

FANNY J. CROSBY Doh is GIRA D. SANKEY

1) Rōngpu! Vânhnōi inthim sûng ah,
Na vârna jōng tir inna;
An intlenpa manhret nasik,
Hring na anhmu nasik ah.

Eh! Rōngpu! Vârna jōng tir roh,
Tuipui re ram rakip ah;
Nalungset na leh makhu na,
Akheng ah pêk na rei kha.

2) Setna jân inthim kârra hin,
Rathla inhmang sik sen roh;
Anhringkhō leh antaksa hi,
Bong khat kârra inhmang sik.

3) Na ṭongkam marit leh Intheng,
Gospel ṭong marit tak hi;
Kan hneng ah man om ṭhak inna,
Kan hringkhō sei bâng kâr seng.
