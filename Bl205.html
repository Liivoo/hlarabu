205. NUMBERLESS AS THE SANDS OF THE SEA (S.S.998)

“The number shall be as the sand of the sea.” (Hos.1:10)

F.A.B. Doh is Ab F.A. BLACKMER

1) Jordan tui rekhat ting ni’ntûp pha leh,
Sandam sa hei lûrna nihmu sik;
Teitor lo tui-ôngpui nêrdi kapō,
En hnōm kaom tak kha kachang sik.

Tei tor lo tui-ôngpui nerdi kapō,
Tei tor lo tui-ôngpui nerdi kha;
En hnōm tak kachang sik,
Sandam hei nihmu pha leh;
Khan tei tor lo tui-ôngpui nêrdi kapōah.

2) Inreng leh setna kârra inphut ah,
Sandam sa hei lûrna nihmu sik;
Marvân mi’ntheng hei leh intō sik me,
En hnōm kaom tak kha kachang sik.

3) Tuidung kalûr pang ah ni’ntûp pha leh,
Hring na thingkûng daihlim hnōi ah khan;
Ahri sa ram kahōi kha thlir pumma,
En hnōm kaom tak kha kachang sik.

4) Achai ning ni’n Tlenpa nihmu pha leh,
Alûrna kha nihmu pha tak leh;
Reng leh Rōngpu’n chung ah vai arôn sik,
Mitin in inlak eh! anti sik.
