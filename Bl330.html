330. LOOK AND LIVE (S.S.411)

“Look unto Me, and be ye saved….” (Isai.45:22)

W.A.O. Doh is G WILLIAM A. OGDEN

1) Rōngpu ṭong kha kajōng rôn Hallelujah!
Kên nahneng ah ka hri kaeh;
Amân num lum alo pui Hallelujah!
Nan en inchu kahring nger sik chei.

Hong en ruh! Hring nasik ah,
Jesu en ruh hring nasik;
Aṭong Intheng in ahri Hallelujah!
Nan en inchu kahring nger sik chei.

2) Lungset ṭong kha kajōng rôn Hallelujah!
Ṭimre leh nang sik ah kaeh;
Jesu ta hri ṭong kaeh Hallelujah!
Ṭong kadik hi kên kamanhret chên.

3) Hring na ṭong inpe jo chei Hallelujah!
Kumsôt rathla hring na kaeh;
Jesu Ama rōk en roh! Hallelujah!
Kumsôt rathla hring na nanei jo.
