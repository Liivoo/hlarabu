161. ALL PEOPLE THAT ON EARTH (S.S.9)

“Enter into His gates with thanksgiving…praise…thankful….” (Ps.100:4)

W. KETHE & T. KEN Doh is GLOUIS BOURGEOIS

[Manpâk Na Hla]
1) Vâng jōng sōk na chung ah Pathen!
Vânhnōi ah mi’n manpâk êt rih;
Apa, Asa, Rathla Intheng,
Manpâk rih chung leh hnōi ah mi’n.

[Thlâi Sâk Na Hla]
2) Eh! Rōngpu! Sâk leh in rakip,
Napêk ung hi vâng jōng sem roh;
Nahring na buhlom sem vâng khan,
Kan rathla hi jōng vei pêk roh –Amen!

[Ṭin Na Hla]
3) Kan intûp na kaṭin jo ung,
Kan hneng ah Rōngpu! om ṭhak roh;
Khôn ênnum kan om na rakip,
Na inn Intheng chang ṭhak rasoh – Amen!
