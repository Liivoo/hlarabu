128. ANYWHERE WITH JESUS (S.S.627)

“…He shall hide me in His pavilion…set me up upon a rock” (Ps.27:5)

JESSIE H. BROWN Doh is DDANIEL B. TOWNER

1) Khôn ênnum kei kasena hmun rakip ah,
Setna ratō mak ning Jesu kanṭhōipui;
Ama om mak na chu inpâk na om mak,
Jesu kanṭhōipui lai seng kei ṭit mak ning.

Ṭi mak ning! Ṭi mak ning! Ahmun rakip ah,
Kut-sôi, bân-sôi nei mak ning Jesu kanei.

2) Jesu kanṭhōipui lai seng thi ṭit mak ning,
Thi nasik kahneng asip jo tâk innum,
Ni Rōngpu’n kei anṭangpui jo eng inchu,
Ṭōn rakip Rōngpu’n kei hme amasō sik.

3) Ṭimre leh he’n kei andahlon eng innum,
Kase, kahong na rakip Jesûn ahmu;
Ṭit na ram rakip hei re amanke sik,
Rōngpu Jesu om jârra kamông sik eng.

4) Tuipui kalên leh ralei hmun rakip ah,
Vâng pêk sik ah Jesûn kei abêk le-le;
Amasôn nasik ka rathla’n rangei roh,
Jesûn nasena hlam amanthei sik che.
