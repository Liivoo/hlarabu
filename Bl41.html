41. HALLELUJAH! HE IS RISEN (S.S.157)

“He is not here, for He is risen, as He said.” (Matt.28:6)

P.P.B.Doh is GPHILLIP P. BLISS

1) Inpâk ruh Ama kathōi jo, marvân ram ah kake jo,
Inreng nasik seng ator jo, amanpâk na hla ansak;
Kathōi sōt jo, kathōi sōt jo, thina om nôk mak jo ning,
Kathōi sōt jo, kathōi sōt jo, thina om nôk mak jo ning.

2) Manpâk ruh Ama kathōi jo, ni hlam akei pêk sik me,
Rathla Intheng atir pêk me, eini sik ahri pêk me;
Kathōi sōt jo, kathōi sōt jo, niset amantheng sō jo,
Kathōi sōt jo, kathōi sōt jo, niset amantheng sō jo.

3) Manpâk ruh Ama kathōi jo, thina tûr amansep jo,
Christa rōk thōisōt na kaeh, thlân ah inphut kathōi jo;
Kathōi sōt jo, kathōi sōt jo, Rōngpu kha la kajōng sik,
Kathōi sōt jo, kathōi sōt jo, Rōngpu kha la kajōng sik.
