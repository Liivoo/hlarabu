171. GO, LABOUR ON! (S.S.815)

“…go work today in my vineyard.” (Matt.21:28)

HORATIUS BONAR Doh is EbORLANDO GIBBONS

1) Se roh, na hrât lai hin tlo roh,
Nipa malung tlai nasik ah;
Rōngpu Jesûn alei tlo kha,
Khaba ni taksen mo kati;
Khaba ni taksen mo kati.

2) Se roh, tlo roh, la tluk mak ning,
Na hring nasik nachen sik hlam;
Rōngpu manpâk na teilo chu,
Mihring manpâk na jong mak roh;
Mihring manpâk na jong mak roh.

3) Se roh, tlo roh, pha la kaom,
Na malung inpâk nasik ah;
Alungset ah amanpâk che,
Na ratha kha bang bōi mak ning;
Na ratha kha bang bōi mak ning.

4) Tlo inna inpâk pui ṭhak roh,
Tlo hrât hi ê-na sik kaeh;
Hmōi kaṭhōi kare nan thei sik,
Rangei ruh! Kei kajōng jo eng!
Rangei ruh! Kei kajōng jo eng!
