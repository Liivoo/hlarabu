284. ALL THIS FOR ME (S.S.613)

“…bearing His cross, went forth…where they crucified Him.” (Jn.19:17)

ELIZA E. HEWITT Doh is C JOHN R. SWENEY

1) Ani’n Tlensōtpa navâr na,
Narathla leh nalungset na;
Nalungset na hi inlak eh!
Akheng ah kei sik jâr kaeh.

Ani’n Tlensōtpa inlak eh!
Akalōng kei sik ah aphe;
Hring na, dam na aheitik ah,
Oh! Akheng ah kei sik kaeh.

2) Na mihei hûkmanhring nasik,
Nade ah na kalōng natôk;
Gethsemane ah khōdeng nathei,
Akheng ah kei sik jâr kaeh.

3) Nalu rahling in asûn ah,
Ani hûkmanhring thisen khan;
Calvary cross inlak tak kha,
Akheng ah kei sik jâr kaeh.

4) Na malungset na alen je,
Na marvân karōi leh neinâk;
Kumsôt ah lungset mankase,
Akheng ah kei sik jâr kaeh.
