312. TRUST ON (S.S.692)

“I will trust and not be afraid.” (Isai.12:2)

ELIZA A. WALKER Doh is GEORGE C. STEBBINS

1) Hlam rin ruh taksen kanei,
Khōdeng nânthei jônnum;
Tor na nanta kachang sik,
Rōngpu’n nan re ade.

Hlam rin, taksen, inreng atlung jônnum,
Hlam rin, taksen, tor na kha kahnai jo.

2) Hlam rin ruh setna kanei,
Mathlêm na kha kahnai;
Hringkhō sûng ah mathlêm na,
Amân amantheng sik.

3) Rōngpu! Kahrât Chunghnungpên!
Ni’n ngam pui leh ni ṭhōn;
Hlam rin ruh takkasen hên,
Anchai ning katlung ah.
