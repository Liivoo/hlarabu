221. JESUS ONLY (S.S.711)

“They saw no man, save Jesus only.” (Matt.17:8)

HATTIE M. CONREY Doh is Ab ROBERT LOWRY

1) Kasûm in kei amakhûnum, kabing rōk ah kase’num,
Lungdong nân akatôm innum inpâk nasik khat kanei;
Jesu rōk kha kanei inchu marvân in amakhûnum,
Kani nêk Ama kavâr jôk, Bethlehem ârsi kaeh.

2) Ka ralei khōhme hlam hro lai inreng nân abôm innum,
Hringkhō sik reipâr kajōng lai rei kathleng rahling kahmu;
Jesu rōk kha kanei inchu chitheilo rei kân hmu jo,
Amachu Sharôn reipâr leh tuichung Lily pâr kaeh.

3) Karalei hringkhō tlang pha leh lungset hei seng kahmu sik,
Nunchen marō kamandon ah inreng kasûm in abôm;
Jesu rōk kha kanei inchu Amân kei a-om pui sik,
Hmu phâk lo marvân Tirle hên kei ani’npâk pui sik eng.

4) Lûrna khōpui katlung tak leh kot khâr ah kên kahngâk lai,
Jesu rahming kamatûn chu Sana kot pui inhlang sik;
Kei ankârra katlâk pha leh Tirle Intheng rup hei leh,
Reng leh Rōngpu Chunghnung Jesu! Amarōk kamanpâk sik.
