5. THE GATE AJAR FOR ME (S.S.372)

“The gates of it shall not be shut at all by day.” (Rev.21:25)

LYDIA BAXTERDoh is BbSILAS JONES VAIL

1) Kot pui inhlang kalên kaom,
Kot kahōi marit tak kha;
Khava Cross kahōi tak ah khan,
Rōngpu lungset na kaom.

Oh! Lungset na marit tak kha,
Kot kha kei sik inhlang tâk mo?
Kei sik! Kei sik! Kei sik inhlang tâk mo?
2) Mi rakip sik mahlang kaeh,
Hring na kajong hei sik ah;
Haisa leh sara hei sik âm,
Vânhnōi mi rakip sik ah.

3) Lungset kot pui inhlang lai hin,
Nân re hei de ṭhak û-na;
Bâng lo jên ah nitor pha leh,
Lênna lukhu nihmu sik.

4) Ralei chung ah inreng na hi,
Tui rekhat ni tlung tak leh;
Ni hringkhōsei abâng innum,
Marvân lên lukhu kaom.
