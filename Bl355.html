355. COME UNTO ME

“Come unto Me…and I will give you rest.” (Matt.11:28)

C.P.J. Doh is C CHARLES P. JONES

1) Tlensōtpân khōdeng kathei hei seng abêk,
Phur kahrik karôn ah kachâm hei hong ruh;
Insûm mak ruh, hong ruh, châm man hngat chei rong,
Nan rôn kahrik rakip hei rôn ruh, ati.

Kahneng hong ruh, châm man hngat chei rong,
Karing-ke inrôn ruh, Ka ṭong rangei ruh;
Keichu kanun innêm, Karatha kahrât,
Rôn kahōi karing-ke, Rôn kajâng kaphur.

2) Namalung hōi lo kalôn – invai che mo?
Naphur amanhrik mo malung hōi mak in?
Namalung ah khōdeng thei leh ṭit na kha,
Jesu hneng hei rôn roh, aman mông sik che.

3) Set inthim in sûmphai kapō amakhu,
Set khur hmeipun sûng ah khōdeng kathei ah;
Inreng tak ah khōdeng thei kade hei kha,
Ni Rōngpu’n ati, “Châm inhngat sik hong ruh.”
