204. COME, GREAT DELIVERER COME (S.S.462)

“…Thou art my help and my deliverer….” (Ps.40:17)

FANNY J. CROSBY Doh is G WILLIAM H. DOANE

1) Rōngpu kaṭa na ei rangei pêk roh,
Tlensōt kalên jōng roh;
Rathla inhmang sik in ahngâk ṭhak che,
Tlensōt kalên jōng roh.

Ram kahla, hme kadai ah invai eng,
Inn ah inphut ram kahla tak;
Ajōng ṭhōi roh na âng sûng ah sik ah,
Tlensōt kalên jōng roh.

2) Sûn leh jân ah om nasik nei mak eng,
Tlensōt kalên jōng roh;
Kahong jôn che hring leh dam nasik ah,
Tlensōt kalên jōng roh.

3) Kalôn lena ânthopui ṭhak inna,
Tlensōt kalên jōng roh;
Na lungset hmai katlai hmu nasik ah,
Tlensōt kalên jōng roh.

4) Ka setna inphut adahlon mak roh,
Tlensōt kalên jōng roh;
Ka derna hi arahlang pêk inna,
Tlensōt kalên jōng roh.
