76. THERE'S A HILL LONE AND GREY (R.S.82)

“The place…called Calvary…there they crucified Him.” (Lk.23:33)

ROBERT CARRADINEDoh is EbJOHN B. BRYANT

1) Tlâng kahōi tak kaom, ram kahla tak ah khan,
Tuipui re ram kahōi tak ah khan;
Rahming lûrna hmun na Rōngpu kha kathi jo,
Kei leh nang leh vânhnōi sik kaeh.

Ade kamandon ah kahmit rathli kasōk,
Atlâng kha hei lai ênnum ka en;
Asir ah khan Jesu inreng tak ah akathi,
Kei kapō kaset sen sōt nasik.

2) Malung inreng pumma cross kahrik an mandom,
Rahling lukhu pâr ankhu lai khan;
Cross ah anhem bêk ah mipui nuisō sik ah,
Khava tlâng kahōi ah anmakhei.

3) Ama anmanuisō khang ah thi lai khan,
Aṭhōn hei inreng ah kaṭap hei;
Ama andahlon jo, nat rakip in abôm,
Tâkkha khava tlâng ah andahlon.

4) Inthim in amanhle hlungpui um kakîk khōi,
Inreng na rasa anmanhret ah;
Rōngpu kare kaeh, thina intak ade,
Mi rakip set rôn intheng nasik.

5) Kani um en ngam mak, ralei lingnu innôk,
An intlenpa anhem bêk jârra;
En ruh! Thina inphut Kemôngte set karôn,
Thlân khur ah inphut kathōi sōt jo.
