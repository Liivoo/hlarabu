53. ROCK OF AGES! (S.S.237)

“…the Lord…is an everlasting Rock.” (Isai.26:4)(RSV)

AUGUSTUS M. TOPLADYDoh is BbTHOMAS HASTINGS

1) Hlungpui inhmang thei lo jên,
Athup roh na hnōi ah khan;
Rōngpu kasei sunna bi,
Akasōk thisen vâng khan;
Ka setna asôp pêk roh,
Kantheng mak na mantheng roh.

2) Kahmit rathli sōk na leh,
Ka ṭōn tlo na rakip ah;
Rōngpu ṭongpêk jui nasik,
Kator mak jârra Rōngpu!
Kaset rôn rakip hei hi,
Amantheng pêk roh Rōngpu!

3) Rōngpu! Nahneng jôn nasik,
Kên heite nei tor mak eng;
Jesu Cross rōk mandon rong,
Kaset inthleng sō nasik;
Jesûn nalungset manchu,
Kei set in athâ sik jo.

4) Kên hing ah hin kamandon,
Vânhnōi ralei atlang leh;
Chungkhōpui katlung tak leh,
Khan ṭong natan pha tak leh;
Hlungpui inhmang theilo jên,
Athup roh na hnōi ah khan.
