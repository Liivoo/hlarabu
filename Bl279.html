279. LOOKING THIS WAY (S.S.1017)

“Neither shall there be any more pain.” (Rev.21:4)

J.W.V. Doh is G JUDSON W. VAN DEVENTER

1) Tuire ram kahōi ah marvân mi,
Khōvâr leh kani vâr kapōah;
Lungjing, lungkham, lungdong om loah,
Anhei en me, anhei thlir ṭhak me.

Hinting an-en ah anthlir ṭhak me,
Rangei hên anthlir, anhngâk ṭhak me;
Khōvâr leh kani vâr in-êm na,
Rangei hên marvân ah anhngâk me.

2) Marvân ram kahōi ah nu leh pa’n,
Ansa rangei re manke sik ah;
Rakōng kator mihei an kahnot,
Anhngâk ṭhak me rakōng ke hmun ah.

3) Sopui rangei hei kase jo hei,
Akase sik mi ralak an hngâk;
Kani vâr leh kavur kapōah,
Thōi ah mi kase sik anhngâk hei.

4) Tlensōtpa Jesu jing-Vârpa khan,
Anhmang sa hei kha ajōng sōt ah;
Abêkna me kare rangei ruh,
“Hong ruh set ah anhmang hei” ati.
