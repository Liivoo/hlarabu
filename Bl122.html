122. WHEN THE BRIDEGROOM COMES (S.S.185)

“Behold! The Bridegroom cometh.” (Matt.25:6)

E.R. LATTA Doh is EWILLIAM J. KIRKPATRICK

1) Nithâi hmei lei kavâr sik mo Rōngpu jōng pha leh?
Nithâi hmei kavâr ṭhak sik mo Rongpu jōng pha leh?
Khava jân kalûr ah khan,
Nithâi hmei kavâr sik mo Rōngpu jōng pha leh?

Inrin ṭhak ruh! Inrin ṭhak ruh!
Inrin ṭhak ruh! Rongpu jōng pha leh.

2) Nilei ratō thei nâk sik mo Rōngpu jōng pha leh?
Inpâk na hla nithei sik mo Rōngpu jōng pha leh?
Khava jân kalûr ah khan,
Nithâi hmei kavâr sik mo Rōngpu jōng pha leh?

3) Inreng na kani’m kachang thei Rōngpu jōng pha leh,
Thâi tui nilei tlâksam inchu Rōngpu jōng pha leh;
Khava jân kalûr ah khan,
Nithâi hmei kavâr sik mo Rōngpu jōng pha leh?

4) Lentluk êt rih inrang tak ah Rōngpu jōng pha leh,
Inṭhen na kaom khâk sik mo Rōngpu jōng pha leh?
Khava jân kalûr ah khan,
Nithâi hmei kavâr sik mo Rōngpu jōng pha leh?
