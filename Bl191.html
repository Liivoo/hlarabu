191. ONLY A FEW MORE YEARS

“…though now for a season…ye are in heaviness….” (1 Pet.1:6)

P.P.B. Doh is Ab PHILIP P. BLISS

1) Kum kathleng bei sik jo,
Lung hlo sik katlôm jo;
Manui na pha katlang sik jo,
Derna pha katlôm jo.

2) Ṭhena hong katlang jo,
Khō thlir pha kadong jo,
Inthei mak jo ning inpâk hla,
Inthla om mak jo ning.

3) Kumsôt ah om nasik,
Hmun pui niphâk sik jo;
Hla kahōi tak kha kumsôt ah,
Are-ah nisak sik.
