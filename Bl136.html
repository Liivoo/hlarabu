136. THE CHILD OF A KING (S.S.946)

“Heirs of the Kingdom.” (Jas.2:5)

HATTIE E. BUELL Doh is FJOHN B. SUMNER

1) Kapa kapō akanei mi om mak,
Sana, dangka leh haisa maṭhi seng;
Vânhnōi nei seng Ama kut ah kaom sō,
Ama nei hri thei loah a-inn kha kasip.

Reng sapa kachang eng,
Reng sa kachang eng;
Jesûn a-en ṭhak eng,
Reng sapa kachang eng.

2) Mihei hring na Pathen Sapa kaeh,
Khōdeng kathei ah ralei ah kajōng;
Tûnchu marvân ram kahōi ahlo jo,
Vōikhat chu marvân ah inn ala pêk sik.

3) Ralei ah inn hichu ka inn chang mak,
Kei inn sik inn marit inkhat kaom;
Ka rahming inzet jo khan ah sik ah,
Reng neinâk rakip kha kachen sō sik jo.

4) Ralei chung ah kei vōikhat kalêng eng,
Jōng inte eng sara kachang tak ah;
Tâk lungset na inphut ahûksōt jo,
Akahrâtpên Reng sapa kachang jo eng.
