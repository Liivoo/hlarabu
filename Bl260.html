260. IT IS WELL (S.S.901)

“He hath delivered my soul in peace from the battle….” (Ps.55:18)

H.G. SPAFFORD Doh is Db PHILIP P. BLISS

1) Tuidung kapō mông nân kahlam ajui lai,
Lungdong tui-ôngpui ah inhêr;
Vângset leh vângṭhat de sik nalei manthei,
Kaṭha jo karathla sik ah khan.

Kaṭha jo kei sik ah,
Kaṭha jo karathla sik ah khan.

2) Seitân vai rôn ah malung inreng kade,
Ṭong kaṭha khan aṭhōi ṭhak eng;
Christa! Kei sik hrât na leh malung mông na,
Athisen kei sik ah kalōng jo.

3) Ka setna rakip Rōngpu’n arôn pêk jo,
Kaset cross chung ah mathlang pêk;
Tûn bân kei sik ah kaset kabōi sō jo,
Manpâk ruh malung ah Christa kha.
