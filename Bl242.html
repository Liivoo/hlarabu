242. IN EDEN ABOVE

“…new Jerusalem…prepared as a bride…for her husband.” (Rev.21:2)

Doh is G

1) Marvân khōpui ther kahōi kaom,
Tlensōt sa seng khan intō sik me;
Lungdong na rakip om mak jo ning,
Marvân khōpui ther inn ni tlung leh.

Oh! Eden! Khōpui ther!
Ahōi je hri tor lo;
Kumsôt ah eini kaom sik me,
Ni Rōngpu khōpui syem ther ah khin.

2) Hring na marvân khōpui sûng ah khin,
Ralei chung hi nidahlon tak leh;
Mi’ntheng hma lei kase hei kha leh,
Chung Pathen hneng kaom sap sik me.

3) Khan mi’ntheng katam tak kaom hei,
Martyr hma lei kase hei kha leh;
Tlensōt sa naipang kaom sa leh,
Marvân khōpui ah tûn hla ansak.

4) Marvân khōpui sana ra-âng rōk,
Khōpui vâr kha Kemôngte kaeh;
Mi’ntheng tlensa hei hla sak na inn,
Khōpui kalûr hmu sik kahngâk hla.
