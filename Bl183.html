183. STAND UP FOR JESUS (S.S.680)

“I will declare what He hath done for my soul.” (Ps.66:16)

G. DUFFIRLD Doh is BbG.J. WEBB

1) Ngir inhnet ruh Jesu sik, nang ni cross remi hei,
Pōnlek kadom mansâng ruh, châmna inhmang mak ning;
Nan re tlo hmun rakip ah Amân abôm sik chei,
Ni re rakip tor hma seng Jesûn ahngâk ṭhak sik.

2) Ngir inhnet ruh Jesu sik, pêngkun anhring pha leh,
Ni re kahrâtpên hei khâm tuning nitor sik hei;
Nang ni Pathen sepei seng, hma ting jôn êt û-na,
Ngir ruh, rathla der û-na, lung ngam na nannei sik.

3) Ngir inhnet ruh Jesu sik, Aratha hrât song ruh,
Taksa hrât na song mak ruh, hnōise tlo jên mak ruh;
Pathen pho manchang û-na, derna manhrât û-na,
Insuk set na om mak ning, heite ra-ôt mak ruh.

4) Ngir inhnet ruh Jesu sik, re tlo sôt mak jo min,
Tuning re ṭong ni thei sik, nôk khat ah tor na ṭong;
Re kator pân re tor man lên lukhu ahmu sik,
Ni Rōngpu leh kumsôt ah kalêng sap sik jo me.
