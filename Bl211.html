211. SEEKING FOR ME! (S.S.706)

“…I…will both search My sheep, and seek them out.” (Ezek.34:11)

A.N. Doh is G E.E. HASTY

1) Kan Tlensōtpa Jesu Bethlehem ah,
Inzak tak ah ranthleng ah lei inte;
Oh! Heituk ah kei alungset eng mo!
Kei alei jong sōt jo;
Kei ajong sōt jo eng, kei sik alei thi pêk,
Arahming heitik ah lûr ṭhak rasoh;
Kei ajong sōt jo eng.

2) Kan Tlensōtpân Calvary thing chung ah,
Kabat rakip alei chôi sō pêk jo;
Heituk ah kei alei lungset eng mo!
Kei alei jong sōt jo;
Kei ajong sōt jo eng, kei sik alei thi pêk,
Arahming heitik ah lûr thak rasoh;
Kei ajong sōt jo eng.

3) Anga-ngai ah ni Rōngpu inthleng mak,
Pha kasôt tak kalei tlân sōt jônnum;
Lungset tak ah kei ajong sōt jo eng,
[Kei ani bêk ṭhak eng] x3
Lungset tak ah karathla ahûksōt,
Kei ani bêk ṭhak eng.

4) Marvân ânphut Jesu kajōng nôk sik,
Alungset ṭongtep sa dungjui ah khan;
Oh! Jesu ajōng ṭum kha kahmu sik,
[Kei sik la kajōng sik] x3
Oh! Jesu ajōng ṭum kha kahmu sik,
Kei sik la kajōng sik.
