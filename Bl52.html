52. JOY COMETH IN THE MORNING (S.S.717)

“Weeping may endure for a night, but joy cometh in the morning.” (Ps.30:5)

M.M. WIENLANDDoh is EbE.S. LORRENZ

1) Marvân ram kajôn sik hei tho jo ruh,
Jing khōvâr kajōng jo;
Kaṭha tak ah len tluk êt jo hei ruh,
A marvân inn ah sik.

Jing khōvâr hōi kajōng jo!
Jing khōvâr hōi kajōng jo!
Ka hringkhō sik amanvâr jo tûnchu,
Kei sik Immanuel!

2) Mi’ntheng hei nân re ṭit kha kabâng jo,
Inpâk ruh, hōi jo ruh;
Nan ṭana hmit rathli kahi sik jo,
Tûnchu inpâk jo ruh.

3) Inpâk ruh jân inthim kabâng sik jo!
Nang ni vâng pêk nasik;
Asa seng jōng kaṭhōi intûp sik ah,
Kalûr tak ah kajōng.

4) Û leh nai ṭimre le hei inhôr ruh,
Hōina khōvâr kajōng;
Pathen in nan hmit rathli ahni sik,
Inpâk ruh tuning chu.
