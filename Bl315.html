315. RESCUE THE PERISHING (S.S.814)

“Go out into the highways and hedges…compel them to come in.” (Lk.14:23)

FANNY J. CROSBY Doh is Bb WILLIAM H. DOANE

1) Anhmang hei vajong roh, hûk roh kathi sik,
Set khur ah kaom hei kha keisōt roh;
Akaṭhe manse roh, akatlu phōn roh,
Hûksōt Pa Jesu ṭong manthei hei roh.

Akathi sik hûk roh,
Anhmang vajong roh;
Lungset kathem Jesûn,
Ahûk nger sik.

2) Anmani’n heite ah amandon man’um,
Ahngâk ṭhak hei malung innêm tak ah;
Kare innêm tak ah ala bêk ṭhak hei,
An taksen inchu alo nger sik hei.

3) Mihring hei malung sûng setna kasip jo,
Khava jârra Rōngpu kha hong kaṭum;
Alungset kut vâng khan akei mathōi hei,
Akathisa he’m aman hring nôk hei.

4) Anhmang kajong sōt sik kha na ṭōn kaeh,
Rōngpu’n ratha hrât apêk sik che nang;
Rathla inhmang sik mi hingja kaom hei,
Tlensōtpa lungset ṭong manthei hei roh.
