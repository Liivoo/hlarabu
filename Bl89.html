89. COME HOLY SPIRIT! (S.S.188)

“He hath given us His Spirit.” (1 Jn.4:13)

ISAAC WATTS Doh is GST. AGNES

1) Jōng roh antheng marvân Vaṭhu,
Kân chung ah jōng chōng roh;
Kan malung ah Pathen! Nang in,
Lungset na jōng da roh.

2) Ralei malung inreng hmun ah,
Kan malung kaom ṭhak;
Kum khō hring na ram kahōi kha,
Mandon rong ti mak jo.

3) Kan hla sak um ṭhat chôm mak jo,
Ṭhat sōt na om mak jo;
Hōina hla khâm kachim jo eng,
Ka taksen inchên mak.

4) Eh! Ka Pathen! Kei heitik chen,
Hing kaom sik eng mo?
Nalungset na eng tei tor lo,
Hing hrep lum nalo eng!

5) Jōng roh antheng marvân Vaṭhu,
Kan sûng ah jōng om roh;
Malung intheng dik na khum roh,
Kumsôt ah om thang roh.
