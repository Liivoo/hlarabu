40. GOD IS HERE AND THAT TO BLESS US (S.S.307)

“The Lord will bless His people.” (Ps.29:11)

JAMES M. BLACKDoh is AbJOHN R. SWENEY

1) Pathen in Arathla Intheng,
Eini ajōng tir pêk me;
En ruh! Marvân jōng innîm jo,
Sa kavôr sik Pa’n ahngâk.

Jōng pe roh Rōngpu kander che,
Mahamṭhat ajōng pe roh;
Kan hngâk ṭhak che, kan hngâk ṭhak che,
Kan lung ri jōng en ṭhak roh.

2) Pathen malungset na nihmu,
Hiva ni karōi hmun ah;
Ni malung manther sik kaeh,
Alungset ni hmu jârra.

3) Ni Pathen khi taksen êt rih,
Ni rangei rôn manhlut rih;
Lungset nahi ni malung ah,
Avâr ṭhak thei nasik ah.

4) Rōngpu! Kader na rangei roh,
Hlam rin ah kader na hi;
Nalungset na hmun ah inphut,
Mahamṭhat ajōng pe roh.
