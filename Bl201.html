201. ONWARD, CHRISTIAN SOLDIERS! (S.S.706)

“Be strong and of good courage.” (Deut.31:6)

S. BARING - GOULD Doh is F ARTHUR SULLIVAN

1) Se ruh Christian remi, se ruh re kapō,
Re hlam kakei Jesu kha en ṭhak û-na;
Christa re kahrât khan eini aṭhōi me,
Re hmun hma ting nam ri, ni re nitor sik.

Christian remi se ruh, re kapō tak ah,
Ni hma kakeipa Jesu kha en ṭhak ruh.

2) Jesu rahming rōk âm Seitân katlân hei,
Christian remi eini’n torna ninei sik;
Arahming anthei pha Seitân katlân hei,
Inhnak tak ah ôn ruh, ni Rōngpu rahming.

3) Hrât ruh Christian remi, se rih Christa sik,
Rōngpu Christa sik ah tor chen ah de rih;
Seitân re tor nasik de rih, hrât chen ah,
Malung khat rōk rôn ruh, Seitân tor nasik.

4) Ralei lên lukhu leh hrât na inhmang sik,
Christa bêkrup rakip chu inhmang mak ning;
Bêkrup re de Seitân kha tor nger nasik,
Christa ṭongtep pêk vâng eini’n nitor sik.

5) Hong hlut ruh mipui hei, hong ruh inpâk ah,
Ni kheng ah injôk inna hma ting jôn rih;
Reng Christa kajana hla kha sak êt rih,
Marvân leh ralei mi’n inpâk hla ansak.
