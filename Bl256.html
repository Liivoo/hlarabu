256. YOU MAY HAVE THE JOY BELL (R.S.79)

“Blessed is…that know the joyful sound….” (Ps.89:15)

J.E. RUARK Doh is Ab WILLIAM J. KIRKPATRICK

1) Inpâk na dâr vō roh namalung sûng ah,
Malung mông na Rōngpu’n apêk ṭhak sik che;
Hlam kasin ah se inna Rōngpu inṭhōipui ṭhak roh,
Vō roh inpâk na dâr namalung sûng ah.

Vō roh namalung sûng ah, nasena hmun rakip ah,
Rōngpu’n aṭhōi ṭhak sik che, nasena hmun rakip ah;
Hring na dâr kha vō roh, namalung sûng ah.

2) Na hringkhō dam lai seng hri roh Jesu sik,
Nang inpe roh natlo theina ṭōn ting khan;
Set kanei hei kha malung intheng tak ah sen hei roh,
Namalung sûng ah hōina dâr inhring sik.

3) Jesûn ani lungset jârra inpâk eng,
Alungset na vânhnōi rakip ah inthang;
Kan Tlensōtpa ka Rōngpu’n anitin ah aṭhōi eng,
Hring na dâr kha vō roh namalung sûng ah.

4) Na hring na Rōngpu ṭong nitin ah hri roh,
Hring na rathla apêk sik che ni Rōngpu’n;
Naset asôp pêk sik che Pathen na taksen inchu,
Hring na dâr kha vō roh malung inpâk ah.
