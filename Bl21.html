21. JUST AS I AM (S.S.473)

“Behold! The Lamb of God.” (Jn.1:29)

CHARLOTTE ELLIOTT Doh is EbW.B. BRADBURY


1) Heite kanei mak kei sara,
Kei jârra na thisen kalōng;
Nahneng ah sik ah nabêk eng,
Eh! Jesu! Tûn kei kahong eng.

2) Heite kanei mak kei sara,
Ka rangei rakip hmu nasik;
Nahneng kahong jōn jo Jesu!
Tuning ah hin kei kahong eng.

3) Heite kanei mak kei sara,
Ei mantheng inna alo roh;
Na ṭongkam hei kataksen jo,
Jesu! Nahneng kahong hnai jo.

4) Kei kapō lungset inhret mak,
Kanei rakip leh eilo roh;
Kapum ah Nangta chang jo rong,
Tiah tûn kên kahong jōn che.
