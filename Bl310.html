310. THE CHURCH’S ONE FOUNDATION (S.S.228)

“Christ is the head of the Church.” (Ephe.5:23)

C. WESLEY Doh is Eb J. P. HOLBROOK

1) Bêkrup inn hmun inkhat rōk, Christa hi a Rōngpu,
Ṭong leh tui vâng ather ah Ama kut syem kaeh;
Ahmōi marit tak sik ah ajōng jong sōt kaeh,
Khava sik ah jōng kathi, Athi vâng arachôk.

2) Phung rakip mi abêk hei, ralei chung rakip ah,
Setna inphut hûksōt ah pumkhat rōk chang nasik;
Vâng rakip ajōng pêk, athlai intheng kasa sik,
Marit na rakip apêk, inhôr na inkhat rōk.

3) Inreng leh khōdeng theina ṭit sik achang jônnum, Lung mông na khan ahngâk ṭhak, lurna khan ahngâk ṭhak;
An ṭana kare rasa heituk mo kasôt sik?
Hmit rathli kahi bei sik, inpâk hla ansak sik.

4) Ralei chung ah Bêkrup hi Pa, Sa, Rathla Intheng!
Inthum ah Inkhat Pathen, pumkhat rōk kachang hei;
Khan ah ânchun hei seng kha heituk kahōi hei mo!
Malung dai leh nun hōina Rōngpu’n apêk jo me.
