134. THE NINETY AND NINE (S.S.97)

“…I have found My sheep that was lost.” (Lk.15:6)

ELIZABETH C. CLEPHANE Doh is AbIRA D. SANKEY

1) Rōngpu! Kemôngte hei kakip him ah,
Kemông rông sûng ah kaom hei;
An kârra inkhat chu tlâng ah inhmang jo,
Sana kekot anphut kahla jo;
Sa kahrâng hei kârra ratu inthûk ah,
Akakhêmpa ah inphut kahla tak jo;
Akakhêmpa inphut ka hla jo.

2) Rōngpu! Sômkō leh kō hin la kaom hei hi,
Nang sik la tluk mak mo?
Ankakhêmpa’n ahri akalôn sōt ah,
Hlam avân vai inkhat hi keita;
A hlam heituk antak ah antên jônnum,
Kakemông kajong sōt kase nger sik eng;
Kakemông kajōng kase nger sik eng.

3) Intlensōt na je tutên manhret mak hei,
Rakan sik tui inthûk kaom;
Heituk anthim jônnum Rōngpu kha kase ah,
Kemông inhmang kha ava jong sōt;
Ram hâng thōi ah akemông rasa athei,
Khōdeng kathei tak ah kathi sik jo kha;
Khōdeng kathei tak ah kathi sik kha.

4) Lôn insâng ratu thōi hlung kare kârra,
Radai, khō tui, kasûm kârra akajong va kase;
Rông sûng ah aheipom manhlût jo,
Marvân kekot ah inphut rasa kha inthei;
Inpâk ruh Rōngpu’n akemông anhmu jo,
Inpâk ruh Rōngpu’n kemông anhmu jo.
