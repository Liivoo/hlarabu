222. OUR GOD IS MARCHING ON (S.S.697)

“…the Lord strong and mighty…in battle.” (Ps.24:8)

JULIA WARD HOWE Doh is C WILLIAM STEFFE

1) Kalûr tak ah Rōngpu ajōng kha kahmit in ahmu jo,
Hnuk nat na vomhrui theibur kha ake vâng arachi jo;
Khō inphe kapō chem jâm kha Amân aradok sōt jo,
Ni Pathen re kakōn.

Lûr ṭhak rasoh Hallelujah! Lûr ṭhak rasoh Hallelujah!
Lûr ṭhak rasoh Hallelujah! Pathen re kakōn jo.

2) Tei tor lo sepei re kakap ka-en Rōngpu ngir kahmu,
Anmani’n maichâm inkhat Rōngpu âng sûng ah anlei da;
Ni Rōngpu ṭongtan kadik takkha khōvâr ah inlâr jo,
Ni Pathen re kakōn.

3) Hnungting inle nôk mak nasik Rōngpu pêngkun inhring jo,
Rōngpu ṭongtan na hmun ah mi rakip malung amathêp;
Malung in amasôn tor nasik inrang ah ke thon roh,
Pathen re kakōn jo.

4) Ram kahla tak ah Christa inte, Lily rei kapōah,
Ni Rōngpu vâr ra âng ah nang leh kei hme hi inthleng sik;
Mi lak hei hûksōt nasik ah eini inpe sōt jo rih,
Pathen re kōn lai hin.
