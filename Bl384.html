384. THINGRAKE ANPHUT ṬONG SARI (M.C.K.29)

“…the place…called Calvary, there they crucified Him….” (Lk.23:33)

M. CHUNGRENGKHUP KOM Doh is G 21/03/2018

1) Jesu’n, cross chung ah anhêm mankop lai,
Mi kaset hei sik Apa hneng ader;
Heimo antatlo anmanhret mannum,
“Apa! Rangei mandam hei roh.”

2) Ṭōnse katlo mi khan ni Rōngpu kha,
Pathen Sapa kaeh phôngsōt na anei;
Jesu’n ama hneng intêp ah ahri,
“Tuning Paradise ah kaom sap sik me.”

3) Thingrake anphut Jesu’n anu hneng,
Sônle “Ama kha nasapa kaeh”
Sônle alungsetpên hneng khan ahri,
“Amanu kha nanu kaeh.”

4) Thingrake anphut ni Rōngpu Jesu’n,
Rasa inhnak tak ah ka-ông ah ahri;
“Eloi! Eloi! Lama Sabachthani”
Oh! “Ka Pathen heisamo andahlôn eng.”

5) Ṭōnpui hei rakip pui katluk sêt ah,
Inchai jo ti kha Jesu’n amanhret;
Ṭong Intheng tahri hōi tlung thei nasik,
Jesu’n “Katui kahre ati.”

6) Khava hmun ah khan vomhrui ju kasep,
Kasip thlengkam vâng ah pōnhlom anmanche;
Ahner katlung ankadom mansâng ah,
Jesu’n amajôp ah “Inchai jo” ati.

7) Bêk-Inn ah pinden pōnzâr inkhei kha,
Achung anphut aṭhōi her hni-ah inhler jo;
Jesu’n inhnak ah a-ôn ah “Apa,
Nakut ah Karathla kapêk che” ati.

# Tune for 2nd 4th 6th & 7th Words from the Cross are similar, to be sung as Chorus #
