370. YESTERDAY, TODAY, FOREVER (H.O.P.133)

“Jesus Christ the same yesterday…today and for ever.” (Heb.13:8)

ALBERT B. SIMPSON Doh is Ab JAMES H. BURKE

1) Oh! Heituk ah ṭong kahōi taksen sik arahōi,
Janing um, tuning um, aheitik ah sik Jesu;
Set hûksōt leh akadam sik Amân amadu,
Lungmông leh lung ngam kapê Ama lûr ṭhak rasoh.

Janing um, tuning um, heitik Jesu anga-ngai,
Hei rakip anthleng jo innum Ama inthleng mak;
Ama lûr rasoh, Ama lûr rasoh,
Hei rakip anthleng jo innum Ama inthleng mak.

2) Set karon leh anhmang hei kajong sōt ṭhōn kaṭha,
Set karon rakip he’n Ake kung ah inkûn hei;
Amân aleiti, “Se jo roh, set nôk mak jo roh”
Khava rangei mandam ṭong kha nahneng um ahri.

3) Peter in-ngei ’dampa nang hmu sik insûm mak roh,
Thomas kapō ah na malung kha amanvâr sik;
Sônle hei lungset leh marit ah karôn Ama,
Lungset ah nang abêk che Ama hneng in-ngai roh.

4) Tui-ôngpui chung ah rekhat ting kalôn ni Rōngpu’n,
Ni ratu, thlipui khōpui rakip Amân akhâm;
Gethsemane ah inreng khōdeng kathei Rōngpu’n,
Eini set na rakip kha Amân arôn pêk me.

Translated By: N. Sumneikhup Mangte, Evant. KRBCA, Manipur.
