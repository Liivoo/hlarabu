28. SAVED BY GRACE (S.S.978)

“By grace ye are saved.” (Eph.2:5)

FANNY J. CROSBY Doh is FGEORGE C. STEBBINS

1) Vânhnōi ralei anhmang ning chu,
Hla sak na om nôk mak jo ning;
Haisa inn kahōi sûng ah khan,
Kase’n chu inpâk sik jo eng.

Ama hmai kha ka hmu pha leh,
Amalungset ṭong kahri sik;
Ama hmai kha ka hmu pha leh,
Amalungset ṭong kahri sik.

2) Vōikhat chu la kathi sik me,
Hri thei chang mak asôt – asei;
Tâkkha kamanhret keita seng,
Kaom nasik hmun khat kaom.

3) Kani vâr hi achi ning chu,
Hme hōi – reipâr achi pha leh;
Rōngpu Jesu’n ati sik eng,
“Kaṭha jo hong hlut roh inn ah.”

4) Ka hmeivâr kadet sik ah kha,
Ka hngâk ṭhak sik sûn leh jân ah;
Ni Rōngpu'n kot amahlang sik,
Ka rathla aman hlut sik jo.
