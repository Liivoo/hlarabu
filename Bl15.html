15. A HOME ON HIGH (S.S.957)

“Building of God…not made with hands, eternal in heavens.” (II Cor.5:1)

L.W. MANSFIELD Doh is AbGEORGE C. STEBBINS

1) Kani tlâk na ram kakhe ah,
Sûmtuk kaina khe ah;
Jân arsi vârna kakhe ah,
Inn kahōi tak kanei.

Kut ah sak lo marvân inn kha,
Kei sik mantluk sa jo;
Tlensōt sahei hla sak na kha,
Kei inn sik kha kaeh.

2) Nat leh sat, thina om mak jo,
Kumsôt hring na kaom;
Pha inthleng mak na ram ah khan,
Ka inn sik kha kaeh.

3) Thina khō inthim kakhe ah,
Jân um om mak jo ning;
Malung inpâk bâng mak na khan,
Inn kalûr tak kaom.

4) Ni setna malung inreng hi,
Khin lei kabâng sik jo;
Kumsôt ah hōina ram ah khan,
Rōngpu’n aman om sik.
