214. CALVARY! (S.S.116)

“The place…called Calvary – there they crucified Him.” (Lk.23:33)

W.M.K. DARWOOD Doh is Bb JOHN R. SWENEY

1) Calvary ah Jesu kathi,
Jesu kaeh cross ah kathi;
Cross chung ah khan kei sik kathi,
Kaset rakip amantheng pêk.

Oh! Calvary! Oh! Calvary!
Kei sik thisen lōng na hmun kha,
Oh! Calvary! Oh! Calvary!
Khan ka Rōngpu Jesu kathi.

2) Hlungpui kakōi, inthim kajing,
Cross ah Rōngpu athi lai khan;
Pinden pōnjâr lei inhlang jo,
Marvân kot pui kaphâm inhlang.

3) Oh! Ka Rōngpu! Kei sik jârra,
Na hringkhō na lei tôk sōt jo;
Malung inrim na rakip kha,
Calvary ah na lei de pêk.
