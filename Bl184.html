184. PRAISE YE THE LORD (S.S.215)

“Praise Him according to His excellent greatness” (Ps.150:2)

FANNY J. CROSBY Doh is DbHORATIO R. PALMER

1) Manpâk ruh ni Rōngpu Hosanna! ti ruh,
Manpâk ruh insângpên ah;
Nan malung ah thô ruh, inpâk pui êt ruh,
Phôngsōt ruh Arahming kha;
Injôk êt ruh amarvân hmun ah,
Taksen ah se ruh, alungset na;
Vâr kahōi tak khin nichung ah aṭhōi sik,
Aheitik âm aṭhōi sik me, Akut vâng eini.

Injôk ruh kahōi ah nichung ah pōn kalek û-na,
Inre tak ah se ruh, hla kahōi ah nisak lai seng;
Injôk ruh kahōi ah sum puihlom kha nihma ting kase,
Marvân kahōi Pathen hneng niphâk hma seng.

2) Manpâk êt ruh, Ama ni kumsôt Rengpa!
Lenna rakip Ama hneng;
Manpâk ruh, alungset na hri manthang ruh,
Sak ruh marvân ram katlung;
Injôk ṭhak ruh malung intheng tak ah,
Jesu hrât na vâng se êt û-na;
Châm inhngat na, inpâk na inn ni tlung leh,
Ni’n Tlensōtpa rahming manpâk rih, aheitik ah.
