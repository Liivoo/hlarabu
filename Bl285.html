285. GOD! MOST HIGH (TUNE R.S.439)

“Praise Him according to His excellent greatness” (Ps.150:2)

Doh is Bb

1) Eh! Chunghnungpên Pathen! Kanhong manpâk che,
Akahring Pathen! Hei rakip katlo tor;
Sōk, khōdeng kathei leh mi kaset hei ṭhōn,
Sara, khōdeng kathei, sara, khōdeng kathei;
Sara, khōdeng kathei, Pathen leh Rōngpu!

2) Mihei derna inhlâng Pathen kachang che,
Anta madu, anta rangei napêk hei;
Nasa hei inreng rasa na rangei pêk,
Ṭong dik leh ṭōn kaṭha, ṭong dik leh ṭōn kaṭha;
Ṭong dik leh ṭōn kaṭha katlo na lungset.

3) Mi kalên leh mi kasin hei man hmu roh,
Na ṭong dik leh mipui hei kadōi hei hi;
Tûn pha hûkmanhring na anchen thei nasik,
Heimo kachang sik eng? Heimo kachang sik eng?
Heimo kachang sik eng? ti rasoh – Amen!

4) Phung tin khō inthim sōk anphut inpâk hei,
Chunghnungpên ah Pathen kha lûr ṭhak rasoh;
Mihring anpâk hei chung ah malung mông na,
Hosanna! Hosanna! Hosanna! Hosanna!
Hosanna! Hosanna! tia hla sak ruh.
