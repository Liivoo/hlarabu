63. WHOSOEVER WILL (S.S.389)

“Whosoever will, let him take the water of life freely.” (Rev.22:17)

P.P.B.Doh is DPHILIP P. BLISS

1) Tu achang innum Rōngpu ṭong kaṭha hi,
Vânhnōi ralei chung ah hring manthang êt ruh;
Inpâk na ṭong hi mi rakip hneng hri ruh,
Akahnōm seng hong êt ruh.

Tu achang innum anhri kathei ah,
Hme leh dâm ah se û-na vaman thei ruh;
Hiva hi Pa lungset ah inpe kaeh,
Akahnōm seng hong êt ruh.

2) Akahong hnōm seng kha intâng phit mak ruh,
Tûn hin kot inhlang ahlut theina kaeh;
Hring na hlam kot kha in Rōngpu rōk kaeh,
Akahnōm seng hong êt ruh.

3) Ṭong Intheng khan num akadik rōk ahri,
Takkasen rakip in hring na nannei sik;
Inhmang theilo Rathla Intheng apêk me,
Akahnōm seng hong êt ruh.
