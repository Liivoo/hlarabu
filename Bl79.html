79. JESUS! SAVIOUR! PILOT ME (S.S.556)

“He guideth them by the skillfulness of His Hands.” (Ps.78:72)

EDWARD HOPPER Doh is BbJOHN E. GOULD

1) Pathen! Kei setna kanei,
Tuipui chung ah kase eng;
Ka setna atam jârra kan rakōng kakōi sō jo,
Rōngpu Jesûn eihûk roh, Nang rōk hringsōt theina che.

2) Kan rakōng kakōi sō jo,
Tui kasip jo asûng ah;
Setna kannei jârra hin kan rathla Nang kanpêk che,
Rōngpu! Nang in aṭhōi roh, hringsōt na chu Nang rōk che.

3) Thlipui-khōpui inthō jo,
Kanthi nasik kahnai jo;
Jōng en roh! Jōng lungset roh! Nang teilo bêr sik om mak,
Akahrât Chunghnung Jesu! Kei hring na amannei roh.

4) Tuipui kalên ah kakhang,
Ei ṭhōi roh – asen sōt roh;
Tûn kamalung dong lai hin, rakōng inn ting se theina,
Rōngpu Jesu! Nang rōk che rakōng kesōt theina che.
