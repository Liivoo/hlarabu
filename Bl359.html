359. THERE IS ALWAYS TIME FOR PRAYER (R.S.836)

“…continueth in supplications and prayers night and day.” (I Tim.5:5)

EDITH H. KINNEY Doh is C GEORGE O. WEBSTER

1) Kani ther jingkâr narôn jâng nasik,
Na rathla rôn kahrik kha;
Ṭhat theina jong roh pha anhnot lai hin,
Der nasik apha kaom.

Jing khōvâr der pha, sûn ṭim derna pha,
Khōhlei derna pha kaom;
Pha rakip ah khan malung kahlut ah,
Der nasik pha kha kaom.

2) Kani ther jingkâr malung mathok roh,
Tûn pha inrang ah kathleng;
Malung kahlut ah naṭōn tlo pumma,
Der nasik pha kha’m kaom.

3) Châm na pha nân-pai inthlûk nasik âm,
Nabing ah nân ngam mak na;
Sûn kani thōi ah na khōdeng theina,
Der nasik pha kha kaom.

4) Kani ahlut pha, khō hong kajing sik,
Hring na thâi-tui nanei mo?
Kani ahlut pha malung manther roh,
Der nasik pha kha kaom.
