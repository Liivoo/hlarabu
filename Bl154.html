154. COME TO THE FOUNTAIN (S.S.352)

“For with Thee is the fountain of life.” (Ps.36:9)

FANNY J. CROSBY Doh is FGEORGE C. STEBBINS

1) Na setna hei inrôn inna,
Na lungdong na rakip leh;
Na setna in-ngeidam sik ah,
Jesûn anṭangpui sik che.

Inrang inna in êng mak roh,
Heisamo na rathla anhmang sik ah?
Jesûn hûksōt sik ahngâk che,
Nang anthopui sik jo che.

2) Naset lai hin Ama jong roh,
Jesu Christa’n ahngâk che;
Nang nalei set jo tâk innum,
Kavur ka ang sik jo che.

3) Christa’n abêk na kare kha,
Insir û-na rangei ruh;
Tu ênnum atak kasen chu,
Kumsôt ah kahring sik jo.

4) Nadam thei nasik ah hong roh,
Abêk na che rangei roh;
Setna kanei hei insir na,
Marvân mi’n an inpâk pui.
