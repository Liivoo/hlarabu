339. SOME HAPPY DAY (S.S.934)

“And they sing the song of Moses and the Lamb.” (Rev.15:3)

E.E. REXFORD Doh is Db FRANK M. DAVIS

1) Hla kahōi tak kha kên kathei,
A hla sak sik kâr kahngâk hla;
Kare kahōipên khan ahri,
Pa lungset hla kha kên sak rong.

Inpâk nasik kani kahnai,
Kare kahōi ah kên sak rong;
Hla kahōi kha kasak pha leh,
Jesu hneng châm inhnga sik eng.

2) Ni khat ka hlam hro kabâng sik,
Ralei hi la hôn hmang nger sik;
Hlam kasei hi atlang ning khan,
Tlensōt Pathen kha kajōn sik.

3) Ni khat kani khan kaṭi sik,
Hlung mantam kot pui anhlang pha;
Insûm loah kahlut sik eng,
Kumsôt châm hngat kachen sik jo.

4) Kase na pha atlung jônchu,
Inn jōnna hlam manhret mak eng;
Pathen kut ah kei in-ngai eng,
Aṭhōi sik eng kani tlung ning.
