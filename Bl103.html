103. FAITH OF OUR FATHERS (Ch.H.5)

“…earnestly contend for the faith….” (Jude.3)

FREDERICK W. FABER Doh is AHENERY F. HEMY

1) Pu leh pa hei taksen kahring,
Hmei khur leh chem jâm kanêk ah;
Heituk ah inpâk kaom mo!
Lûrna ṭong hi nithei jo pha.

Pu leh pa hei taksen intheng,
Tak chang ru ung thi katlung ah.

2) Pu leh pa sōk lei kachang hei,
Ning leh hnōm ah kaom jo me;
Ansa’n vângṭhat hing hrep ninei,
Ah sik ah nithi ngam inchu.

3) Pu leh pa taksen kan madu,
Ṭimre leh hei indōi pha leh;
Hringkhō kahōi ṭong innêm vâng,
Lungset na ṭong hri tor nasik.
