358. HAVE THINE OWN WAY LORD! (3rd ALEX.146)

“…Thou our potter…we all are the work of Thy hand.” (Isai.64:8)

ADELAIDE A. POLLARD Doh is F GEORGE C. STEBBINS

1) Na lungdo hlam ah kei asyem roh,
Ka kasyem Pa che, na syem eng kei;
Na lungdo hrep ah amahe roh,
Nahneng ah hlut sik ah kên kahngâk.

2) Na lungdo hlam ah kei aṭhōi roh,
A-en sui inna amanse roh;
Kavur nêk ah asôp mantheng roh,
Na-âng sûng ah kên kakamuk che.

3) Na lungdo hrep ah kei ahûk roh,
Khōdeng kathei kei, enṭangpui roh;
Ratha hrât na seng Nang ta kaeh,
Kadam thei nasik a-en sui roh.

4) Na lungdo hlam ah kei aṭhōi roh,
Inrim, khōdeng kathei na hei ah;
Na Rathla Intheng amansip roh,
Christa kasûng ah om ṭhak rasoh.
