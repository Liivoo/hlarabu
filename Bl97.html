A SHELTER IN THE TIME OF STORM (S.S.539)

“…my God is the rock of my refuge.” (Ps.94:22)

VERNON J. CHARLESWORTH Doh is FIRA D. SANKEY

1) Jesu kapho leh kân thup na,
Thlipui kârra Jesu kân sik;
Heituk ṭit na achang innum,
Thlipui kârra Jesu kân sik.

Kamalung dong na Jesu hneng kader sik,
Kalung dong na, kalung dong na;
Kamalung dong na Jesu ka hlam kakei,
Thlipui kârra Jesu kân sik.

2) Thlipui in aphâk eng innum,
Thlipui kârra Jesu kân sik;
Rōngpu a-om pui ṭhak inna,
Thlipui kârra Jesu kân sik.

3) Eh! Marvân ah pho kân thup na,
Thlipui kârra Jesu kân sik;
Rōngpu’n a-om pui ṭhak inna,
Thlipui kârra Jesu kân sik.
