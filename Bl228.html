228. GO YE INTO ALL THE WORLD (S.S.1090)

“Go ye therefore and teach all nations….” (Matt.28:19)

G.M.J. Doh is A JAMES McGRANAHAN

1) Ram kahla tak khō inthim kârra mihei,
Rathla inhmang hûksōt sik kaom hei;
Tumo kase sik hringsōt na ṭong kahri?
Jesu rōk en ah châm man jōng loah.

[Ratha rakip apêk sōt eng] x2
Nang ni se ruh hmun tin ah;
Gospel ṭong va hri ruh,
Kên nang ni kathlop sik chei.

2) Christa sepei! Ralei chung hmun rakip ah,
Rathla inhmang sik mi hûksōt hei ruh;
Christian! thōi ruh malung khat rôn êt û-na,
Gospel ṭong vâng set rake lo sōt ruh.

3) Rōngpu thlop jârra nang ni thi mak tin chei,
Arahming in nang ni ahûk sik chei;
Thina sik hmun ah Jesu lei kathi jo,
Rathla hringsōt na ṭong hi phôngsōt ruh.

4) Phung rakip sik Pathen in pha aman hnai,
Aheitik âm Rōngpu lûr ṭhak rasoh;
Katlensōt Sandam sa hên hing ah ôn ruh,
Hallelujah! Christa Rengpa! ti ruh.
