143. HE HAS TAKEN MY SINS AWAY (S.S.899)

“Behold the Lamb of God…taketh away the sin of the world.” (Jn.1:29)

LYMAN G. CUYLER Doh is BbIRA D. SANKEY

1) Rōngpu manpâk na hla kasak sik,
Ahneng ah inpâk sik eng;
Hla kasak sik alungset na hla,
Mahngi nôk mak jo ning.

Amân kaset rakip arôn sō,
Arahming inthang, Arahming inthang;
Amân kaset rakip arôn sō,
Hallelujah! Manpâk rih.

2) Kaset kahrik tak leh kahong eng,
Amân ê-na apêk sik;
Akatlensōt na marit tak kha,
Hla vâng kasak ṭhak sik.

3) Ahneng katlung leh kamanpâk sik,
Thi nasik ahûk manhring;
Ka manpâk ṭhak sik aheitik âm,
Manpâk chim thei mak ning.
