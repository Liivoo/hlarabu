152. OH! LET US ALL ENDEAVOUR (S.S.1109)

“…take unto you the whole armor of God…to withstand…the evil….” (Ephe.6:13)

W.B. WILLIAMS Doh is BbGEORGE C. STEBBINS

1) Rahnak ah inṭang êt rih, malung leh ratha vâng,
Ni Rōngpu ṭōn tlo vâr ah aṭōn ni tlo pumma;
Tor sō ah inpâk nasik aṭongkam manhret rih,
Inpâk na tui kalōng sik, nipa lungdo kaeh.

Christa bêkrup sik kannei rakip kanpêk,
Christa bêkrup sik kanhring ṭhak thei nasik.

2) Nikheng ah hlim ṭhak êt rih, vânhnōi set tor nasik,
Khôn ênnum setna kârra rathla hring nasik ah;
Ṭong kaṭha hi hri pumma vânhnōi set katlung ah,
Thina daihlim hnōi ah hin arasa anthei sik.

3) Mi rakip in tlo êt rih vânhnōi khō inthim ah,
Taksen tak ah tlo êt rih vânhnōi nidam lai hin;
Ama sik neinâk rakip, ni hrât na rakip um,
Rōngpu ṭōn hi nitlo’n chu kumsôt hring ninei sik.
