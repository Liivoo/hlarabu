381. KUMTHER CHIBEI (M.C.K.3)

“For thy name’s sake, lead me and guide me.” (Ps.31:3)

M. CHUNGRENGKHUP KOM Doh is Eb 13/06/2016

1) Rōngpu lungset alen jârra,
Kumther nihong hmu jo;
Arahming chôimansâng jo rih,
Chunghnungpên ah lûrpên rasoh;
Ti-ah phôngsōt jo rih.

Hallelujah! Phôngsōt jo rih,
Alungset len je hri ah hri tor lo;
Chamna, môngna, damna hringkhō,
Jōng rôn rasoh, jōng rôn sōt roh;
Sopui ṭimre leh khon ênnum,
Ni omna hmun ah khan;
’Dam seng ah om rih,
KUMTHER CHIBEI!

2) Kum marō kathleng jo kapō,
Sopui û leh nai hei;
Dahlon jo rih taksa hōina,
Ṭōn ther tlo sik intluk jo rih;
Vâng ṭhat nichen sik jo.

3) Sopui lungset khangja, khangja,
Hma andahlon jo me;
Nitin malung ah nirôn hei,
’Pa ang sung ah lan hmu sik me;
Inṭhen nôk mak jo min.

4) Kan Tlensōtpa Chunghnung Pathen!
Na vâng ṭhat kander che;
Kum katlung bit ah aṭhōi roh,
Mi rakip in hmu hei rasoh;
Na lungset inlak hi.
