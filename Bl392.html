392. PA MARIT! (M.C.K.21)

“…but as for me and my house, we will serve the Lord.” (Josh.24:15)

M. CHUNGRENGKHUP KOM Doh is Eb 13/07/2017

1) Pathen tapêk innshung kahōi,
Sōn leh pâr kaṭha jônlâr sōt;
Pathen rōisyem nang ah inphut,
Insyem sōt jo innshung kahōi.

Joshua kapō na innshung sik,
Rōngpu rajen na ṭōn pui kha;
Hlam hma nakei, nathlop ṭhak ung,
Kanmanpâk che oh! Kan PA MARIT!

2) Nalungset na hingtuk kalên,
Innshung sik na inpêk sōt na;
Hnem na kase chang mak sona,
Na hmu nger sik naṭōn tlo man.

3) Lungset pum ah na mansena,
Kajui sa leh nai rakip he’n;
Ralei ah chungsōk na anchen,
Hringsei sôt na kham anchen sik.

4) Innshung, Bekrup, jât leh phōng sik,
Taksen theina jōng malâr roh;
Damna, hlimna, ratha hrât na,
Rōngpu in jōng pêk che rasoh.
