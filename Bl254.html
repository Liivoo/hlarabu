254. ONLY TRUST HIM (S.S.392)

“Take My yoke upon you…learn of Me and find rest….” (Matt.11:29)

J.H.S. Doh is G JOHN H. STOCKTON

1) Hong ruh, set kahrik karôn seng,
Rōngpu lungset sûng ah;
Aṭong nan taksen manhnet ruh,
Amân ahûk sik chei.

Taksen êt ruh, taksen êt ruh,
Rōngpu Jesu kha;
Ahûk sik me, ahûk sik me,
Tak kasen rakip.

2) Rōngpu Jesu thisen kalōng,
Calvary tlâng chung ah;
Set hei lai ah atam innum,
Asôp mantheng sō thei.

3) Jesu ṭong kadik leh hlampui,
Châm inhngat na’m Ama;
Aheitik ah hrâtna, hoina,
Tak kasen hei sik ah.

4) Hong ruh, ram kahōi ting jōn rih,
Mi’ntheng rakip lêng na;
Kum leikō hring bâng mak jo ning,
Rōngpu Jesu hneng ah.
