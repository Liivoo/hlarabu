229. LO! HE COMES (S.S.161)

“Go ye therefore and teach all nations….” (Matt.28:19)

J. CENNICK & C. WESLEY Doh is Bb M. MADAN

1) En ruh! Amân kasûm chung ah jōng kaṭum, mi kaṭhōi sik,
Taksen kanei lising raja’n tor na hla vâng anmanpâk;
Hallelujah! Hallelujah! Hallelujah!
Jesûn vai arôn sik jo.

2) Alûrna pōn marit si kha hmit rakip in anhmu sik,
Anlei jōr sōt, cross ah anthâ, rahling, thir kir, kasei bi;
Akatlen hên anhmu pha leh inpâk pumma,
Messiah! kajōng anti.

3) Bêkna rasa anthei pha leh marvân ralei katlân sik,
Ankamuk ah mi rakip in katlu hei ah ankamuk;
Ṭong katan sik, ṭong katan sik, ṭong katan sik,
Ṭong katan jōng roh Rōngpu!

4) Ami hei kaṭhōi sōt sik ah Rōngpu kha jōng inlâr sik,
Atak kasen mi rakip in vân ṭim ah ani’ntōpui;
Hallelujah! Hallelujah! Hallelujah!
Rōngpu kha lûr ṭhak rasoh.

5) Ȏn ruh mi rakip in Amen! Chi tik neilo avai rôn,
Rōngpu lûrna leh lu lênna chang rasoh bâng tik nei loah;
Inrang tak ah, in rang tak ah, in rang tak ah,
Hallelujah! Rōngpu jōng roh!
