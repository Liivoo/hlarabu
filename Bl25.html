25. WHEN WE ALL GET TO HEAVEN (ALEX.136)

“...caught up…to meet the Lord…shall be with the Lord.” (1 Thess.4:17)

ELIZA E. HEWITT Doh is CEMILY D. WILSON

1) Jesu lungset inlak tak kha,
Ama manpâk na hla sak ruh;
Inn marit leh inn kalûr tak,
Amantluk pêk sik.

Marvân ram ni tlung pha leh,
Inpâk na kani lunpui kachang sik;
Jesu ni hmu pha leh,
Tor na hla kahōi nisak sik.

2) Marvân kajôn ah nise lai,
Kasûm in amakhu sik;
Hme khō hlam hro nitor pha leh,
Jing nôk mak jo ning.

3) Intheng tak leh kadik tak ah,
Eini’n aṭōn tlo inna;
Pha bong khat rōk achang innum,
Tlo man ni hmu sik.

4) Inpâk man hmu nasik tlo rih,
Aṭhat ni hmu bei sik jo;
Kot kalûr kha ânhlang pha leh,
Kahlut sik jo me.
