72. WHAT A GATHERING! (S.S.158)

“…unto Him shall the gathering of the people be.” (Gen.49:10)

FANNY J. CROSBYDoh is CIRA D. SANKEY

1) Kani vâr kapō mihring Sapa ajōng ṭum pha tak leh,
Vâr kahōi Ama jōng lûrna ni hmu sik;
Vânhnōi ah phung rakip Ama mihei seng amantûp sik,
Tlensōt hei intûp na kani kaom sik.

Eini intûp ning kaom sik,
Eini Tlensōt sahei intûp na kani kha kaom sik;
Eini intûp ning kaom sik,
Chungkhōpui ah khin eini lântûp sik me.

2) Christa rahming ah kathi Ama ṭongpêk ah kathōi sik,
Thlân thim sûng ah leh tuipui sûng ah rakip;
Marvân ravōi ah anthleng hei leh khan hlam intō sik me,
Intûp na inpâk na kalûr kaom sik.

3) Kahmit in khōpui kavâr alûrna kha ahmu tak leh,
Kavadung kahōi kadai alōng na ram;
Niṭhōn akathi hei leh khan ah khan hlam intō sik me,
Eini intûp inhmu tō ning kachang sik.

4) Rengpa kha soilo jên ah ajōng nger sik pha kahnai jo,
Vâng syem na ṭongpêk na kani ni hmu sik;
Khava intet-ther ning kha hmit-phet khat kâr rōk kachang sik,
Eini Ama leh kum khōah leng nasik.
