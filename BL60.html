60. I LOVE HIM (R.S.715)

“We love Him, because He first loved us.” (I Jn.4:19)

ENGLISH HYMN BOOKDoh is DSTEPHEN G. FOSTER

1) Se sōt jo roh kamalung ah lungdong!
Jesu thisen in kei ahûk jo eng;
Kamalung cross kûng rōk ah kaom jo,
Athisen marit vâng kaset amantheng jo.

Kalungset! Kalungset! Amân hma alungset,
Kaset arôn pêk jo Calvary cross ah khan.

2) Kei len hmang eng setna sumpui kârra,
Kên setna khōhri hneng karathla kapêk;
Vōikhat chu Pathen hnuk nat kaṭit jo,
Tûn kaset na rakip Jesûn amantheng jo.

3) Kei len hlôn eng, tûnchu alo nôk jo,
Kahmit kacho, tûn vârna kanei jo;
Kalei thina Christa kahring jo eng,
Vânhnōi ah aṭong kaṭha tak kha hri jo rong.
