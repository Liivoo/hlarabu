179. EVERY DAY AND HOUR (S.S.570)

“Keep me as the apple of the eye….” (Ps.17:8)

FANNY J. CROSBY Doh is AbWILLIAM H. DOANE

1) Tlensōtpa! Kumsôt hring na,
Na hneng ah om ṭhak hi kamadu;
Na thi vâng ei mantheng roh,
Kumsôt ah ei om pui ṭhak jo roh.

Nitin ah, nitin ah,
Na manhret ah ei ṭhōi roh;
Na milungset thei na hi,
Oh! Rōngpu! Nang in ei om pui roh.

2) Hiva ralei ah hin chu,
Kalêng lai seng aṭhōi ṭhak inna;
Ka taksen che bâng mak ning,
Ka hlam um hro ṭhe nôk mak jo rong.

3) Na lungset vâng aṭhōi roh,
Hring lai nunchen katôi têk ah hin;
Ka hring na bâng mak sonna,
Marvân ram ah khin kaom sik eng.
