98. BY GRACE ARE YE SAVED (S.S.18)

“For by grace are ye saved through faith….” (Ephe.2:8)

D.W. WHITTLE Doh is DJAMES McGRANAHAN

1) Pathen lungset ṭong in,
Sandam na ṭong asem;
Alungset marit amantluk,
Set karôn hring nasik.
Taksen jârra sên sōt chei, taksen jârra,

Ṭōn tlo ah nanta hmu e-mak, ṭōn tlo ah nanta hmu e-mak;
Kha e-mak, kha e-mak, lu lênna tutên nei mak rasoh,
Pathen tapêk kaeh, lu lênna tutên nei mak rasoh;
Pathen tapêk kaeh, Pathen tapêk kaeh,
Pathen tapêk kaeh, Pathen tapêk kaeh.

2) Pathen lungset na hi,
Christa chung ah inlâr;
Thing cross chung ah alei de pêk,
Alungset amanthei.

3) Tak kasen rakip hneng,
Christa ah inlâr jo;
Alungset nân akatlen me,
Pathen sa amanchang.
