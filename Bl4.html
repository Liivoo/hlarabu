4. THE SONG OF THE SOLDIER (S.S.1174)

“…endure hardness as a good soldier of Jesus Christ.” (II Tim.2:3)

E.NDoh is BbJAMES McGRANAHAN

1) Thô ruh nang ni Tlensōt sahei,
Christa hlam kei pêk hei seng;
Har jo ruh oh! Phung rakip hei,
Re hma kajôn teng se ruh.

Pathen ṭong kalûr phôngsōt ruh,
Tuipui mahring ka ang ah;
Ni’n Tlensōtpa Christa jârra,
Re kator hei nikhe jo;
Re kator hei nikhe jo,
Re kator hei nikhe jo ;
Ni’n Tlensōtpa Christa jârra,
Re kator hei nikhe jo.
2) Mi kahrât he’n hlam ankei jo,
Ralei ah ni om lai seng;
Nichung ah hrât na anpêk me,
Christa hrât na kanei he’n.

3) Thi leh nat ṭit nôk mak jo min,
Jesu hringsōt na jârra;
Ajō’n lârna hmun rakip ah,
Akahnung nijui ṭhak sik.
