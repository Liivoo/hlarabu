168. LIFE AT BEST IS VERY BRIEF (S.S.437)

“For all our days are passed away….” (Ps.90:9)

E.L.K. Doh is EbWILLIAM J. KIRKPATRICK

1) Mihring hringkhō katôi hêr, thingbō katla kapōah,
Chom khat kârra inhmang thei, inrin ruh;
Hring na kani katlôm jo, vângset na jōng katlung sik,
Hring na kani katlang sik, inrin ruh.

Inrin ruh! Inrin ruh!
Jesûn abêk lai chei ahin inrin ruh;
Hring na kot inhlang hmu loah set ah ni om ṭhak inchu,
Hringkhō jōng katlung sik jo, inrin ruh.

2) Reipâr kaṭha tak hei hi’m anṭhat na katlang rih jo,
Chom khat kârra inhmang thei, inrin ruh;
Rathla’n abêk lai chei hin rangei loah om mak ruh,
Thina hlam ting se tinnei, inrin ruh.

3) Apha – achâng kahnai jo, thi vairôn na kahnai jo,
Jesu ṭong ah inhmang ruh, inrin ruh;
Nan hringkhō atluk kârra nan rathla’n hmeikhur sûng ah,
Ade sik kha en û-na, inrin ruh.

4) Bêkna kare rangei ruh, Rōngpu rahming matu ruh,
Marvân mi’m inpâk sik hei, inrin ruh;
Setna inthim dahlon ruh, Jesu khi taksen û-na,
Marvân hlam ting jôn û-na, inrin ruh.
