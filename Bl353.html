353. VICTORY IN JESUS

“God…giveth us the victory through our Lord Jesus Christ.” (I Cor.15:57)

E.M.B. Doh is Ab EUGENE M. BARTLET

1) Tlensōtpa kalûr tak ah ajōng ṭum thuhming kên kathei,
Hingtuk mi kaset kei hûksōt na Calvary ah kathi;
Athisen marit lōng pha, ade nat rasa kathei pha,
Kaset malung kânsir jo pha, torna kha kachen jo.

Torna Jesu ah kaom, kumsôt ah kan Tlenpa!
Ajong sōt, Arachôk, Athisen vâng;
Alungset eng kamanhret, kên kalungset sik,
Kabat intheng na thisen ah khan torna amannei jo.

2) Mantheng na leh mandam na Ratha anei ṭong kên kathei,
Amân hmit kacho khō aman hmu, ke katlek amanlôn;
Kên ka ôn ah Oh! Rōngpu! Ka rathla amandam pêk roh,
Jesu kajōng ah kei torna aṭhōi manhlut jo eng.

3) Kei sik innpui kalûr tak amantluk pêk eng ṭong kathei,
An hlampui Sana vâng rōk ansyem, khōpui kahōi sûng ah;
Tlensōtpa thuhming marō marvân Ngeva hên hla ansak,
Chungkhōpui ah son kêm nikhat torna hla kasak sik.
