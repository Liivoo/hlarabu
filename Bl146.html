146. MY ANCHOR HOLDS (3rd ALEX.87)

“…an anchor of the soul…sure and steadfast…” (Heb.6:19)

W.C. MARTIN Doh is CDANIEL B. TOWNER

1) Tui-ôngpui an-nông innum, thlipui-khōpui anthō’num,
Kamanhret ah kamông eng, thlipui ahôn hnak ravâng;
Ka rakōng khit na kahnet, kumsôt âm set mak jo ning.

Rakōng khit na kahnet,
Thlipui hrâng anhnak innum;
Ka rakōng asin ravâng lungset jârra kator sik,
Ka rakōng kahnet, rakōng kahnet.

2) Tui in-nông in avur eng, vângset na jōng katlung sik,
Sumtuk marvân kasip ah tui in-nông anhnak innum;
Kangir eng ṭit lo jên ah, rakōng hlungpui ah inkhit.

3) Rakōng hnet kamanhret sik, thli ajing ankai lai hin,
Rakōng hrui ândang mannum akahrik seng arôn sō;
Thlipui kâr âm kahong sik, tuipui in-nông bâng hma seng.

4) Ka rathla achâm jônnum, inreng na asip innum,
Kamalung mandon intlûn, kasûm in khō amanjing;
Christa ah kangir tor eng, rakōng thirchak-ai kanei.
