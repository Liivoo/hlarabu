397. OH! KAN MATHLA CHE! (M.C.K.23)

“The Lord watch…me and thee, when we are absent one from another.” (Ps.144:4)

M. CHUNGRENGKHUP KOM Doh is G 28/08/2017

1) Ralei mihring hringkhō pha marit hi,
Manhret manlo kathleng rih jo;
Tui katlân kapō pha marit hei hi,
Kathleng ṭhak rih jo hei hing ah hin.

Oh! Kan mathla che (chei),
Ṭimre rangei se jo pa roh (ruh);
Ralei ah hin ni’nṭhen jônnum,
Lan hmu nôk sik me marvân ah.

2) Nat leh khōdengthei, hōina, inpâk na,
Ajōng rôn ni hringkhō sung ah;
Mahngi thei loah kaom ṭhak sik jo,
Ralei hringkhō hi a-om kârseng.

3) Tûn eini ni inṭhen jo tâk innum,
Pha rakip ah indon ṭhak rih;
Damna leh hōina, malung ah mông na,
Pathen in apêk ṭhak chang rasoh.

4) Hmu phâk mak na ram ah nilêng jônnum,
Pathen ṭōn sik ah inṭang rih;
Apêk jo me aṭongpêk kalûr kha,
Pha chai katlung anṭhōipui ṭhak sik.
