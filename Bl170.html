170. ABUNDANTLY ABLE TO SAVE (S.S.400)

“…He will abundantly pardon.” (Isai.55:7)

ELISHA A. HOFFMAN Doh is APHILIP P. BLISS

1) Tu achang innum cross chung ah thi kahmu,
Tu achang innum Christa tak lei kasen;
Mankase jên ah hringsōt na ahmu sik,
Amân hûkmanhring torna ratha anei.

Eh! Kasōpui hei! Rōngpu khan abêk me,
Ama ma oina lungset na mankase;
Ama thisen vâng set karôn amantheng,
Ama thisen kha mankase apêk me.

2) Pathen ṭong Intheng kathei ah kahmu hei,
Ataksen jâr khan thisen vâng intheng sik;
Intheng na thisen kumkhō ahmu ṭhak sik,
Ama thisen kha akheng ah asem eh!

3) Tu achang innum set intheng na kajong,
Amalung mahlang sonna mantheng rasoh;
Amalung sûng ah Rōngpu manhlut rasoh,
Jesu Christa khan mantheng sik ahngâk eh!
