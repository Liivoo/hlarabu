114. WHEN THE KING SHALL COME (S.S.174)

“Let the children of Zion be joyful in their King.” (Ps.149:2)

FANNY J. CROSBY Doh is AbIRA D. SANKEY

1) Setna inthim ṭit kaom hi sumpui ang ah akatôm,
Tâkkha khō jōng kavâr sik jo marvân ah Rōngpu vârna;
Zion tlâng chung ah chung pumma Sandam hei hla nisak sik,
Inpâk tak ah intûp sik me Rōngpu jōng pha leh.

Eh! Zion! Mi’nreng hei!
Nan inpâk nasik kani jōng kahnai jo;
Eh! Zion! Eh! Zion!
Ni Rōngpu ajōng pha inpâk sik jo chei.

2) Rōngpu thisen ah katlensa sak leh thlang, sim leh hmâr ah,
Inpâk tak ah hla ansak sik, hōina ram anjōn sik jo;
Chi tin leh phung tin ah mi leh ram tin ah kaom mi leh,
Inpâk tak ah intûp sik me, Rōngpu jōng pha leh.

3) Asûn-ajân ah kahngâk hei, Amân ala jōng ṭhōi sik,
Bâng lo jên ah aṭōn tlo rih, Rathla chem jâm hmang inna;
Hiva ralei inthim sûng ah ni hmeivâr indet inna,
Khan inpâk tak ah hla sak rih, Rōngpu jōng pha leh.
