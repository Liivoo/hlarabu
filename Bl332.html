332. SINCE I FOUND MY SAVIOUR (S.S.885)

“Whom having not seen, ye love.” (1 Pet.1:8)

ELIZA E. HEWITT Doh is Ab JOHN R. SWENEY

1) Hringkhōther hmaisō inlak eh!
Tlensōtpa kahmu jo;
Tlensōtpa kamalungset eh!
Cross chung ah khan kên kahmu.

Lungdong, lungjing inhmang sō,
Jesûn asat manvâr jo;
Marvân ram kha kahnai jo,
Tlensōtpa kanhmu jo.

2) Lungset kare innêm kathei,
Tlensōtpa kahmu jo;
Ahûksōt na ṭong ajōng rôn,
Kei akatlen sōt jo eng.

3) Tlensōtpân kei abêk kathei,
Rahming lûr kachen jo;
Inri tak ah kei athlop eng,
Hûksōt kha kên kachen jo.

4) Alungset kut vâng aṭhōi eng,
Tlensōtpa kanhmu jo;
Alên in-ông na daihlim ah,
Hûksōtpa hmai kahmu sik.
