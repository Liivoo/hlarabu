198. THE SOLID ROCK (S.S.902)

“…the Lord is my defence; and…the rock of my refuge.” (Ps.94:22)

E. MOTE Doh is G WILLIAM B. BRADBURY

1) Jesu thisen leh aṭhat na,
Kamalung Ahneng ah pe rong;
Heite om mak ralei chung ah,
Jesu thisen rōk kha hring na.

Christa hlungpui ah kangir eng,
Hmun lak chu ngir hnet na om mak;
Hmun lak chu ngir hnet na om mak.

2) Khō inthim in amakhun chu,
Ni malung pe rih Ahneng ah;
Thli leh khōpui anthim innum,
Ṭit mak na Rōngpu hneng kaom.

3) Aṭongkam leh athisen khan,
Hmeipui – tuipui kâr âm a-en;
Marvân ralei abâng innum,
Ama hneng kaom sik jo eng.

4) Ama ajōng ṭum pha tak leh,
Ni taksen ajōng hmu sik mo?
Setna nilei nei mak inchu,
Alen hmun ah in-ông sik me.
