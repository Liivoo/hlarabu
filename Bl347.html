347. EVENING PRAISE (S.S.292)

“Evening…morning and at noon, will I pray.” (Ps.4:17)

MARY A. LATHBURY Doh is Ab WILLIAM F. SHERWIN

1) Kani vâr ahlut sō pha,
Marvân, ralei inchip jo;
Jân hngâk na an kamuk che,
Chung vân pumpui hmeiser;
Indet invâr sō.

Ântheng! Ântheng! Ântheng!
Rōngpu Pathen!
Marvân ralei kasip che,
Nang anchôi ka-ân êt che;
Chunghnung Rōngpu!

2) Vânhnōi ralei chung ah hin,
Hring na rông sûng ah sik ah;
Nahmai kajong hei seng kha,
Nahneng ah mantûp hei roh;
Hringna Rōngpu!

3) Jân kasûm ajōng jâm pha,
Lungset malung châm inhnga;
Marit na leh lungset na,
Nahmai ârsi akatōm;
Kan kamuk che!

4) Sûn leh jân ah chung ârsi,
Inhmu ṭhak aheitik ah;
Bâng jo rasoh arahle,
Rōngpu Tirle! Jōn lâr roh;
Kumsôt nisō!
