338. RESTING IN THE EVERLASTING ARMS (S.S.647)

“…God is thy refuge…underneath are the everlasting arms….” (Deut.33:27)

ELISHA A. HOFFMAN Doh is A A.J. SHOWALTER

1) Hingtuk ah kahōi! Hingtuk ah anlak!
Abân ratha hrât ah in-ngai hi!
Malung mông na leh vângṭhat na kachen,
Kumsôt ratha hrât ah in-ngei eng.

Châm inhnga ruh,
Ṭit na rakip ah anihûk;
Châm inhnga ruh,
Hring na op ah khan;
Châm inhnga ruh.

2) Kahme khō hlam hro hingtuk ah kahōi,
Abân ratha hrât ah in-ngai hi!
Anitin ah kavâr chêt chêt ka hlam,
Abân ratha hrât ah in-ngei eng.

3) Heimo kaṭit? Heimo kakaja sik?
Abân ratha hrât ah in-ngai hi!
Rōngpu leh inhnai eng, kei ṭi mak eng,
Abân ratha hrât ah in-ngei eng.
