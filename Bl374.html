374. OH! HOLY NIGHT! (C.H.B.193)

“…which shall be born of thee shall be called the Son of God.” (Lk.1:35)

JOHN SULLIVAN DWIGHT Doh is Bb ADOLPHE ADAM

1) Oh! Jân Intheng! Chung ârsi kavâr in nêng,
Khava jân khan kantlensōtpa le’ntet;
Ralei chung set na leh thina’n aleitor,
Khava jân kha ralei chung inpâk na;
Inhôr na leh mihring inpâk nasik ah,
Akalûrpên Reng chunghnung Reng Sapa.

Inkûn inna Tirle kare rangei ruh,
Oh! Jân Intheng! Jân ah khan Christa le’ntet;
Oh! Jân Intheng! Oh! Jân! Oh! Jân Intheng!

2) Alei manthei mi rakip inlungset sik,
Ama dân chu lungset leh lung mông na;
Ator sik jo set ratha neina rakip,
Arahming ah set ratha neina hei;
Ator sik jo kare insâng ah ôi rih,
Arahming intheng khi manpâk jo rih.

Christa Rōngpu! Arahming khi manpâk rih,
Aratha hrât leh lûrna phôngsōt jo rih;
Hri manthang rih tûn um, aheitik âm.




Words & Musical Notes Modification: M. Chungrengkhup Kom
