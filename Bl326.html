326. COME, THOU ALMIGHTY KING (S.S.6)

“…O Lord God Almighty…come….” (Rev.11:17)

J. MARRIOTT Doh is G F. GIARDINI

1) Jōng ṭum roh, Reng Chunghnung!
Hla vâng chôi mansâng rung;
Kander che Nang! Na lûrna rakip kha,
Kei ni ajōng en roh!
Nalungset na eng vâng, anitin ah.

2) Mihring ah jōn jōr che,
Na hrât na rakip leh;
Kan hong der che, vângṭhat ajōng pe roh,
Na ṭong hui tlung rasoh;
Rathla Intheng hrât nân aṭhōi rasoh.

3) Antheng lung mông jōng roh,
Kan chung ah jōng om roh;
Kanthi nasik, akahrât Chunghnungpên!
Kan sûng ah jōng om roh;
Adahlon jên mak roh, Rathla Intheng!

4) Lênna, hrât na Nangta!
Manpâk ah om ṭhak roh;
Aheitik ah akalûr ṭhak Rengpa!
Eini’n nihmu ṭhak sik;
Inhmang theilo jên ah alungset me.
