112. LOOK AND THOU SHALL LIVE (S.S.427)

“Look unto me, and be ye saved, all the ends of the earth….” (Isai.45:22)

F.T.W. Doh is AbJOHN H. STOCKTON

1) En roh! Calvary tlâng cross chung ah,
Nang leh kei sik khōdeng alei thei;
Rangei ruh lungset ah abêk che,

En roh! Hring nasik.
En roh! Hring nasik,
En roh! Hring nasik;
Nang sik alei thina thing cross so,
En roh! Hring nasik.

2) Na rathlân hring na anei jo mo?
Kumsôt hring na kha nachen jo mo?
Jesu ni’nthôina inphe sōt jo,
En roh! Hring nasik.

3) Thlân na kaze Sandampa en roh!
Hmun om lai Ahneng ah hong hlut roh;
Nahmai kamōk amanvâr sik jo,
En roh! Hring nasik.

4) Jesûn nang sik chung ting ader ṭhak,
Mi kaset ṭhat nasik amanhret;
Nase-nahong na aṭhōi sik che,
En roh! Hring nasik.
