1. JESUS BIDS US SHINE (S.S.1138)

“Let your light so shine before men….” (Matt.5:16)

SUSAN WARNERDoh is GEDWIN O. EXCELL

1) Vâr intheng tak leh Jesu’n atir me,
Jân ah khoilu hmeivâr kasin kapōah;
Vânhnōi khō inthim sat manvâr nasik,
Nang na omna hmun ah, kei kahmun ah.

2) Jesu’n nivâr nasik amanthei me,
Nivâr antheng mak na Amân ahmu sō;
Marvân ânphut nivâr ajōng en ah,
Nang na omna hmun ah, kei kahmun ah.

3) Vânhnōi kakim ve Jesu’n atir me,
Vânhnōi mihring rakip manpâk nasik ah;
Set malung inreng rakip manvâr sik,
Nang na omna hmun ah, kei kahmun ah.
