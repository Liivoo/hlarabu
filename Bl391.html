391. KAN-ȎI CHE OH! APA! (M.A.K.01)

“…life…is even a vapour…appeareth for a little time…then vanisheth away.” (Jas.4:14)

M. ATON KOM Doh is D 12/03/2012

1) Tlângbung leh hlungpui um akik khōi pha kaom,
Mihring hringkhō sung hin thina’n akeiṭhen rih me;
Inreng na laithuk kasip, inpâk na pui tluk tor mak,
Eh! Thina, Eh! Thina, kan inre pui kachang che.

Hlungpui kapō Apa! Kan ngam na, kan ngaina che!
Jōng hi roh kan hneng ah nasa keini’n kan hngâk che;
Inrang tak ah anhmang daihlim ka ang jo che,
Kan-ôi che oh! Apa! In-ôi ung ralei ah hin.

2) Phebi jân thangṭim ah kajōng sik che mo?
Jân thlavâr lai ah kajōng sik che mo? Apa!
Kan hngâk che, kan rangei che, ṭongjai kahōi hri sōm sik,
Oh! Heituk hrep inpâk na, chung leh hnōi ah inthang sik.

3) Ni khat akathi nger sik ah ni e-num,
Pa rangei na phat hi’m jōng katlung hringkhō sung ah;
Ralei hin thina’n aṭhen me ah kan invai innum,
Inhmu rih marvân ram ah, ni innpui kalûr sung ah.
