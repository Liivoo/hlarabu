283. JESUS WILL WELCOME ME THERE (S.S.988)

“A great cloud of witnesses.” (Heb.21:1)

FANNY J. CROSBY Doh is F JOHN R. SWENEY

1) Tui rekhat ting khan anbêk me,
Ṭimre ni mahngi hla hên;
An hmaisō nihmu bei sik jo,
Inṭhen om nôk mak jo ning.

Tui re kahōi Eden rahōn!
Inn kalûr tak sûng ah khan;
Mi’ntheng hên kei anṭhōi sik eng,
Jesûn alei ratō sik.

2) Tui rekhat ting khan anbêk eng,
Bêkna rasa kên kathei;
Jing nisō kahōi thli hrâng na,
Kamalung ah kaom ṭhak.

3) Tui rekhat ting khan anbêk eng,
Lungdong leh jân om mak jo;
Jesu kha khōpui vâr kaeh,
Ret rakip kha in-êm reng.

4) Tui rekhat ting khan anbêk eng,
Lungset hmit vâng an-en eng;
Tui re kajôn ah kase eng,
Kamalung in amasôn.
