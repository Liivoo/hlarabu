276. RING THE BELL ON HEAVEN (S.S.650)

“There is joy in the presence of the angels…over one sinner that repent.” (Lk.15:10)

WILLIAM O. CUSHING Doh is Bb GEORGE F. ROOT

1) Inpâk ning kani hin, inpâk hla sak ruh,
Malung insir mi inkhat kahong;
En ruh! Malung inpâk tak ah Pathen in,
Asa a-âng ah arahlâng jo.

Inpâk tak ah marvân Ngeva hên,
Serângdâr thô pumma hla ansak;
Set rake karôn mi tuning insō jo,
Hla ansak malung inpâk tak ah.

2) Marvân dâr manhring ruh tuning, inpâk ah,
Hlam invai inkhat hônle jârra;
Amân ahei dahlon setna hlampui kha,
Kahong jo Apa Pathen hneng ah.

3) Inpâk ning kani hin revei thlai dôn ruh,
Mi rakip hneng hri ruh, hring na ṭong;
Hmit rathli’m lōng rasoh, ni inpâk jârra,
Rathla inkhat inte ther nôk jo.
