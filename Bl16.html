16. THERE’LL BE NO DARK VALLEY (S.S.1026)

“Yea, though I walk through the valley of death…” (Ps.23:4)

WILLIAM O. CUSHING Doh is FIRA D. SANKEY

1) Khō inthim mak jo ning ajōng pha leh,
Khō inthim mak jo ning ajōng pha leh;
Khō inthim mak jo ning ajōng pha leh,
Alungset seng aṭhōi sik.

Alungset seng aṭhōi sik,
Alungset seng aṭhōi sik;
Khō inthim mak jo ning ajōng pha leh,
Alungset seng aṭhōi sik.
2) Inreng na kabōi sik ajōng pha leh,
Inreng na kabōi sik ajōng pha leh;
Inreng na kabōi sik ajōng pha leh,
Alungset seng aṭhōi sik.

3) Ṭana om mak jo ning ajōng pha leh,
Ṭana om mak jo ning ajōng pha leh;
Ṭana om mak jo ning ajōng pha leh,
Alungset seng aṭhōi sik.

4) Inpâk hla nisak sik ajōng pha leh,
Inpâk hla nisak sik ajōng pha leh;
Inpâk hla nisak sik ajōng pha leh,
Alungset seng aṭhōi sik.
