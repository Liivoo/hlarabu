329. SINCE I HAVE BEEN REDEEMED (R.S.379)

“My lips shall greatly rejoice…thou hast redeemed.” (Ps.71:23)

E.O.E. Doh is G EDWIN O. EXCELL

1) Hla marit kha kên kasak hnōm,
Kei akatlen ânphut;
Tlensōt Rengpa! Keita kaeh,
Akatlen ah inphut.

Kei akatlen ânphut, kei akatlen ânphut,
Arahming ah kalûr eng, kei akatlen ânphut;
Tlensōtpa rahming ah kalûr eng.

2) Lung ngam na Jesu kha kanei,
Kei akatlen ânphut;
Alungdo ṭōn tlo kei hnem na,
Kei akatlen ânphut.

3) Avâr leh ântheng kahmu jo,
Kei akatlen ânphut;
Ṭit na rakip karakan jo,
Kei akatlen ânphut.

4) Kaom nasik mantluk sa jo,
Kei akatlen ânphut;
Kumsôt ah kaom sik jo eng,
Kei akatlen sōt jo.
