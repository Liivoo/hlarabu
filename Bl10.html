10. TO THE WORK (S.S.751)

“Always abounding in the work of the Lord.…” (I Cor.15:58)

FANNY J. CROSBYDoh is EbWILLIAM H. DOANE

1) Ṭōn tlo ruh! Ṭōn tlo ruh! Eini Pathen sa me,
Ni Rōngpu ṭōn tlo kapōah khan tlo êt rih;
Nichâm pha leh Amân châm aman hngat sik me,
Tlo sik om lai seng ah nitor chen ah tlo rih.

Ṭōn tlo ruh! Ṭōn tlo ruh!
Ṭōn tlo ruh! Ṭōn tlo ruh!
Der inna hngâk ṭhak rih,
Ni Rōngpu ajōng mak kâr seng.

2) Ṭōn tlo ruh! Ṭōn tlo ruh! Von kaṭâm hei hneng ah,
Hring nasik tui leh thlai vasem pêk hei hi ruh;
Tu ênnum hring na ṭong indu rakip pe ruh,
Aman pêk lo jên ah arahlâng thei sik jo.

3) Ṭōn tlo ruh! Ṭōn tlo ruh! Atlo pha katluk jo,
Setna leh mathlêm nân eini ator mak ning;
Ṭōn tlona hi ninei inchu mi rakip in,
Ni Rōngpu rahming hi nimanpâk êt sik jo.

4) Ṭōn tlo ruh! Ṭōn tlo ruh! Hōina kha ninei sik,
Tlo lo jên ah kaom hên rôn neimak hei ning;
Taksen na hmun kha eini ta kachang sik jo,
Ahûksōt jo me tiah eini’n ôn ṭhak rih.
