207. CALLING THE PRODIGAL SON (R.S.125)

“...his father saw him…had compassion…ran…and kissed him.” (Lk.15:20)

C.H.G. Doh is Bb CHARLES H. GABRIEL

1) Nei kahmang sō sapa! Hong roh, Rōngpu’n abêk che,
Rangei roh lungset kare’n nang abêk che;
Alei kalôn inhmang nachang innum hong jo roh,
Tuning Rōngpu’n nang abêk che.

Nang abêk ṭhak che,
Malung inreng nang hong roh;
Nang abêk ṭhak che,
Malung inreng nang hong roh.

2) Hong roh napa inn ah nang sik ah karōi kaom,
Rangei roh malungset kare’n abêk che;
Hong inle jo roh Rathla Intheng khan abêk che,
Rangei roh Rōngpu’n abêk che.

3) Dehrât leh malung innêm tak ah nang abêk che,
Rangei roh lungset kare vâng abêk che;
Marvân ah thlâi intheng kaom, nang ala bêk che,
Lungset na kare’n abêk che.
