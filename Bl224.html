224. ONE SWEETLY SOLEMN THOUGHT (S.S.987)

“Now they desire a better county, that is, an heavenly.” (Heb.11:16)

PHOEBE CARY Doh is Bb PHILIP PHILLIPS

1) Malung inpâk tak ah,
Sak rong Rōngpu lûrna;
Inn kha kên kahong hnai chêt jo,
Ka marvân inn kaeh.

Inn kahong hnai,
Marvân inn kha;
Ka marvân inn kha kahong hnai,
Kumsôt kaom nasik.

2) Kapa inn kahnai che,
Hei rakip kakip sō;
Na Reng vai rônna hmun kavâr,
Kên tuning kahong jōn.

3) Hringkhō ṭōn kachai leh,
Karôn kahrik kahlon;
Cross hlam kha kên kahong jōn jo,
Lên lukhu kahmu sik.

4) Rōngpu! Kei ahûk roh,
Set kârra inphut ah;
Inn kha tuning kahong hnai jo,
Ka marvân inn pui kha.
