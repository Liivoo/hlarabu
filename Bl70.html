70. PRAISE OUR CREATOR (S.S.219)

“Praise ye the Lord.” (Ps.106:1)

FANNY J. CROSBYDoh is CTHEODORE F. SHEWARD

1) Ani kasyempa ni Pathen manpâk rih!
Mi kathi hei inhring thei kha Ama!
Kaja tak ah Ama manpâk ṭhak jo rih!
Amân manhring theina ratha anei.

Manpâk êt rih! Akalûrpên Ama! Manpâk na Ama chen,
Manpâk êt rih! Vânhnōi m’in Ama kha manpâk rih!

2) Ani kasyempa ni Pathen manpâk rih!
Eini inhôr na pên kha Ama jôm ruh;
Kemông kakhêm nun kahōi Pa khan eini,
Tui kalōng daina hmun phaichâm ah khan.

3) Ani kasyempa ni Pathen manpâk rih!
Arahming inthang pên Ama manpâk rih!
Amalungset na ṭong tak lei kasen mi,
Malungset vâng khan anṭhōipui ṭhak sik.
