261. BEHOLD! WHAT LOVE! (S.S.21)

“Behold! What manner of love the Father hath bestowed.…” (1 Jn.3:1)

M.S.S. Doh is Db JAMES MCGRANAHAN

1) En ruh! Hing tuk hrep lungset na,
Pa khan apêk jo me;
Setna kârra anhmang eini,
Pathen sa! ati me.

En ruh! Alungset inlak, alungset inlak kha,
Pathen in eini apêk me;
Rōngpu lungset jârra khan,
Pathen sa! inti sik me.

2) Pathen kha masō lo jên ah,
Thisen jârra hnai ruh;
Lungset tak ah ânhlâng hei kha,
Pathen leh inhnai hei.

3) Ni lûr nasik pha sôt mak jo,
Ama hme nipō sik;
Rōngpu hmai kha nihmu tak leh,
Inchen ah inlâr sik.

4) Khava vâng pêk inhôr puina,
Intheng seng ah om rih;
Marvân innpui ni tlung tak leh,
Rōngpu hme nihmu sik.
