193. A SOLDIER OF THE CROSS (S.S.883)

“Fight the good fight of faith.” (1 Tim.6:12)

ISAAC WATTS Doh is D IRA D. SANKEY

1) Rōngpu Jesu sepei sik ah,
Akahnung jui inna;
Arahming inthang na hmun ah,
Hlo ninei sik tâk mo?

Arahming Chunghnung rahming!
Ka setna arôn pêk;
Lên lukhu kha ka hmu sik jo,
A cross kadom inna.

2) Thisen tuipui re ke nasik,
Châm inhngat na om mak;
Bêkrup in ander pêk na me,
Pathen in anṭangpui.

3) Hring na rei apârna hmun ah,
Kei aṭhōi sik tâk mo?
Lên lukhu hmu sik karei chu,
Thisen marit ka ang.

4) Re de sik antak ravâng ah,
Hrât na Jesûn apêk;
Cross kadom na ni’nreng innum,
Rōngpu’n anṭangpui sik.
