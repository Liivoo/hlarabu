116. SEEDS OF PROMISE (S.S.768)

“In due season we shall reap if we faint not.” (Gal.6:9)

JESSIE H. BROWN Doh is AbF.A. FILLMORE

1) Ralei thlâi om na hlei ah khan,
Lungset hmuchi vôr roh;
Apâr na khôm sik ah,
La hong kakêk sōt nger hi sik.

Ani tin ah sena hong na,
Ṭongtep hmuchi vôr roh;
Hme leh dâm ah akahmin seng,
Achai ning na lôk sik.

2) Kum kase alei chang jônnum,
Hmuchi kha kahring sik;
Pathen in avôr sik ah kha,
Karâ aman nei sik.

3) Lung hlo ah ṭōn tlo anchai leh,
Pathen in akhôm pha;
Na tlo karâ rakip hei kha,
Rōngpu’n amantûp sik.
