410. HMURAI KAPŌ (M.C.K.39)

“Lo, children are an heritage of the Lord….” (Ps.127:3)

M. CHUNGRENGKHUP KOM Doh is Eb 03/10/2018

1) Pathen lungset kutchôi kansa marit!
Nu leh Pa, Pi leh Pu’n kan rahlâng che;
Vâng ṭhat chen roh tûn um aheitik âm.

Nu leh Pa, Pi leh Pu lûrna hong chang roh!
Chung insângpên ah jōng roh hmurai kapō ah,
Dam sōt roh oh! Kansa marit!

2) Pathen! Na âng sung ah kanpêk sōt che,
Na lungdo hrep ah ei syem sōt in na;
Na vâr, na lûrna chang rasoh Amen!






# A composition of an hearty joyfulness upon God’s greatest & precious gift of a baby boy Hmangte Singneithang Kom @Jadon, @Jaden, @Jether. A blessed son of M. Chungrengkhup (Mangboi) & L. Darthatchong (Grace) Born on 08/12/2018 at Siliguri, West Bengal.
