178. A SONG OF SALVATION (S.S.1182)

“…God so loved the world, that He gave His only begotten Son…” (Jn.3:16)

D.G. Doh is GDORA GREENWELL

1) Rōngpu’n alungset ṭong kathei,
Lungset na inlak tak kha kaeh;
Marvân ah inphut jōng kasōk,
Alungset leh arangei jârra, jârra;
Alungset leh arangei jârra.

Kha, kha, kha, kei sik, kei sik,
Kha, kha, kha, kei sik;
Rōngpu ajōng leh alungset nihmu sik,
Cross chung ah athi kha ni hring na.

2) Ade leh athi sōk je kha,
Ânreng leh thinglêr ah athi kha;
Khôn amo mihring Sa inthâ?
Ade pêk leh ânreng pêk jârra, jârra;
Ade pêk leh ânreng pêk jârra.

3) Khan ah nunchen sik kha anhri,
Jesu ami hei khan anhmu sik;
Marvân ah khin inn kahōi tak,
Kei sik mantluk sa ada pêk eng, pêk eng;
Kei sik mantluk sa ada pêk eng.

4) Rōngpu! Kader amasôn roh,
Nang teilo hlut nasik lak om mak;
Rathla Intheng ajōng pe roh,
Rōngpu madu marvân ah kaom, kaom;
Rōngpu madu marvân ah kaom.
