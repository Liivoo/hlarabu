133. THERE SHALL BE SHOWERS OF BLESSING (S.S.306)

“There shall be showers of blessings.” (Ezek.34:26)

DANIEL W. WHITTLE Doh is BbJAMES McGRANAHAN

1) Khō sûr kapō vâng apêk sik,
Alungset ṭongkam kaeh;
Ni malung amanhring sik jo,
Marvân ah jōng kasûr sik.

Vâng jōng mathla roh,
Khō sûr kapō jōng sûr roh;
Lungset rahme jōng mansûr roh,
Nasōk keini’n kander che.

2) Khō sûr kapōah vâng apêk sik,
Eh! Rōngpu! Amanhring roh;
Malung kathi amatho sik,
Kanta der hi jōng pe roh.

3) Khō sûr kapō vâng apêk sik,
Eh! Rōngpu! Vâng jōng pe roh;
Chingtlâng leh phaichâm rakip ah,
Khō kasûr jōng man ang roh.

4) Khō sûr kapōah vâng apêk sik,
Tuning kani jōng pe roh;
Eh! Pathen! Set jōng mantheng roh,
Rōngpu Jesu! Kan rangei.
