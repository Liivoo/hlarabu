87. WAIT AND MURMUR NOT (S.S.710)

“Rest in the Lord and wait patiently for Him.” (Ps.37:7)

WILLIAM H. BELAMY Doh is CWILLIAM J. KIRKPATRICK

1) Oh! Khōdeng thei inreng bâng na!
Anreng hei sik ah inn kaom;
Inreng invai om mak jo ning,
Om hnōm mak min mo khan ah khan?

Oh! Hngâk ṭhak rih ratha set loah,
Oh! Hngâk ṭhak rih ratha set loah;
Oh! Hngâk ṭhak rih ratha set lo jên ah.

2) Na châm na leh nânreng na khâm,
Malungset nân ahûk sik che;
Thlir roh aram jōng kahnai jo,
Lei hngâk ṭhak rih eini’n Ama.

3) Na hlam rahling asip innum,
Nipu Jesu mandon inna;
Na malung kha anreng innum,
Mi’ntheng hei khan hmen ande eh!

4) Inreng na khan um tlo ṭhak roh,
Derna kha nân mahngi mak roh;
Na châm inhngat nasik kahnai,
Hngâk ṭhak roh malung tla loah.
