49. GENTLE JESUS! MEEK AND MILD (S.S.1149)

“Suffer the little children to come unto Me.” (Mk.10:14)

CHARLES WESLEYDoh Is EKING THIBAUT OF NAVARRE

1) Jesu! Nang nun kahōi Pa!
Naipang hei inthopui thei;
Nahneng katlung thei nasik,
Ka ṭhat mak na ei en roh!

2) Pathen Kemôngte Jesu!
Kahring na leh damna che;
Nang innêm leh nun kahōi,
Vōikhat naipang kachang che.

3) Inpâk tak ah kahong eng,
Rōngpu! Alei hōt mak roh;
Na marvân ram kahōi ah,
Naipang hei na om pui thei.

4) Nang nun hōi dung kasui sik,
Nahring na ṭong hri jo rong;
Mi’nlungset thei Nang rōk che,
Vânhnōi mi’n hmu hei rasoh.
