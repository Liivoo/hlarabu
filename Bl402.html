402. RAHMANG KAPŌ HRINGKHŌ (M.A.K.03)

“Thou carriest them away as with a flood; they are as a sleep….” (Ps.90:5)

M. ATON KOM Doh is F 20/03/1992

1) Rahmang kapō kaeh ni hringkhō,
Jingkâr radai anhmang rih kapō;
Inṭhen na kachang jo nu leh pa,
Reipâr pui tluk lo kabōi jo.

Kan jong hla jo che sopui Melody*
Khonting mo andahlôn jo ung hi?
Thina’n aṭhōi jo che Pa Rengram ah,
Kan mathla che ṭimre rangei nu! (Melody)

2) Pathen in naipang alungset me,
Hlut nasik marvân kot amahlang;
Aṭhōi manhlut jo ni Melody!
Kahlut sik me eini’m nikhat.






*(L) Miss R. Melody Kom (19/06/1985 – 20/06/1991) beloved daughter of R. Sumchung Kom & (L) R. Simvarpui Kom of Lairamwangma Baptist Church, Sagang, KRBCA, Manipur. This sentimental song was composed by the then Local Pastor M. Aton Kom and was sung by local primary to bid a farewell to their beloved sister on her Memorial Prayer Day on 20/05/1992.
