352. SUNSHINE IN THE SOUL (S.S.872)

“Light of the morning…sun riseth…as a morning without cloud.” (II Sam.23:4)

ELIZA E. HEWITT Doh is Ab JOHN R. SWENEY

1) Tûn kamalung kanin asat,
Kalûr tak ah kavâr;
Chung ânphut avâr ajōng pêk,
Jesu kha kei sik vâr.

Oh! Kani vâr! Mahamṭhat vâr!
Inpâk na leh incham na kaom;
Jesu hmai-tlai kha anlâr pha,
Kamalung sûng kavâr jo.

2) Tûn kalung ri ah hla kasak,
Ka Rengpa manpâk hla;
Jesu manpâk na hla kathei,
Hla kha sak thei mak eng.

3) Tuning kamalung inpâk jo,
Rōngpu kha ahnai pha;
Kamalung sûng ah hla insak,
Lungset reipâr inlâr.

4) Kamalung sûng inpâk kasip,
Inhôr, inpâk, lungset;
Vâng rakip kei apêk jo eng,
Chunghnung ah inpâk na.
