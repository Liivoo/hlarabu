92. GATHER THEM IN (S.S.779)

“Go out into the highways…hedges and compel…to come in.” (Lk.14:23)

FANNY J. CROSBY Doh is AbGEORGE C. STEBBINS

1) Se roh hmun la kaom, vaṭhōi roh,
Ralei thlai kasa sik be roh;
Va be hei roh ka inn sip hei rasoh,
Kaṭâm seng in sa hei rasoh.

Hlam kalên ah leh vâng ah se roh,
Setna hlam inthim tak ah khan;
Se roh, se roh ṭhat chôm rangei ah,
’Reng – invai hei vaṭhōi roh.

2) Hmun la kaom vaṭhōi nôk hei roh,
Bêkna heite kasak mak chu;
Anbêk adon mak jo hei seng chu,
Tutên be nôk mak jo hei ning.

3) Tûn um hmun la kavât vaṭhōi roh,
Pathen in ṭong apêk nôk che;
Oh! Alungset kûr sûng ah hlut nasik,
Tlensōtpa lungset kûr sûng ah.
