82. THE BETTER LAND (S.S.981)

“A better country.” (Heb.11:16)

GURDON ROBBINS Doh is FDANIEL B. TOWNER

1) Kahmit in ram kahōi ahmu,
Inpâk na leh inhôr na ram;
Akaom seng kha kavâr sō,
Akheng ah ara-âng sō jo.

Eh! Ram kahōi lungset na ram,
Vânhnōi inthim na manvâr ah;
Na marvân ram kha keini sik,
Kan hringkhōsei abâng pha leh.

2) Khiva ram kahōi tak ah khin,
Thi, malung inreng, om mak jo;
Akase inhmang phung mak hei,
Inṭhen nasik om nôk mak jo.

3) Ralei hmun leh te-thei chang mak,
Kani vâr um rangei mak jo;
Aram ah khō inthim mak jo,
Sûn leh jân ah kavâr ṭhak jo.

4) Ṭit om mak na marvân ram ah,
Inhmang sik jo inreng invai;
Khōdeng kathei anvai hênnum,
Châm hngat na inn anhmu sik jo.
