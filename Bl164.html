164. NO NIGHT THERE (S.S.1012)

“…for there shall be no night there.” (Rev.21:25)

JOHN R. CLEMENTS Doh is EbH.P. DANKS

1) Kani tlâk mak na ram ah,
Khōpui kahōi kaom;
Kum leikōah set mak jo ning,
Jân um om phung mak jo.

Hmit rathli kahi sik jo,
Thi leh nat ṭit om mak ning;
Pha leh châng tei mak jo hei,
Jân um om phung mak jo.

2) Akot um inhlang ṭhak jo,
Khōpui rakir manli ah;
A hlam sana rōk ansyem,
Jân um om phung mak jo.

3) Akot inkam phung mak jo,
Khōpui kahōi ah khin;
Sana – dangka tui lōng na,
Jân um om phung mak jo.

4) Kani vâr rangei mak hei,
Khōpui rakir manli ah;
Kemôngte kavâr jârra,
Jân um om phung mak jo.
