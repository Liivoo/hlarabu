141. THE PRODIGAL SON (3rdALEX.382)

“I will arise and go to my father….” (Lk.15:18)

Doh is G

1) Thōi eng sonna Kapa hneng ah kei vase rong,
Ka hma sumpui ang ah kajing;
Dahlon hla sik kên nei mak eng,
Ralei ah hin Kapa teilo chu kare rōk.

Set ah kân vân vai ravâng,
Ṭhat na la kan mak ning;
Lung ri ah kân jong inchu,
Oh! Kahōi sik jo eng.

2) Kapa inn ah inchung nasik kakip sō jo,
Inreng invai kabōi sik jo;
Ka taksa ralei von ṭâm ah kathi sik mo?
Heimo ring vai tak ah kaom.

3) Oh! A-âng sûng khi kân hôrna kahōi tak khi,
Vânhnōi ṭhat na kajong ravâng;
Kahong hnai che Kapa âng sûng karangei kha,
Malung insir na kanei jo.

4) Kei mikase malung inrim na kanei ṭhak,
Kaṭa sik naphe mo Rōngpu?
Oh! Ralei ah set na rei ah pârna hichu,
Apâr jôn chu aṭōi intak.
