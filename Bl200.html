200. GATHERING AFTER TEARS (S.S.992)

“God shall wipe away all tears.” (Rev.7:17)

FANNY J. CROSBY Doh is Bb WILLIAM J. KIRKPATRICK

1) Ram kahōi kajôn sik ah se rih,
Pathen ṭong kha phôngsōt inna;
Anhmang sik hei kaṭhōi sōt sik ah,
Châm inhngat na ram nitlung hma seng.

Châm hngat na hmit rathli sōk nôk mak ning,
Khava kumsôt châm hngat na kahōi ah;
Katlensa hei hla sak kare kha nithei sik,
Inpâk ah Rōngpu hneng ah intûp rih.

2) Ram kahōi kajôn sik ah se rih,
Ṭit lo jên ah se êt inna;
Châm hngat na ram kahōi kahnai jo,
Inṭhum hmun kahōi kha nihmu sik.

3) Ram kahōi tak tlâng inchôi ah khan,
Reipâr marim kahōi kapâr;
Khō vârna ram ah khan ni Rōngpu,
Lungset na hla kahōi nisak sik.

4) Ram kahōi tak, thli hrâng na ram ah,
Khan ah khan khō kavâr ṭhak jo;
Ni’n Tlênsōtpa hme nihmu sik jo,
Thi leh nat, inreng, om mak jo ning.
