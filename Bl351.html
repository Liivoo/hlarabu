351. HE THAT WINNETH SOUL (R.S.317)

“…How beautiful are the feet of them that preach the gospel of peace…!” (Rom.10:15)

ELIZA E. HEWITT Doh is A JOHN M. HARRIS

1) Rōngpu ṭōn katlo mihei kha vâng kaṭha,
Marvân hlung mankatam kha antachen sik;
Rathla kahûk inhring sōt mi hei khachu,
Rōngpu vâng pêk anchen sik.

Rathla kahûk inhring mi,
Marvân innpui sûng ah khan;
Amân lên lukhu kalûr achen sik jo,
Rōngpu’n vâng apêk sik jo.

2) Inn kahōi ah khan lungset hmaisō kavâr,
Anitin Jesu leh kalêng sap sik jo;
Anhmang sik rathla kahûksōt mihei kha,
Mi kasing kachang jo hei.

3) Der leh inṭhōi ah eini’n aṭōn tlo rih,
Hmun tin ah anhmang sik rathla jong sōt rih;
Kemông kakhêm Rōngpu hneng kaom sik me,
Rōngpu vâng pêk nichen sik.

4) Gospel ṭong kha hmun tin ah vaphông sōt ruh,
Lungset na ṭong nan malung ah ching ṭhak ruh;
Rōngpu âng sûng ah malung kathleng mihe’n,
Rōngpu vâng pêk nichen sik.
