145. TRUSTING JESUS (S.S.836)

“Though He slay me, yet will I trust in Him….” (Job.13:15)

EDGAR P. STITES Doh is AbIRA D. SANKEY

1) Nitin kataksen ṭhak che,
Ka hlam khō anthim innum;
Ka taksen asin ravâng,
Christa rōk kamandon sik.

Ka taksen anitin ah,
Ka taksen sûn leh jân ah;
Hei lai ênnum kajui sik,
Jesu rōk kha taksen rong.

2) Rathla Intheng kavâr khan,
Ka malung asat manvâr;
Hmun rakip ah ṭi mak ning,
Christa kamandon ṭhak sik.

3) Hōina hmun ah hla sak rong,
Inreng na hmun ah der rong;
Setna karatō jônnum,
Christa rōk kha taksen rong.

4) Ka hringkhō abâng kâr seng,
Ralei hi anhmang mak hma;
Marvân ram katlung nasik,
Christa rōk taksen ṭhak rong.
