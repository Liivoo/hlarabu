403. PATHEN CHȎI KA-ÂN (M.A.K.06)

“Praise ye the Lord: for it is good…pleasant…and…comely.” (Ps.147:1)

M. ATON KOM Doh is F 05/09/2015

1) Pathen lungset ah kajōng KR khangdông!
Ralei neinâk kaṭha rakip hei karra;
Akaṭha tak inkhat nilei chang hi chu,
Pathen chôi ka-ân nasik lei kachang jo.

Inpâk tak ah kan mathla chei sopui hei!
Ṭong kaṭha phôngsōt nasik intluk rih;
Mankhang rih Pathen Rengram vânhnōi ah hin,
Ajōng rôn pêk sik me Manna ni Rōngpu’n.

2) Taksen leh inhôr tak ah ṭōn lei tlo rih,
Tûn tak hi hnōm pêk na pha kha kaeh;
Mathlêm na’n anitin ah ajui ṭhak me’num,
Ratha hrât kapê sik ah Christa ninei.

3) Rōngpu! Kan hneng ah ajōng tir pêk nôk roh,
Na Rathla Intheng harna, hlim na;
Christa sik ah kaom ṭhak KR khangdông,
Chang êt rung vânhnōi ah hin Bêkrup hmeivâr.
