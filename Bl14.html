14. MORE ABOUT JESUS (S.S.571)

“Follow on, to know the Lord.” (Hosea.6:3)

ELIZA E. HEWITT Doh is GJOHN R. SWENEY

1) Jesu nunchen kamanhrêt sik,
Alungset kahri manthang sik;
Asen sōt na kahmu ṭhak sik,
Kei sik kathi, lungset jârra.

Jesu kamanhrêt sik,
Jesu kamanhrêt sik;
Asen sōt na kahmu ṭhak sik,
Kei sik kathi, lungset jârra.

2) Jesu nunchen kamanhrêt hnōm,
Antheng na kamanhrêt ṭhak sik;
Rathla Intheng kadik tak khan,
Christa ṭong amanthei rasoh.

3) Jesu nun leh aṭong ah khan,
Ka Rōngpu nunchen leh injom;
Akaresō kamanhrêt sik,
Ahri rakip kanpâk nasik.

4) Alên hmun ah Rōngpu Jesu’n,
Alûrna aman hmu sō sik;
Alênna kha insângpên sik,
Mông na Rōngpu ajōng pha leh.
