81. WE’VE A STORY TO TELL (R.S.636)

“…ye shall be witnesses unto Me…to the uttermost part of the earth.” (Acts.1:8)

COLIN STERNE Doh is FH. ERNEST NICHOL

1) Jât rakip hri pêk sik thuhming ninei,
Ṭong kasōk anmanhret nasik;
Ṭong dik leh thuhming kahōi hi,
Mông na leh vârna thuhming;
Mông na leh vârna thuhming.

Khan anthim hmun kha hong kavâr sik,
Avâr kha sûn vâr ka ang sik;
Ralei ah lungset leh vâr hmun kha,
Christa reng ram kachang sik.

2) Jât rakip hla sak pêk sik khat ninei,
Rōngpu ah malung hlut nasik;
Ṭhat mak tor nasik hla kaeh,
Chem jâm leh the kahlon sōt;
Chem jâm leh the kahlon sōt.

3) Jât rakip ṭong mathlung pêk sik ninei,
Rōngpu khan chung ah vai arôn;
Ni kahûk Asa ajōng tir,
Pathen lungset malâr na;
Pathen lungset malâr na.

4) Jât rakip hneng ah ni malâr sik ah,
Hûksōtpa kha eini’n ninei;
Vânhnōi ralei mihring rakip,
Pathen hneng anhong nasik;
Pathen hneng anhong nasik.
