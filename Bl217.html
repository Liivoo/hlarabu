217. THE COMFORTER HAS COME (S.S.198)

“…He shall give you another Comforter….” (Jn.14:16)

F. BOTTOME Doh is C WILLIAM J. KIRKPATRICK

1) Ralei chung rakip ah hring na ṭong va phông ruh,
Gospel hring na ṭong hi mitin hneng phôngsōt ruh;
Christian mi rakip in phôngsōt ruh inpâk ah,
Inṭangpui kajōng jo.

Ni’nṭangpui kajōng jo, ni’nṭangpui kajōng jo,
Marvân Rathla Intheng, Rōngpu ṭongtep pêk kha;
Hringsōt na ṭong Intheng phông ruh mi rakip hneng,
Inṭangpui kajōng jo.

2) Khōhlei jân kathleng jo, jingkâr kasōk nôk jo,
Ralei chung inthim hi avâr sō nasik ah;
Rathla Intheng in vâr ahong rôn hmun tin sik,
Inṭangpui kajōng jo.

3) Reng rakip Reng Chunghnung! rōngpu rakip Rōngpu!
Dam na leh hûksōt na ratha hrât ajōng rôn;
Mi rakip dam na leh hûksōt na amannei,
Inṭangpui kajōng jo.

4) Lungset chi theilo ṭong kamalei in hri roh,
Hringsōt na ṭong Intheng hmun tin ah phôngsōt roh;
Alungset inlak hi mi rakip hneng hri roh,
Inṭangpui kajōng jo.
