22. JOY TO THE WORLD (S.S.111)

“O sing unto the Lord a new song.” (Ps.98:1)

ISAAC WATTS Doh is EbGEORGE F. HANDEL

1) Vânhnōi ralei inpâk êt ruh,
Rōngpu Jesu jârra;
Nan malung ri mantheng êt ruh,
A-om thei nasik ah, A-om thei nasik ah;
Hla sak, hla sak, manchôi ṭhak ruh.

2) Rōngpu’n vânhnōi vai arôn jo,
Inpâk na hla sak ruh;
Phaichâm leh chingtlâng rakip ah,
Alûrje hi hri ruh, Alûrje hi hri ruh;
Ator jo tiah manpâk ruh.

3) Lungset ṭongdik vâng vai arôn,
Phung tin ah inchên jo;
Adik, Aṭhat, Alûrje hi,
Alungset na inlak, Alungset na inlak;
Vânhnōi! Vânhnōi! Mi’m thei rasoh.
