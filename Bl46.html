46. WE’RE MARCHING TO ZION (S.S.823)

Let the children of Zion be joyful in their King.” (Ps.149:2)

ISAAC WATTSDoh is GROBERT LOWRY

1) Hong ruh, Pathen mihei! Inpâk na hla sak ruh,
Inpâk na hla sak êt û-na;
Inpâk na hla sak êt û-na,
Intûp û-na om ruh, intûp û-na om ruh.

Zion tlâng ah se rih,
Zion ram kahōi pên ah khan;
Se rih Zion tlâng kahōi ah,
Pathen khōpui kahōi ah khin.

2) Rathla kabe mihei, lei om hnâm hei rasoh,
Eini Pathen sahei seng chu;
Eini Pathen sahei seng chu,
Phông ruh nan inpâk na, phông ruh nan inpâk na.

3) Pathen ṭong kajôm hei, ralei ah nan om lai,
Marvân ah hōina anhmu jo;
Marvân ah hōina anhmu jo,
Taksen leh hlam rin ah, taksen leh hlam rin ah.

4) Pathen kamuk na khan, hingja ratha hrôk na,
Marvân khōpui niphâk hma seng;
Marvân khōpui niphâk hma seng,
Hlam kahōi ni hro hma, hlam kahōi ni hro hma.
