348. IS MY NAME WRITTEN THERE? (L.H.389)

“…rejoice, because your names are written in heaven.” (Lk.10:20)

MARY A. KIDDER Doh is Ab FRANK M. DAVIS

1) Sana, dangka maduna hringkhō kalei hmang ah,
Marvân ṭong kamandon pha kên rangei mak jo eng;
Marvân Lekha Rabu ah apheng kalûr chung ah,
Ka Rōngpu! Amanthei roh, ka rahming kajao mo?

Ka rahming kajao mo?
Apheng kalûr chung ah;
Na marvân Lekha Rabu ah,
Ka rahming kajao mo?

2) Oh! Khōpui kalûr ah khan karōi kalûr kaom,
Sandam mihei rathla khan pōnvâr kalûr ansi;
Setna kha katlang sik jo khava khōpui ah khan,
Ngeva hei khōpui anhngâk, ka rahming kajao mo?

3) Aheitik ah kavâr ṭhak Sana Khōpui sûng kha,
Taksen na kanei mak mi tute hlut thei mak hei;
Lekha sûng ah ansûn seng kumsôt kalêng sik hei,
Marvân Lekha Rabu ah ka rahming kajao mo?
