253. COMING HOME (3rd ALEX.396)

“Deliver me, O Lord…I flee unto Thee to hide me.” (Ps.143:9)

ALFRED H. ACKLEY Doh is Bb BENTLEY D. ACKLEY

1) Mi kaset sa kachang jo innum,
Rōngpu! Nahneng kahong jōn nôk jo;
Set intheng nasik nahneng kaom,
Kên inn kahong jōn.

Inn ah sik kahong eng,
Setna kârra kei inreng mak jo ning;
Inn kajôn ah kahong eng,
Rōngpu Jesu! Kei kahong eng.

2) Pa’n sapa katlân ajong kapōah,
Nân ram kâr ânphut najong sōt eng;
Dam mak leh setna kârra inphut,
Inn kahong jōn jo.

3) Na bêkna kare innêm kathei,
Heisamo kên dâng kati sik ah?
Kabat rakip nachôi pêk sik eng,
Kên inn kahong jōn.

4) Jesu thisen marit kalōng khan,
Kaset kavur aman ang pêk jo;
Nang rōk kamalung inpâk na che,
Kên inn kahong jōn.
