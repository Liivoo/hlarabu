379. TLUNG HMUN KARŌK (M.C.K.19)

“…there was no room for them in the inn.” (Lk.2:7)

M. CHUNGRENGKHUP KOM Doh is G 23/06/2017

1) Marvân Rengram lûrna lênna,
Kei sik najōng dahlon;
Tâk Bethlehem Khōpui sung khan,
Tlung hmun karōk om mak jo.

Tlung hmun karōk kaom nâk mo?
Tlensōtpa tlung thei nasik,
Ka malung sung hmun karōk kanei;
Tlensōtpa tlung nasik ah.

2) Ralei hōina – haona hei hin,
Amanbōi rih jo eng;
Tlensōtpa tlung hmun pêk tor loah,
Vâng ṭhat kha chen mak jo ning.

3) Chamna Rengpa tlung nasik ah,
Hmun karōk kanei he’n;
Hōina, mông na leh inpâk na,
Kumsôt ah achen sik jo.
