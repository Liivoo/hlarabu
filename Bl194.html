194. ’TIS THE BLESSED HOUR OF PRAYER (S.S.303)

“The hour of Prayer.” (Acts.3:1)

FANNY J. CROSBY Doh is Eb WILLIAM H. DOANE

1) Rōngpu Pathen leh Asa Jesu ṭhōn kaṭha pên!
Ama malungset na leh rangei mandam na;
Taksen ah ni’ntûp lai hin der nasik pha kachang,
Akahrât mak hei sik ah taksen nasik kaeh.

Derna hmun ahin, derna hmun kahōi,
Takkasen mak hei sik ah taksen nasik kaeh.

2) Tlensōtpa hneng ah derna pha kaṭha tak ahin,
Ni malungdo Ahneng ah nider thei lai hin;
Ning ṭhat nasik hei seng khâm nider nger sik kaeh,
Ni malung amantheng sik, anṭhōipui ṭhak sik me.

3) Mathlêm na omlai hin derna man hnak chêt rih,
Ama hneng ah om nasik eini’n der ṭhak rih;
Taksen na leh hrât nasik apêk mankip sik me,
Bâng lo jên ah der ṭhak rih, nider kha nihmu sik.

4) Ni malung ah hlam rin tak ah nider hei seng kha,
Amalungset na kahmu sik hei derna kha;
Taksen na anṭam rahmu re-hrep ninei inchu,
Tlo hla heite nei mak tin, tiah ahri pêk me.
