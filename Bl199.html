199. TENDERING PLEADING (S.S.449)

“I am the Door, by Me if any man enter in, he shall be saved.” (Jn.10:9)

FANNY J. CROSBY Doh is G IRA D. SANKEY

1) Hong inle jo ruh set hlam kahro hei,
En ruh! Jesu Christa’n ahngâk ṭhak chei;
Ajong ṭhak chei nan hringsōt nasik ah,
Ama taksen ruh, insûm lo jên ah.

Malung innêm tak ah nang abêk che,
Rangei roh Jesûn abêk che nang;
Malung innêm tak ah nang abêk che,
Amalungset chei, hong ruh nang ni.

2) Amân ahngâk chei nân hmang mak nasik,
Kum hingja nalei man hmang jo ravâng;
Na hring nasik ah ajōng thi pêk che,
Alungset len je hri thei lo vâng khan.

3) Aṭong leh alungset na mandon roh,
Naset rakip arôn sō pêk jo che;
Rathla Intheng kadik tak apêk che,
Naset arangei mandam sō jo che.

4) Hong hlut ruh nang ni hlam kot kahōi ah,
Jesu hneng se ruh, hring na kot kaeh;
Malung katlai seng ah abêk ṭhak che,
Tlâksam na heite nei nôk mak jo tin.
