100. THEY THAT WAIT UPON THE LORD (S.S.753)

“…shall renew their strength…mount up…run…walk….” (Isai.40:31)

G.M.J. Doh is AbJAMES McGRANAHAN

1) Rangei ruh ṭōn katlo rakip in,
Rōngpu hrât na hngâk rih;
Ama vâng sem kha hngâk ṭhak inna,
Asyem kaṭha sik me.

Rōngpu hrât na kahngâk hei kha,
Anratha kangar, mathla annei sik jo;
Hmurai kapō kajōng tor sik hei,
Katlân hei tla sam mak hei;
Anlôn innum châm mak hei,
Katlân hei tla sam mak hei;
Anlôn innum châm mak hei,
Anratha kha kangar chêt jo, kase ṭhak tor hei.

2) Nichâm malung atlâk pha leh chu,
Ama ni hri maset;
Tlensōtpa hring na kha ni taksen,
Heimo ni tlai mak na?

3) Inpâk ruh a-om pui ṭhak sik me,
Ralei inhmang kâr seng;
Ama lang en û-na se ṭhak ruh,
Ṭhat mak kha nator sik.
