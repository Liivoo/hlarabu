167. IF GOD BE FOR US (S.S.508)

“If God be for us, who can be against us?” (Rom.8:31)

J.M.G. Doh is GJAMES McGRANAHAN

1) Rōngpu ah inpâk ruh, amanpâk hla sak ruh,
Rup kalên sik ah akadei;
Athi vâng akatlen, heimo ni ṭit sik?
Jesu ni hlam kakei kaom.

Pathen in asen, Pathen in asen, Pathen in asen,
Tumo ni kadōi sik? Tu tâk mo?
Tumo ni kator sik, kator sik?

2) Rōngpu ah hrât û-na Ama hrât na en ruh!
A hlam rin tak ah ṭōn tlo ruh;
Ṭong kadik jui ruh! Seitân ahrât innum,
Ama ni hrât na kachang sik.

3) Ama ṭong jui ruh, aṭongpêk kalên kha,
Christa an om inchu Amân;
Ralei anhmang innum inhmang mak tinnei,
Hing ah hin Bible ah insûn.

4) Rōngpu ah inpâk ruh, aṭong neina sûng ah,
Kum leikōah hring na ṭhak Ama;
Rathla nat damna Rōngpu Jesu kaeh,
Tute lak in mandam thei mak.
