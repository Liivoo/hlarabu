104. I WILL (S.S.474)

“I will trust and not be afraid.” (Isai.12:2)

DANIEL W. WHITTLE Doh is AJAMES McGRANAHAN

1) Nân Tlensōtpân ṭong ajōng mathlung che,
Tûnchu ka hmu sō jo;
Tûnchu Rōngpu! Kei sik katluk sō jo,
Na ṭong rōk taksen rong.

Ka taksen che, enṭangpui roh Rōngpu!
Nang ta khet chang jo rong;
Kei sik jârra na thisen kalōng jo,
Na ta khet chang jo rong.

2) Mahâm ah nalungset na kan hmu jo,
Ka malung inlôp jo;
Nang jârra Rōngpu! Kei kahring sik eng,
Nang rōk taksen che rong.

3) Setna kanei kachang na manhret sō,
Hei lak mo kaṭit sik?
Rōngpu! Tûnchu kei kahring sōt jo eng,
Nang rōk ka taksen che.

4) Rōngpu’n hei rakip tûn ape jo roh,
Nalungset na hla kha;
Malung ah inpâk tak ah hri jo rung,
Kên Christa taksen rong.

5) Akahong rakip hei kha oh! Rōngpu!
Lei malungset hei roh;
Kanṭangpui ṭhak sik chei nati jârra,
Nahneng kan hei hnai che.
