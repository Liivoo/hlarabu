11. JESUS IS MINE (S.S.1045)

“My beloved is mine.” (Songs of Solomon.2:16)

JANE C. BONAR Doh is EbTHEODORE E. PERKINS

1) Ralei anhmang innum Jesu keita,
Hei rakip nêk ah khan Jesu keita;
Ralei chung rakip âm malung mông na ommak,
Jesu hneng ah kaom, Jesu keita.

2) Ka ratha set loah Jesu jui rong,
Na rathla dam nasik Jesu jui roh;
Ni’nreng kabâng sik jo, Amân aṭhōi sik me,
A marvân inn ah khan, Jesu jui roh.

3) Thina ahnai innum Jesu’n ajui,
Ahneng ah aṭhōi sik, marvân ram ah;
Kumsôt hōi kahmu sik, Ama kamanpâk sik,
Alungset marit kha chimmak jo ning.
