65. WHEN THE BEAUTIFUL GATES UNFOLD (S.S.970)

“And the gates…shall not be shut at all by day….” (Rev.21:25)

P.P.B.Doh is GPETER P. BILHORN

1) Jân thim hnōi ah lôn chavai sōt mak jo min,
Hlam kot kaṭha anhlang pha leh;
Pathen ram kahōi ah kaom sik jo me,
Hlam kot kahōi anhlang pha leh.

Hlam kahōi kha anhlang pha leh,
Hlam kahōi kha anhlang pha leh;
Inpâk tak ah ni Rōngpu nihmu sik jo,
Hlam kot kahōi anhlang pha leh.

2) Karangei hên intûp na anlei hngâk eng,
Hlam kot kahōi anhlang pha leh;
Inchit lo jên ah hlam anlei ratō eng,
Hlam kot kahōi anhlang pha leh.

3) Nat leh sat, khōdeng thei de nôk mak jo min,
Hlam kot kahōi kha anhlang pha leh;
Christa leh hmun khat ah kaom sik jo me,
Hlam kot kahōi anhlang pha leh.
