56. WILL THERE BE ANY STAR? (S.S.787)

“…shall shine as the stars forever and ever.” (Dan.12:3)

ELIZA E. HEWITTDoh is AJOHN R. SWENEY

1) Tuning ram kahōi ṭong kha kên kalei mandon,
Kani tlâk ah kaom nasik kha;
Rōngpu lungset jârra kei kaom nasik ram,
Kei inpâk na thei kaom tâk mo?

Ka lukhu ârsi kakop tâk mo?
Ka ralei hring na kani abâng sō tak leh;
Châm inhngat na inn ah kei kaom pha tak leh,
Ka lukhu ârsi kakop tâk mo?

2) Rōngpu hrât na kator chen ah takkasen sik,
Mi manhring nasik ah ei syem roh;
Kahōi tak ah Ama manpâk na a-om leh,
Kei inpâk na thei kaom tâk mo?

3) Rōngpu hme kahmu tak leh kan hôrna rakip,
Heituk ah anpâk sik eng tâk mo!
Sana khōpui kahōi ah kalêng pha tak leh,
Kei sik inpâk na kaom sik jo.
