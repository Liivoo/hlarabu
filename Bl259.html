259. WHILE SHEPHERDS WATCHED (S.S.33)

“Behold! I bring you good tidings of great joy.” (Lk.2:10)

N. TATE Doh is G ESTE’S PSALTER

1) Janṭim lai kemông kakhêm hên,
An kemông ankhêm lai;
Rōngpu Ngeva jōng kaṭum ah,
Ara-âng manvâr hei.

2) Ṭi hêr mak ruh, kei eng kaeh,
Ngeva khan ati hei;
Nang ni sik ah hring leh dam na,
Ṭong kajōng rôn, ati.

3) David phōng ah jōng inte jo,
Vânhnōi intlensōtpa;
Ralei ah naidông ah inte,
Vânhnōi Rengpa kaeh.

4) Marvân ah Rōngpu Ngeva hên,
Chung ah hla anjōng sak;
Vânhnōi Rengpa lênpên sonna,
Mihring hûksōt rasoh.

5) Sak ruh, en ruh, mi rakip in,
Ȏn ruh marvân Rengpa!
Marvân ah hringna, damna ṭong,
Ajōng rôn pêk jo me.
