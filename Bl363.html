363. SEEKING THE LOST (S.S.817)

“Go ye into all the world and preach the Gospel to every creature.” (Mk.16:15)

W.A.O. Doh is Bb WILLIAM A. OGDEN

1) Anhmang sahei kha vajong sōt hei roh,
Hmun rakip ah inreng invai hei;
Kahneng ah hong ruh ti-ah abêk hei,
Tuning hinnum Rōngpu’n abêk hei.

[B] Hme leh tlâng ah [S;A;T] Hme leh tlâng ah,
[B] Vajong hei inna [S;A;T] Vajong hei inna;
[B] Anhmang sa hei [S;A;T] Anhmang sa hei,
[B] Kha hei ṭhōi roh [S;A;T] Kha hei ṭhōi manhlut hei roh;
[B] Rōngpu hneng ah [S;A;T] Rōngpu hneng ah,
[B] Ṭhōi manhlut hei roh [S;A;T] Ṭhōi manhlut hei roh;
[B] Jesûn ni jâr [S;A;T] Jesu eini jârra jōng,
[B] Ah jōng kathi [S;A;T] kathi Pa hneng khan.

2) Anhmang sahei kha Jesu hneng ṭhōi roh,
Ânreng leh khōdeng kathei hei kha;
Hring na hlam kha anchen thei nasik ah,
Kumsôt hring na hlam manthei hei roh.

3) Se roh, lungset na ṭong vaphông sōt roh,
Jesu kahnung kha jui ṭhak inna;
Akatlu hei khâm va mathô hei roh,
Jesu jôn na hlam manthei hei roh.

[B-Bass; S-Soprano; A-Alto; T-Tenor]
