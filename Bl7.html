7. MY JESUS! I LOVE THEE (S.S.659)

“Whom, having not seen, ye love.” (I Pet.1:8)

W.R. FEATHERSTONEDoh is FADONIRAM J.GORDON

1) Eh! Jesu! Nasōk kên kamalungset che,
Rōngpu! Nahneng ah kên kahong rahlâng che;
Ka Rōngpu kha mi inlungset thei kaeh,
Kân hôrna rakip Rōngpu hneng ah kaom.

2) Rōngpu lungset jârra intlensōt jo eng,
Ka setna akheng ah amantheng pêk jo;
Kei jârra Ama ralei ah reng invai,
Ka Rōngpu rōk kha keichu kalungsetpên.

3) Thi leh kahring na rakip Nang kapêk sōt che,
Kahring lai Rōngpu kên kamanpâk sik che;
Kahring na Hlungpui Jesu rōk kha kaeh,
Ka Rōngpu rōk kha keichu kalungsetpên.

4) Oh! Rōngpu! Kei namalungset jârra khan,
Namalungset na ṭong hi manghi mak ning;
Rōngpu lungset na hla kahōi kaman-eh,
Kân hôrna Rōngpu Ama rōk kha kaeh.
