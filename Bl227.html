227. CHRIST, THE LORD IS RISEN TODAY (S.S.158)

“The first-fruits of them that slept.” (I Cor.15:20)

CHARLES WESLEY Doh is D H. CAREY

1) Christa tuning kathōi jo – Hallelujah!
Ralei marvân mi’n an ôn – Hallelujah!
Inpâk ah nang ni’n ôn ruh – Hallelujah!
Marvân mi’ntheng hên an ôn – Hallelujah!

2) Lungset vâng akatlen jo – Hallelujah!
Re kahrât ator sō jo – Hallelujah!
Kani nêk âm kavâr jôk – Hallelujah!
Christa thisen sōk mak jo – Hallelujah!

3) Thlân kot hlung mi’n kam tor mak – Hallelujah!
Hmeikhur kot avōk khōi jo – Hallelujah!
Thina’n Rōngpu khâm tor mak – Hallelujah!
Paradise kot amahlang – Hallelujah!

4) Reng Christa’n vai arôn jo – Hallelujah!
Thina! Na tur kho jo mo? – Hallelujah!
Hûksōt nasik lei kathi – Hallelujah!
Eh! Thlân! Na hrât na khomo? – Hallelujah!

5) Lûrna Rengpa kumsôt sik – Hallelujah!
Rathla rakip ahûksōt – Hallelujah!
Tor na hi Ata kaeh – Hallelujah!
Vânhnōi mi’n manpâk êt rih – Hallelujah!
