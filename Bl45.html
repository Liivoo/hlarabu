45. ANYWHERE, MY SAVIOUR (S.S.596)

“I will follow thee whithersoever Thou goest.” (Matt.8:19)

E.C. ELLSWORTHDoh is BbJOHN H. TENNEY

1) Khôn ênnum kase na Rōngpu’n aṭhōi eng,
Na Âng sûng ah malung inpâk tak ah om rong;
Ka hlam rahling asip innum kajui sik che,
Khôn ênnum na ṭhōina chen ah ka Rōngpu!

Khôn na ênnum kân Tlensōtpa!
Kên kajui sik che, kên kajui sik che;
Khova ram ênnum kan Tlenpa!
Na ṭhōina chen ah.

2) Rōngpu kha malung katlai seng ah jui rong,
Hlam akei inna ka malung man mông ṭhak roh;
Ka hlam khō anthim innum kên kajui sik che,
Khôn ênnum Ama hneng chu kahōi ṭhak eng.

3) Khava ram ênnum Jesu kên jui ṭhak rong,
Hlam kase na aṭhōi roh masō lo jên ah;
Setna kârra Nang in kei enṭangpui ṭhak roh,
Rōngpu’n aṭhōi roh hlam kahro thei nasik.
