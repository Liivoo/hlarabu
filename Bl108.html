108. FOR CHRIST AND THE CHURCH (S.S.1096)

“…in the strength of the Lord God.” (Ps.71:16)

ELIZA E. HEWIT Doh is DWILLIAM J. KIRKPATRICK

1) Christa bêkrup sik hla sak mansâng ruh,
Ni Rengpa Ama rahming kha kaja êt rih;
Malung kahlut ah tlo rih ni hrât lai seng,
Pathen ram ṭong kadik jârra ngir êt rih.

Christa Rōngpu madu pên!
Ni jârra jōng kathi;
Bêkrup sik Athisen kalōng,
Rōngpu’n amantheng roh.

2) Christa bêkrup sik jârra der ṭhak rih,
Anitin asena akahnung jui ṭhak rih;
Rathla Intheng hrât na ratha invon na,
Kaja tak ah tlo êt rih ni hring lai seng.

3) Christa leh bêkrup sik ah pêk nei rih,
Neinâk marit Rōngpu jârra khan nihmu sik;
Ṭhat na ninei rakip Ahneng ah pe rih,
Ni malung kavâr kha inthim mak jo ning.

4) Christa bêkrup sik ah inpe sōt rih,
Ṭit lo jên ah Ama hrât na kha sôn inna;
Ni hringkhō abâng leh alungset hmun ah,
Achung ting kahōi ah kaom sik jo me.
