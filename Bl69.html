69. OPEN WIDE THE DOOR (S.S.358)

“Behold, I stand at the door and knock.” (Rev.3:20)

WILLIAM KITCHINGDoh is DbJOHN H. BURKE

1) Jesûn abêk chei nang ni,
Hong ruh khōdeng kathei hei;
Ahûk manhring nger sik chei,
Akot mahlang ruh.

Akot mahlang ruh, akot mahlang ruh,
Ahûk manhring nger sik chei, akot mahlang ruh.

2) Na kahûk sik ah kajōng,
Nang sik jârra jōng kathi;
Tûnchu thina ator jo,
Na malung thleng roh.

3) Jesûn nang ahngâk ṭhak che,
Malung khat ah majôm ruh;
Na malung inpâk sik jo,
Akot mahlang roh.

4) Tûn Jesu hneng ah hong roh,
Kantlen nasik kahnai jo;
Na hringkhō abâng hma hin,
Akot mahlang roh.
