281. BE NOT DISMAYED (3rd ALEX.2)

“Be careful for nothing…for…He careth for you.” (Phil.4:6 & 1 Pet.5:7)

CIVILLA D. MARTIN Doh is Bb WALTER S. MARTIN

1) Khava hmun achang jo innum,
Malung mandong mak roh;
Pathen in nang aṭhōi ṭhak sik che,
Kamông ah nang om roh.

Malung mangdong mak roh,
Nasena hmun rakip ah khan;
Nang anṭhōipui sik che,
Rōngpu’n aṭhōi sik che.

2) Nang in nanei mak rakip kha,
Rōngpu’n apêk sik che;
Nang in nata der hei rakip kha,
Amân apêk sik che.

3) Na khōdeng theina rakip kha,
Pathen in amanhret;
Rōngpu kha nân masō hêr mak roh,
Vâng apêk nger sik che.

4) Inreng, khōdeng thei rakip ah,
Malung mathla mak roh;
Na lungdo amathluk pêk sik che,
Hlam rin mak tlo mak roh.

5) Pha kaom rakip ah khan um,
Derna mahngi mak roh;
Eini seng inpâk na inn ah khan,
Vōikhat lântûp sik me.
