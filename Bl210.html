210. WHO IS HE? (S.S.66)

“…we see Jesus…crowned with glory and honor….” (Heb.2:9)

B.R.H. Doh is A B.R. HANBY

1) Tu tâk mo serông sûng ah kemông kakhêm ta kamuk?

Thuhming inlak tak kha kaeh,
Reng rakip Rōngpu leh Rengpa!
Kaja tak ah kamuk rih,
Reng rakip Reng leh Rōngpu!

2) Tu tâk mo nisôm manli ram kârra von-ṭâm kade?

3) Nat le sat, malung inrim lei indam Pa kha tumo?

4) Lazar(us) thlân ah lei kaṭap malung inrim kha tumo?

5) Mipui katam tak hei hneng ṭong lei inthei Pa tumo?

6) Gethsemani rahōn ah Pathen kader kha tumo?

7) Tumo cross thing lêr chung ah inrim tak ah lei kathi?

8) Thlân ah inphut kathōi sōt kumsôt hring kape tumo?

9) Marvân, ralei Rōngpu leh vai karôn Rengpa tumo?
