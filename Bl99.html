99. JESUS! THINE ALL-VICTORIOUS LOVE (S.S.187)

“The love of Christ, which passeth knowledge.” (Ephe.3:19)

CHARLES WESLEY Doh is ATHOMAS HASTINGS

1) Siloam tuikhur daihlim hnōi ah,
Reitui marit kapâr;
Amarim katui inthang kha,
Sharon reipâr kaeh, Sharon reipâr kaeh.

2) Khava kapō tûnlai naipang,
Anjōng intêt pha leh;
An malung ri intheng tak ah,
Pathen hlam anrahlâng, Pathen hlam anrahlâng.

3) Siloam tuikhur daihlim hnōi ah,
Rei kha kachi nger sik;
Marim kahōi inlak tak kha,
La kachi nôk nger sik, la kachi nôk nger sik.

4) Khava ângphung mihring hei hi,
Ankani bâng pha leh;
Anthi sik an innêr innum,
Akathi nger sik hei, akathi nger sik hei.
