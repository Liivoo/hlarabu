405. HMŌIṬHŌI NA KANI (M.C.K.27)

“Marriage is honourable in all….” (Heb.13:4)

M. CHUNGRENGKHUP KOM Doh is G 23/01/2018

1) Pathen lungdo tak ah hmōiṭhōi kani,
Kalûr leh marit tak ah;
Jōng katlung jo tuning nan hringkhō sung ah,
Pa lungset na inlâr jo.

Hmōiṭhōina kani! Sak rih! Ȏi rih! Inpâk ah,
Mi rakip in kaja hei rasoh;
Christa vairôn na innshung,
Dam na leh mông na innshung;
Chang ṭhak jo rasoh pha rakip ah.

2) Rōngpu ṭit leh kaja na kanei hin,
Inn hmun kahnet kachang sik;
Neina, haona, khang na apêk ṭhak sik chei,
Rōngpu’n ahrung ṭhak sik chei.

3) Inreng leh inpâk na pha rakip ah,
Alungset mahngi mak ruh;
Pathen tamanchun jo sangram khat kaeh,
Tute’n ṭhen mak hei rasoh.
