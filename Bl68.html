68. COME TO THE SAVIOUR (S.S.1165)

“Make a joyful noise unto God, all ye lands.” (Ps.66:1)

G.F.R.Doh is BbGEORGE F. ROOT

1) Hong ruh! Rōngpu hneng ah intûp rih,
A Bible sûng ah hlam amanthei;
Tû’num nihneng ah a-om pui me,
Lungset vâng abêk me.

Ratha hrok tak ah intûp êt rih,
Niset malung rôn anthleng pha leh;
Ama hneng ah kaom sik jo me,
Marvân inn kahōi ah.

2) Khōdeng kathei hên rangei êt ruh,
Ni malung inpâk êt jo rasoh;
Manpâk na hla kahōi sak inna,
Ama hneng jōn jo rih.

3) Mandon ruh eini ahngâk ṭhak me,
Aṭongpêk me kha majôm inna;
Rangei ruh! Eini abêk ṭhak me,
“Hong ruh nang ni” ati.
