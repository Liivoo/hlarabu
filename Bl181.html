181. THE MASTER’S CALL (S.S.756)

“Go, work today in My vineyard.” (Matt.21:28)

JULIA STERLING Doh is AbIRA D. SANKEY

1) Rangei ruh! Ni Rōngpu’n abêk me,
Sapâr kakhôm sik ah;
Akakhôm sik mi antlôm jârra,
Sapâr kaṭōi sō jo.

Rōngpu rahming ah vase inna,
Malung innêm tak ah;
Katlensōt na inpâk kaom hi,
Vânhnōi ah phôngsōt rih.

2) Se roh, anhmang hei vaṭhōi sōt roh,
Setna inthim kârra;
Rōngpu ratha vâng vaṭhōi hei roh,
Vâr hmun kajôn sik ah.

3) Ânreng hei seng lei mantûp hei roh,
Lungset hmun ah sik ah;
Hring na kot ah manhlut hei inna,
Kumsôt hring nasik ah.

4) Se roh, kani om lai hin tlo roh,
Hma tor thei nasik ah;
Jân inthim kha ajôn thim inchu,
Ṭōn tlo thei mak jo min.
