387. CHȎI KA-ÂN RIH JESU (M.A.K.7)

“Blessed be the Lord…for He hath visited and redeemed His people.” (Lk.1:68)

M. ATON KOM Doh is Ab 21/12/1998

1) Hmun hlop leh phōng hlop ah nilei lêng jo innum,
Christa lungset hrui in hmun khat ah ada jo me;
Ni Reng Christa manpâk jo rih û leh nai hei,
Hong katlung jo inpâk na Silver Jubilee*

Achung ah chunghnungpên Jesu rōk kha kaeh,
Amân set na hrui kha aman hmang sō pêt jo;
Kum katlung aṭhōi ah vâng ajōng syem pêk me,
Chôi ka-ân rih Jesu Christa ni Jubilee.

2) Set sōk ah inphut ani kahûksōtpa kha,
Lûrna leh kajana Ahneng ah pêk êt jo rih;
Taksa hringkhō hmang hi mathlêmna kasip jo,
Dahlon jo rih eini’n tuipui rekhat ting son.

3) Ni taksa leh ni rathla anṭhen mak kâr seng,
Rōngpu’n alungset na me hri manthang êt jo rih;
Pha chai ning katlung ah athlop ṭhak sik jo me,
Sathing atei sik jo ni ṭōn tlo anchai leh.





*This jubilant song was composed by the then Local Pastor
M. Aton Kom to mark the 25th Silver Jubilee celebration of Lairamwangma Baptist Church, Sagang, KRBCA. Celebrated from 11th - 12th April 1999.
