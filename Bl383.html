383. HMUCHI KARÂ (M.C.K.26)

“…God loveth a cheerful giver.” (II Cor.9:7)

M. CHUNGRENGKHUP KOM Doh is D 06/12/2017

1) Malung inpâk ah intûp rih,
Ni ṭōn tlo man kha ni hmu jo;
Hmuchi karâ nilôk sōt jo,
Alûrna kha in phôngsōt jo.

Malung katlai ah pêk sōt rih,
Rum-hrat loah, manêr loah;
Malung katlai ah kapêk mi kha,
Pathen in alungset.

2) Ralei ah khō najōng mansur,
Aṭhat na najōng mansip phet;
Mihring sik hmuchi namankêk,
Na lungset na kha inlâr sōt.

3) Hmuchi katlôm têk kavôr khan,
Hmuchi katlôm têk alôk sik;
Hmuchi katam tak kavôr khan,
Hmuchi katam tak alôk sik.

4) Taksa hlei ah hmuchi vôr kha,
Thina karâ kha inlôk sik;
Rathla hlei ah hmuchi vôr chu,
Kum leikō hring na inlôk sik.
