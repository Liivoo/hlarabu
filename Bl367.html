367. THE LORD IS MY SHEPHERD

“The Lord is my Shepherd; I shall not want.” (Ps.23:1)

FRANCIS ROUS Doh is F JESSIE S. IRVINE

1) Rōngpu! Kaka khêmpa kaeh,
Kei heite vât mak ning;
Tui insim reng tuikhur pang ah,
Kei châm aman hngat eng.

2) Amân karatha ather ah,
Aman nger pêk nôk eng;
Hlam kadik ah kei aṭhōi eng,
Aṭongtep dung jui ah.

3) Khō inthim hlam achang innum,
Kei ṭit mak eng Rōngpu!
Na không hre leh na tingpum be’n,
Ahûk eng ah kaeh.

4) Kan dōipui hmu nasik hmun ah,
Kei sik revei na tlo;
Marit tak ah naratō eng,
Kakok – lei na mansip.

5) Nalungset nân kahring lai seng,
Kei ajui ṭhak sik eng;
Na innpui kha aheitik ah,
Ka inn kachang sik jo.
