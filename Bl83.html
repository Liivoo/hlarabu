83. GATHERING THE REAPERS (S.S.755)

“Thrust in thy sickle and reap…the harvest…is ripe.” (Rev.14:15)

JENNIE E. JOHNSON Doh is DbJOHN R. SWENEY

1) Inpâk na hlather kha nathei nâk mo?
Ram kahōi ah jōng inphut;
Saphe hei kha chôm mantûp jo hei rih,
Tûnchu apha sôt mak jo.

Hlei kaneipa kha jôn lâr bei sik jo,
Ahmaisō, Anunchen ni hmu sik;
Sapâr rakip kakhôm intûp sik ah,
Rōngpu jōng inlâr sik jo.

2) Ram kahōi ah inphut lungset hei khan,
Ralei chung hi anjōng en;
Dehrât na vâng ṭōn rakip anchai ah,
Lên lukhu kalûr annei.

3) Marvân khōpui anphut hla jōn sak khan,
Ratha hrât na apêk me;
Ni ṭōn tlona inphut nichâm pha leh,
Hla khan châm aman hngat me.

4) Ram kahōi tak anphut hla kahōi kha,
Tuning hinnum lân sak ṭhak;
Eini’n nitaksen ânphut nimanhret,
A hla kha nisak bei sik.
