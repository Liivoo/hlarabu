265. DRAW ME NEARER (S.S.607)

“Let us draw near with a true heart.” (Heb.10:22)

FANNY J. CROSBY Doh is Ab WILLIAM H. DOANE

1) Nangta eng kaeh oh! Kan Tlensōtpa!
Nalungset kare kathei;
Kalung ri ah taksen na om rasoh,
Nang kahnai thei nasik che.

Aṭhōi roh Rōngpu! Aṭhōi ṭhak roh,
Na thina cross kûng ah khan;
Aṭhōi roh Rōngpu! Nahneng aṭhōi ṭhak roh,
Na thisen sōk na hmun na.

2) Na ṭōn katlo sik ah amantheng roh,
Nalungset ratha hrât vâng;
Karathla Nang in ahûksōt pêk roh,
Nahneng kaom thei nasik.

3) Rōngpu! Na âng sûng ah hong inkûn eng,
Nang hneng der sik inhôr eng;
Ka Pathen! Na âng ah kader che hi,
Ṭhōn sik ah kahei hnai che.

4) Thina tui-ôngpui re-ah lungset na,
A kaom manhret mak eng;
Khan ah khan inpâk na chungpên kaom,
Ka tlung leh ka rahlâng sik.
