389. TORNA PŌNLEK (M.C.K.15)

“…proclaim liberty throughout all the land unto all the inhabitants….” (Levi.25:10)

M. CHUNGRENGKHUP KOM Doh is Ab 05/12/2016

1) Ralei vairôn ṭhōi ah lei kaom me,
Pathen kadōi ah nilei om jônnum;
Rōngpu Pathen lungset leh marit vâng,
Torna kanei kha kachang jo me.

Re torna kha Rōngpu ah ni hmu jo,
Torna Pōnlek kha mansâng jo rih;
Ning leh hnōm ah masō chenna kha,
Inpâk katlâi ah phôngsōt jo rih.

2) Adahlon jên mak jo ung roh Rōngpu!
Kan hneng ah hong inle nôk jo inna;
Na ratha hrât vâng ahûksōt jo roh,
Pumkhat ah asyem manthar jo roh.

3) Khōdeng thei pha Rōngpu’n amasôn sik,
Ni Pathen in ahrung ṭhak sik jo me;
Inṭangpui na ajōng pêk sik jo me,
Hmesō na kha amannei sik me.

4) Pathen kha ni langting a-om inchu,
Re chai katlung hmesō na ninei sik;
Ni kadōi hei kha tuning nitor sik,
Ni ngir na hmun kha manhnat jo rih.
