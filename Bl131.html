131. AWAKE! THE TRUMPET IS SOUNDING (S.S.671)

“Put on the whole armor of God….” (Ephe.6:11)

FANNY J. CROSBY Doh is BbWILLIAM F. SHERWIN

1) Thô ruh bêkna pêngkun inhring jo,
Abêk na chei chu rangei ruh;
Ni Rōngpu’n hma ting atir jo me,
Eini hin om sôt mak jo rih.

Re tor hma inhngat na om thei mak,
Re von um da thei mak min;
Re chai hma inchamna om thei mak,
Tor nân lên lukhu ahmu.

2) Rathla chem jâm vâng nakhôn khit roh,
Pho leh ôm-pho, lukhu thô roh;
Kangam tak ah na Captain jui roh,
Nân repui hnōi hlut mak nasik.

3) Zion remi hên hma ting nam ruh,
Malung dik leh kangam tak ah;
Cross pōnlek thōi ah ngir inhnet ruh,
Hûksōt kalên kha taksen ruh.
