378. KEMȎNG KAKHÊM HEI (M.C.K.14)

“Glory to God in the highest, and on earth peace….” (Lk.2:14)

M. CHUNGRENGKHUP KOM Doh is A 02/12/2016

1) Rōngpu Ngeva’n ṭong kahōi ajōng rôn,
Hleipuk ah kemông kakhêm hei hneng ah;
David Khōpui Bethlehem serông sung ah,
Tlensôtpa Christa Rōngpu inte jo.

Hong jo ruh inpâk tak ah sak jo rih,
Pathen chôi ka-ân na Ngeva hei hla;
Marvân chung insângpên ah lûr rasoh,
Ralei ah lungmông na om rasoh.

2) Insinsak mihei an mahâm kaṭha,
Rōngpu Pathen in lum alo sik hei;
Apha hrep ah chôimansâng kachang sik hei,
Marvân chung âm kalen pên kachang sik.

3) Pa âng sung ah insinsak ah om rih,
Aratha hrât ṭhōi ah nitor chen ah;
Kemông kakhêm kalen kha ajôn lâr pha,
Lûrna lên lukhu kha nichen sik jo.

4) Pathen vâng ṭhat kaṭha chen tor nasik,
Anitin ah malung insinsak kha;
Kemông kakhêm hei kapō ni’nvôn inchu,
Aheitik ah hri manthang kachang sik.
