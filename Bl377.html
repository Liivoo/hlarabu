377. WHAT CHILD IS THIS? (C.H.B.307)

“…babe wrapped in swaddling clothes, lying in a manger.” (Lk.2:12)

W.C.D. Doh is F WILLIAM CHATTERTON DIX

1) Hiva naidông hi tu jo mo?
Mary ke chung ah kaje;
Tirle he’n hla kahōi vâng an ratō,
Kemông kakhêm he’n kemông ankhem lai.

Ama hi Christa Reng,
Tirle he’n hla marit anjōng sak;
Inrang ruh! manpâk nasik,
Mary Sapa naidông hi.

2) Hei jârra Ama hin kaje?
Ran hei thai sâk na hmun hin;
Rangei êt jo ruh! Christian sopui hei!
Ni set na jârra Amân ade pêk.

3) Marim katui thai invon ruh,
Ama kha ni Reng ka-eh;
Hûkmanhring na kha ajōng rôn pêk jo,
Ni malung sung ah vai rôn jo rasoh.






Words & Musical Notes Translation: M. Chungrengkhup Kom
