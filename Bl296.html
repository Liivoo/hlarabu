296. BEAUTIFUL ISLE! (S.S.753)

“…no need of the sun…moon…for the glory of God…lighten it….” (Rev.21:23)

JESSIE B. POUNDS Doh is A JOHN S. FEARS

1) Kani leh thla rangei mak,
Mi’ntheng hei lêng na kha;
Khan setna om mak jo ning,
Pathen om na kaeh.

Khava ram kha,
Miti’n inhôr na kaeh;
Tlensōtpa lêng na ni om nasik,
Miti’n inhôr na kaeh.

2) Lungdong, lungjing om mak jo,
Châm inhngat na hmun ah;
Marvân mi’n katlai seng ah,
Khan ah khan kalêng hei.

3) Khava ram vâr kahōi kha,
Kur leh khōpui kavâr;
Kemôngte vâr ra-âng in,
Sûn leh jân om mak jo.
