140. TEAR FOR DEPART WILL NEVER FALL

“God shall wipe away all tears from their eyes.” (Rev.21:4)

Doh is D

1) Hmit rathli hui ah inṭhen na om nôk mak jo ning,
Chung Pathen omna thang-vân ram kahōi nitlung leh;
Ni’nreng na nibâng sik jo, inpâk ah kalêng sik me,
Ni’n Tlensōtpa Jesu leh kalêng sap sik jo me.

2) Kemôngte Jesu lêng na ram kahōi nitlung leh,
Ni ṭimre marō hei seng khâm nihmu sō sik jo;
Amanpâk hla nisak sik, hmit rathli’m sōk mak jo ning,
Malung inreng, ṭana hme inlâr nôk mak jo ning.

3) Ṭimre rangei inṭhen na kha om nôk mak jo ning,
Oh! Ni’nreng na ralei hmun hi’m inhmang sō sik jo;
Lily rei pârna hmun ah kumsôt ah kalêng sik me,
Hmit rathli lōng, khōdeng thei, inlâr nôk mak jo ning.

4) Oh! Karangei ṭimre kân ṭhenpui hei se lai seng,
Ka en ṭhak sik ka ṭimre madu hei sena hlam;
Christa’n aching ṭhak rasoh, ni lân hmu nôk mak kâr seng,
Kadam seng ah Pathen in le’nthōipui chei rasoh.
