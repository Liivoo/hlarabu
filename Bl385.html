385. CHRISTA JÂRRA TORNA (M.C.K.28)

“O death, where is thy sting? O grave, where is thy victory?” (I Cor.15:55)

M. CHUNGRENGKHUP KOM Doh is G 19/03/2018

1) Ni’n repui kalenpên thina hi kaeh,
Suk inhmang hi sik ator na rakip;
Ṭong Intheng tahri kha hōi katlung sik jo,
Achaina pêngkun hôn hring pha tak leh.

Eh! Thina! Na torna kha khomo?
Na ratha neina tûr kha khomo?
Christa jârra torna anikapêk,
Pathen kha manpâk ah om ṭhak jo rasoh.

2) Christa in thina inphut kathōi sōt hin,
Ni’n ṭhen pui ṭimre-le rakip hei khâm;
Kathōi sōt sik hei ti manchên na kaeh,
Oh! Heituk hrep ah kahōi jo tâk mo!

3) Rōngpu rajên na niṭōn tlo rakip hi,
Môk ah inhmang mak ning ti nimanhret;
Inhling lo jên ah kahnet ah ngir ṭhak rih,
Rōngpu ṭōn tlo sik insaler ṭhak rih.
