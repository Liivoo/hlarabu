398. HLAM KADIK (M.C.K.16)

“In all your ways acknowledge Him, and He shall direct thy paths.” (Prov.3:6)

M. CHUNGRENGKHUP KOM Doh is D 13/12/2016

1) Pathen malung tlai nasik ah,
Nganse nahei masō mak roh;
Na malung ah mazet inna,
Mihring malung um katlai sik.

Na malung pumpui ah taksen inna,
Na bing ah in-ngai hêr mak roh;
Na tlo rakip chung ah mandon ṭhak roh,
Hlam kadik kha Rōngpu’n aman hmu sik che.

2) Sing na, them na, nei leh haona,
Rōngpu vâng ṭhat hei rakip;
Apêk sik jo tei tor lo ah,
Na hringkhō ah amanbuk sik.

3) Hringkhō kahōi chen thei nasik,
Mansena kha matu tlit roh;
Nang ah inphut hlon sōt mak roh,
Kut suk, ke suk nei mak jo tin.

4) Ṭōnse rakip ra-ôt mak roh,
Khava hei kha Rōngpu’n ahōt;
Mi kasing he’n lurna anchen,
Mahâm ṭhat vâng anchen sik jo.
