121. SAFE IN THE ARMS OF JESUS (S.S.57)

“Underneath are the everlasting arms.” (Deut.33:27)

FANNY J. CROSBY Doh is GWILLIAM H. DOANE

1) Jesu bân ratha hnōi ah, Jesu âng sûng ah khan,
Ka rathla inthup sik jo, kahōi ah châm inhnga;
Alûrna leh inthōi ah jōng inte jo tûnchu,
Tiah marvân Tirle hei an hla sak rangei rih.

Jesu bân ratha hnōi ah,
Jesu âng sûng ah khan;
Ka rathla inthup sik jo,
Kahōi ah châm inhnga.

2) Jesu bân ratha hnōi ah ni’n ngaina inphut ah,
Vânhnōi in anmathlêm na inthup inhmang mak ning;
Khōdeng theina rakip âm, ṭit nasik rakip ah,
Ka rathla asen sōt jo, ṭong rakip intan jo.

3) Rōngpu Jesu Amachu lei ka hringsōt nôk jo,
Ka rathla hring nasik ah ka Rōngpu’n athi pêk,
Dehrât na leh inchun ah khō kavâr kapōah,
Avâr kha kahmu joah Rōngpu kahmu kârseng.
